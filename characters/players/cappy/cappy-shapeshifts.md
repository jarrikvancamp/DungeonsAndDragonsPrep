# Cappy's Druid Shapeshift Templates

## Core Rules

- **Available Forms:** Predator, Defender, Scout, Aquatic (unlocked at level 4)
- **Mental Stats:** Keep your Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma
- **Physical Stats:** Use the form's AC, HP, and attacks
- **HP Reset:** Each transformation grants fresh HP for that form
- **Attack Rolls:** Wisdom modifier + proficiency bonus
- **Save DCs:** 8 + proficiency bonus + Wisdom modifier
- **Scaling:** All forms improve with druid level (see progression table below)

## 🐺 Predator Form
*Think: wolf, panther, tiger*

**Purpose:** High damage and mobility

```yaml
AC: 12 + PB (15 at level 5)
HP: See progression table (12 at level 5)
Speed: 40 ft, climb 30 ft
Attack: Bite/Claw - See table for damage (2d8+3 at level 5)
Special: Pounce - Advantage on first attack after moving 20+ ft straight
```

**Tactical Use:**
- Best for focused damage dealing
- Excellent mobility for hit-and-run tactics
- Pounce ability rewards positioning
- Scales to highest damage at higher levels

## 🐻 Defender Form
*Think: bear, turtle, rhino*

**Purpose:** Tank and frontline protection

```yaml
AC: 13 + PB (16 at level 5)

Speed: 30 ft
Attack: Slam/Maul - See table for damage (1d10+3 at level 5)
```

*Unlocked at 4th level - Think: crocodile, giant frog, shark*

```yaml

Attack: Bite - See table for damage (2d6+3 at level 5)
```

- Grappling ability for battlefield control
- Moderate damage and survivability
- Essential for aquatic encounters

## Combat Tactics by Situation

### **Frontline Tank Needed**
- Use **Defender Form**
- Position between enemies and squishy allies
- Use reaction ability on biggest threats

### **High Damage Output**
- Use **Predator Form**
- Focus on positioning for Pounce attacks
- Target isolated or high-value enemies

### **Exploration & Scouting**
- Use **Scout Form**
- Take advantage of climb speed and keen senses
- Use bonus action Dash for quick repositioning

### **Water Encounters**
- Use **Aquatic Form**
- Only viable option for underwater combat
- Grapple to control enemy movement

### **Utility & Flexibility**
- **Scout Form** for most utility options
- **Predator Form** for general combat effectiveness
- Remember you can change forms with each Wild Shape use

## Level Progression Notes

**Key Milestones:**
- **Level 4:** Aquatic Form becomes available
- **Level 5:** Major damage boost + Primal Strike (extra attack for Moon Druids)
- **Level 6:** Elemental Wild Shape unlocked (CR 1 elementals)
- **Level 8:** Aquatic Form gains permanent Water Breathing
- **Level 9:** Significant damage scaling across all forms
- **Level 11:** Major scaling bump - all forms become much more powerful

**Scaling Philosophy:**
- **HP scales linearly** with level to maintain relevance
- **Damage scales in tiers** at levels 5, 9, and 11
- **AC scales with proficiency bonus** to stay competitive
- **Special abilities unlock** at key breakpoints

**Long-Term Strategy:**
- Focus on Wisdom increases for better attack bonuses and save DCs
- Consider Constitution for survivability in human form
- Elemental forms at level 6+ provide new tactical options
- Forms remain viable throughout all levels of play
