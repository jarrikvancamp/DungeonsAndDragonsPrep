## Spells

### Cantrips (at will)

- **Guidance:** +1d4 to one ability check

### 1st Level Spells

- **Cure Wounds:** 1d8+3 healing, touch
- **Faerie Fire:** 20ft cube, DEX save or outlined + advantage to hit them

- **Heat Metal:** 2d8 fire damage + disadvantage on attacks

### 3rd Level Spells

- **Conjure Animals:** Summon 8 wolves OR 4 brown bears

## Quick Combat Reference

## Quick Combat Reference

### Standard Tactics by Role

**Tank:** Wild Shape → 🐻 Defender → Position to protect allies  
**Damage:** Wild Shape → 🐺 Predator → Move 20ft+ for Pounce advantage  
**Scout:** Wild Shape → 🦅 Scout → Use Keen Senses + bonus Dash  
**Water:** Wild Shape → 🐟 Aquatic → Grapple enemies (DC 14)

### Alternative Approaches

- **Control First:** Cast Entangle/Faerie Fire before Wild Shaping
- **Spell Support:** Stay human form, cast Heat Metal or Moonbeam
- **Emergency:** Drop Wild Shape (free action), cast Cure Wounds
- **Utility:** Minor Wild Shape for scouting/infiltration

### Form Selection Guide

- **Need survivability?** → 🐻 Defender (AC 16, HP 16, damage mitigation)
- **Need damage output?** → 🐺 Predator (2d8+3 with Pounce advantage)
- **Need mobility/scouting?** → 🦅 Scout (Perception advantage, bonus Dash)
- **Water encounter?** → 🐟 Aquatic (only underwater-capable form)

### Damage Mitigation

- Brown Bear form = 34 extra HP buffer
- When Bear form drops, revert to human with original HP
- Use Cure Wounds or Goodberry between fights
