🌿 Druid Shapeshift Templates (Level 5)

✅ Rules:

You can shift into Predator, Defender, Scout, or Aquatic Form.

You keep your mental stats, but replace AC/HP/attacks with the form’s stats.

HP resets each time you shift (like normal Wild Shape).

All attack rolls = Wisdom modifier + proficiency bonus.

DCs (for grapples, trips, etc.) = 8 + PB + Wis.

🐺 Predator Form (damage & mobility)

Think wolf, panther, tiger

AC: 12 + PB (so 15 at level 5)

HP: 30 (6 × druid level)

Speed: 40 ft, climb 30 ft

Attack: Bite or Claw → +Wis+PB to hit, 2d8 + Wis damage

Special: Advantage on the first attack after moving 20 ft straight toward a creature.

🐻 Defender Form (tank)

Think bear, turtle, rhino

AC: 13 + PB (16 at level 5)

HP: 40 (8 × druid level)

Speed: 30 ft

Attack: Slam or Maul → +Wis+PB to hit, 1d10 + Wis damage

Special: When hit by an attack, you can use your Reaction to impose disadvantage (representing thick hide, shell, or size).

🦅 Scout Form (speed & utility)

Think hawk, spider, fox

AC: 12 + PB (15 at level 5)

HP: 25 (5 × druid level)

Speed: 40 ft, climb 30 ft

Attack: Talons/Bite → +Wis+PB to hit, 1d8 + Wis damage

Special: Advantage on Perception checks that rely on sight/smell/hearing, and you can Dash as a bonus action.

🐟 Aquatic Form (unlocked at 4th)

Think crocodile, giant frog, shark

AC: 12 + PB (15 at level 5)

HP: 30 (6 × druid level)

Speed: swim 40 ft, land 20 ft

Attack: Bite → +Wis+PB to hit, 2d6 + Wis damage

Special: You can breathe underwater and grapple a creature (escape DC = your Spell Save DC).

📜 Quick Reference (Level 5 Druid)

ACs: 15 (Predator/Scout/Aquatic) | 16 (Defender)

HPs: 25 (Scout), 30 (Predator/Aquatic), 40 (Defender)

Damage Dice: Predator 2d8, Defender 1d10, Scout 1d8, Aquatic 2d6

Attack Bonus: Wis + PB (so likely +6 if Wis 16)
