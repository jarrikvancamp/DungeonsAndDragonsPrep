## Character Overview

**Name:** Valindra Shadowmantle  
**Race:** Human  
**Class:** Necromancer (former Druid)  
**Age:** 42  
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral (formerly Neutral Good)  
**Role:** Researcher, manipulator, reluctant ally/enemy

## Physical Description

Valindra appears as a tall, slender woman with prematurely white hair that seems to float slightly as if underwater. Her pale skin shows faint black veins beneath the surface, a side effect of her necromantic experiments. She wears elegant robes of deep green and black, adorned with silver embroidery depicting various phases of the moon and intertwining vines that seem to writhe when not directly observed.

Her most striking feature is her eyes, which shift between emerald green (her original color) and obsidian black depending on the type of magic she's channeling. When casting necromantic spells, her eyes become completely black with tiny points of starlight visible within.

**Height:** 5'8"  
**Build:** Slender, graceful  
**Distinguishing Features:** Floating hair, color-changing eyes, visible dark veins

## Backstory

### Early Life & The Emerald Enclave

Valindra was once a respected member of the Emerald Enclave, specializing in the study of natural cycles—particularly the balance between life and death in forest ecosystems. She was known for her innovative research on decay, renewal, and the magical energies that flow through all living things.

Her work was considered valuable but unorthodox, focusing on the "darker" aspects of nature that many druids preferred to avoid. She studied how death feeds life, how decay creates fertility, and how the natural world maintains its balance through what others saw as "negative" forces.

### The Discovery

Three years ago, while researching ancient texts in a forgotten Enclave library, Valindra discovered references to the Shattered Obelisk and its connection to primordial forces. The texts hinted that the obelisks were not merely artifacts of aberrant magic, but somehow connected to the fundamental forces that govern life and death.

Her superiors in the Enclave dismissed her theories as dangerous speculation, but Valindra became obsessed. She began conducting secret experiments, trying to understand how the obelisk fragments affected the flow of life energy.

### The Fall

Her experiments grew increasingly bold and morally questionable. She began studying undead creatures, not to destroy them, but to understand how they maintained their existence outside the natural cycle. When the Enclave discovered her research, they expelled her immediately.

Bitter and convinced that the Enclave was too narrow-minded to understand her work, Valindra embraced necromancy fully. She made contact with the Twilight Sovereign through a ritual involving an obelisk fragment, becoming its primary agent in the material plane.

### Current Motivations

- **Research:** Prove her theories about the connection between druidic magic and necromancy
- **Vindication:** Show the Emerald Enclave that they were wrong to exile her
- **Power:** Gain greater understanding of the Twilight Sovereign's realm
- **Curiosity:** Study unique magical phenomena, especially involving life/death boundaries

- **Isolated:** Her exile has made her suspicious of forming new relationships

### Special Abilities

**Spellcasting.** Valindra is an 11th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). She has the following spells prepared:

- **Cantrips (at will):** Chill Touch, Mage Hand, Prestidigitation, Minor Illusion
- **1st level (4 slots):** Detect Magic, Magic Missile, Ray of Sickness
- **2nd level (3 slots):** Misty Step, Scorching Ray, Web
- **3rd level (3 slots):** Animate Dead, Counterspell, Fireball, Vampiric Touch
- **4th level (3 slots):** Blight, Greater Invisibility, Polymorph
- **5th level (2 slots):** Cloudkill, Dominate Person, Scrying
- **6th level (1 slot):** Circle of Death, Disintegrate

**Twilight Channel.** Once per day, Valindra can channel the Twilight Sovereign's power. As an action, she gains the following benefits for 1 minute:
- Resistance to all damage types except radiant
- Her necromancy spells deal maximum damage
- She can cast spells without expending spell slots (up to 3rd level)

**Undead Mastery.** Valindra can control up to 8 HD worth of undead creatures simultaneously (instead of the normal 4 HD from Animate Dead).

**Research Focus.** When examining magical phenomena related to life/death or the obelisks, Valindra has advantage on all relevant ability checks.

### Combat Tactics

**Round 1:** Cast Greater Invisibility and reposition
**Round 2-3:** Use Twilight Channel and cast high-damage necromancy spells
**Backup Plan:** Summon undead minions to distract while she escapes via Misty Step or flight
**Retreat Threshold:** 40 HP or when facing overwhelming odds

## Roleplay Information

### Voice & Mannerisms
- Speaks in a cultured, academic tone
- Often pauses mid-sentence to examine interesting magical phenomena
- Has a habit of absently stroking her hair when thinking
- Her voice echoes slightly when channeling the Twilight Sovereign's power

- *"You misunderstand—I'm not desecrating nature, I'm studying its most fundamental truth: all life feeds on death."*
- *"The Enclave cast me out for asking the difficult questions. Will you make the same mistake?"*
- *"Your friend's death created such fascinating resonances. I simply had to investigate."*
- *"The Twilight Sovereign offers understanding that your narrow minds could never grasp."*

- Present her as morally gray rather than purely evil
- Use her to advance the main plot while maintaining her own agenda

### Character Development
- Track how the party treats her to determine her arc
- Show consequences of her research over time
- Provide opportunities for redemption or further corruption
- Use her knowledge to reveal important plot information
