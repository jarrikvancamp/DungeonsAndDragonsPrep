# Owlbear (Cragmaw Castle)

## Role
Territorial guardian kept by King Grol in the castle courtyard

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Aggressive melee bruiser with grabbing attacks
**Preferred Range**: Close quarters combat with keen senses
**Key Abilities**: Keen Sight and Smell, Powerful Build, Grab attacks

## Stats
```yaml
Owlbear:
  AC: 13 (Natural Armor)
  HP: 59 (range 45-75)
  Speed: 40 ft
  STR: 20 DEX: 12 CON: 17 INT: 3 WIS: 12 CHA: 7
  
  Skills: Perception +3
  Senses: Darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 13
  Languages: None
  Challenge: 3 (700 XP)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Keen Sight and Smell: Advantage on Perception checks using sight or smell
  
  Actions:
    Multiattack: One beak and one claw attack
    Beak: +7 to hit, 1d10+5 piercing damage
    Claws: +7 to hit, 2d8+5 slashing damage
      If target is prone: Extra 1d8 slashing damage
```

## Location: Cragmaw Castle Courtyard (C4)

### Environmental Features
- **Overgrown Courtyard**: Dense vegetation provides partial cover
- **Stone Walls**: 10-foot walls surround the area
- **Debris**: Fallen masonry creates difficult terrain
- **Central Position**: Can reach most areas of courtyard quickly

### Territorial Behavior
- **Patrol Pattern**: Circles courtyard perimeter every few minutes
- **Alert Triggers**: Loud noises, unfamiliar scents, intruders
- **Aggression Level**: Attacks anything not recognized as belonging

## Personality & Motivation
- **Territorial**: Fiercely protective of courtyard domain
- **Hungry**: Constantly seeking food, sees intruders as potential prey
- **Simple**: Basic animal intelligence, responds to immediate threats
- **Trainable**: Somewhat conditioned by Cragmaw feeding schedule

## Combat Tactics
**Round 1**: Charges strongest-looking enemy with full attack
**Round 2+**: Focuses on prone targets for extra damage
**Positioning**: Uses courtyard walls to prevent flanking
**Pursuit**: Follows fleeing enemies within courtyard bounds

## Environmental Tactics
- **Terrain Use**: Pushes enemies into difficult terrain
- **Wall Advantage**: Uses corners to limit enemy movement
- **Scent Tracking**: Can follow party through castle if they retreat
- **Sound Alarm**: Roars when combat begins, alerting castle

## Training & Control
- **Cragmaw Handlers**: 2 goblins can attempt to direct it (rarely)
- **Food Motivation**: Can be distracted with fresh meat
- **King Grol**: Only respects the hobgoblin leader's commands
- **Escape Route**: Knows secret ways out of castle if cornered

## Encounter Variations
**Surprised Party**: If owlbear detects intruders first
- Advantage on initiative
- May catch party in unfavorable position
- Roar alerts entire castle

**Prepared Party**: If party spots owlbear first
- Can set up favorable positioning
- Might avoid encounter entirely
- Could use environment to advantage

## Alternative Outcomes
**Peaceful Resolution**:
- Animal Handling DC 18 to calm temporarily
- Distraction with food allows passage
- Speak with Animals reveals simple territorial nature

**Taming Attempt**:
- Requires multiple successful Animal Handling checks
- Could become temporary ally against Cragmaws
- Useful for intimidating other creatures

## Environmental Hazards
- **Crumbling Walls**: DC 15 Dex save to avoid 1d6 falling stone
- **Overgrown Areas**: Difficult terrain, potential hiding spots
- **Debris Piles**: Cover from ranged attacks, unstable footing
- **Narrow Passages**: Owlbear size advantage in open areas

## Development Options
- **Castle Alert**: Combat here warns all inhabitants
- **Pursuit**: May follow party if they retreat into castle
- **Distraction**: Can be used to create chaos during stealth mission
- **Environmental**: Courtyard battle affects other encounter areas

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce HP to 45, remove multiattack for first two rounds
**Harder**: Increase HP to 75, add second owlbear or goblin handlers
**Tactical**: Add more complex terrain features and hiding spots
**Stealth**: Emphasize avoiding encounter rather than direct combat

## Treasure
**No Hoard**: Owlbear doesn't collect treasure
**Environmental**: May uncover hidden items while digging
**Feeding Remains**: Bones and equipment from previous victims scattered about
**Castle Connection**: Defeating it removes one guardian from overall defenses
