# Venomfang (Young Green Dragon)

## Role
Territorial young green dragon claiming Thundertree as its lair

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Aerial mobility with poison breath and cunning tactics
**Preferred Range**: Flight for breath weapon, then melee if grounded
**Key Abilities**: Poison Breath, Flight, Amphibious, Legendary Actions

## Lair: Old Tower (U7)

### Lair Features
- **Tower**: 40-foot ceiling, crumbling stonework
- **Cottage**: Adjacent building with dusty furniture
- **Perches**: Multiple levels for aerial advantage
- **Escape Routes**: Large gaps in walls for quick flight

### Approach Detection
- **Keen Senses**: Notices party approaching from 120 feet
- **Initial Response**: Emerges to investigate, not immediately hostile
- **Territorial Warning**: Demands explanation for intrusion

## Personality & Motivation
- **Arrogant**: Believes all creatures should acknowledge her superiority
- **Territorial**: Claims all of Thundertree as her domain
- **Cunning**: Prefers manipulation and fear to direct confrontation
- **Pragmatic**: Retreats when seriously wounded rather than fight to death

## Tactics
**Round 1**: Uses Poison Breath from optimal range and height
**Round 2+**: Stays airborne, uses Wing Attack to knock down enemies
**Hit-and-Run**: Flies out of range, returns when breath recharges
**Retreat Threshold**: Flees when reduced to 1/4 hit points (34 hp)

## Negotiation Potential
- **Initial Approach**: DC 15 Charisma (Persuasion) to avoid immediate combat
- **Tribute Demand**: Wants valuable offerings or service
- **Information Trade**: Knows about regional threats and treasures
- **Respect Hierarchy**: Responds to shows of strength and cunning

## Environmental Tactics
- **Aerial Advantage**: Uses tower height and flight speed
- **Breath Weapon Positioning**: Maximizes cone area of effect
- **Lair Actions**: Can cause minor tremors or environmental effects
- **Escape Routes**: Multiple exit points through damaged walls

## Treasure Hoard
Located in the tower chamber:
- **Coins**: 2,100 cp, 130 gp
- **Art Objects**: 4 silver goblets with moonstones (60 gp each)
- **Magic Items**: Spell scroll of *misty step*, spell scroll of *lightning bolt*
- **Weapon**: **Hew** (+1 battleaxe, maximum damage vs. plants/wood)

## Dragon Cultists (U13)
- **Followers**: 6 human cultists led by Favric
- **Relationship**: Venomfang tolerates but doesn't trust them
- **Plans**: Cultists plot to betray party to dragon for favor
- **Tactics**: May attack party before they reach dragon

## Development Options
- **Peaceful Resolution**: Dragon allows party passage for tribute
- **Combat Victory**: Treasure hoard and regional reputation
- **Escape**: May return later with vengeance motive
- **Cultist Relations**: How party handles followers affects dragon's attitude

## Environmental Hazards
- **Crumbling Tower**: DC 15 Dex save to avoid 1d6 falling debris (triggered by combat)
- **Unstable Floors**: Some areas may collapse under heavy impact
- **Limited Space**: Tower confines movement, favors dragon's aerial mobility
- **Poor Visibility**: Dust and debris can obscure vision
