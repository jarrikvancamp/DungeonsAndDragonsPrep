# Reidoth the Druid

## Role
Elderly human druid protecting Thundertree and serving the Emerald Enclave

## Hook
Wise guardian of the wilderness who knows the land's secrets and dangers.

## Motivation
Protect the natural world from corruption while helping worthy travelers navigate its perils.

## Appearance
- Elderly human with long white hair and beard, weathered by years outdoors
- Wears simple earth-toned robes adorned with natural trinkets and symbols
- Moves with quiet confidence of someone completely at home in the wilderness

## Personality
- **Traits**: Laconic speaker who chooses words carefully; deeply knowledgeable about nature
- **Bond**: Sworn to protect the balance between civilization and the wild
- **Flaw**: Sometimes too willing to let natural dangers eliminate those he deems unworthy

## Quest Hooks
- Provides directions to Cragmaw Castle (with conditions)
- Emerald Enclave faction recruitment opportunity
- Knowledge of regional wilderness and its dangers

## Key Information
- **Thundertree Dangers**: Ash zombies, plant blights, dragon, cultists
- **Cragmaw Castle Location**: Northeast, along Neverwinter river
- **Regional Threats**: Knows about aberrant influences affecting local wildlife
- **Safe Paths**: Understanding of wilderness routes and hidden dangers

## Services Offered
- **Wilderness Guidance**: Safe routes through dangerous areas
- **Natural Healing**: Basic medicinal herbs and healing
- **Animal Communication**: Can gather information from local wildlife
- **Weather Prediction**: Accurate forecasts for travel planning

## Emerald Enclave Recruitment
- **Requirements**: Respect for nature and balance
- **Benefits**: Access to druid circles, natural allies, wilderness safe houses
- **Missions**: Protecting natural sites from corruption or exploitation
- **Philosophy**: Maintaining balance between civilization and the wild

## Cragmaw Castle Information
**Prerequisites**: Either drive off Venomfang or demonstrate respect for nature

**Directions**: 
- Northeast of Thundertree along the Neverwinter river
- Follow deer trails through dense forest
- Look for seven overlapping towers on a hilltop
- About two days' travel on foot

## Lines to Speak
1. "The forest whispers of strange corruptions spreading from the north. You would do well to heed nature's warnings."
2. "Venomfang is a blight upon this land, but she is still a creature of nature. Prove your worth by handling this wisely."
3. "The goblins' castle lies northeast, but first you must show me you understand the balance of life and death."

## Thundertree Warnings
- **Ash Zombies**: Plague victims from Mount Hotenow eruption
- **Plant Blights**: Corrupted vegetation throughout ruins
- **Giant Spiders**: Web traps along main paths
- **Venomfang**: Young green dragon in the old tower
- **Dragon Cultists**: Human fanatics seeking to serve the dragon

## Combat Role (If Necessary)
- **Support Caster**: Uses *Entangle* and *Thunderwave* for crowd control
- **Defensive**: Casts *Barkskin* for protection
- **Environmental**: Uses knowledge of terrain to advantage
- **Escape**: Can use *Animal Messenger* to call for help from local wildlife

## Development Options
- **Faction Leader**: Regional Emerald Enclave coordinator
- **Ongoing Ally**: Provides wilderness support throughout campaign
- **Information Network**: Animal messengers keep him informed of regional events
- **Safe Haven**: Tower serves as secure rest point in dangerous area
