# Vyerith (Doppelganger)

## Role
Advisor and spymaster to King Grol at Cragmaw Castle

## One-Sentence Hook
A cunning doppelganger masquerading as a hobgoblin advisor while secretly working for the Black Spider.

## Motivation
Seeks to manipulate the Cragmaw tribe for the Black Spider's benefit while maintaining her own survival and influence.

## Appearance
**Current Form (Hobgoblin)**: Tall, imposing figure with grayish skin and sharp features
**Natural Form**: Gray-skinned humanoid with no facial features except for indentations where eyes and mouth would be
**Tells**: Occasionally shifts slightly when emotional or concentrating

## Personality
- **Trait 1**: Naturally deceptive and calculating, always thinking three moves ahead
- **Trait 2**: Maintains perfect composure even under pressure, rarely shows genuine emotion
- **Bond**: Secretly loyal to the Black Spider who promised her freedom from persecution
- **Flaw**: Overconfident in her shapeshifting abilities, sometimes takes unnecessary risks

## Location: Cragmaw Castle (C7)

### Quarters
- **Private Chamber**: Modest but well-organized
- **Hidden Items**: Concealed correspondence with Black Spider
- **Escape Route**: Secret passage to exterior wall
- **Shapeshifting Space**: Area with mirrors and disguise materials

## Quest Hooks
1. **Secret Letters**: Hidden correspondence reveals Black Spider operations
2. **Shapeshifter Hunt**: Party may need to identify the impostor
3. **Double Agent**: Could be turned against the Black Spider with right approach

## Key Dialogue
1. **To King Grol**: "My lord, these intruders threaten our domain. We must act swiftly."
2. **If Discovered**: "You think you're clever, but you know nothing of the forces at play here."
3. **Bargaining**: "Perhaps we can reach an... understanding. I have information you need."

## Faction Connections
- **Black Spider Network**: Secret agent and informant
- **Cragmaw Tribe**: Trusted advisor to King Grol
- **Lords' Alliance**: Potential information source if turned

## Combat Tactics
**Preferred Strategy**: Avoid direct confrontation, use deception and misdirection
**Opening Move**: Attempt to read thoughts to understand party intentions
**Mid-Combat**: Shape-change to confuse enemies or escape
**Desperate**: Reveal true loyalties and call for Black Spider aid

## Negotiation Potential
- **Insight DC 15**: Something seems off about this advisor
- **Investigation DC 18**: Find hidden correspondence with Black Spider
- **Persuasion DC 16**: Convince her to betray the Black Spider
- **Intimidation DC 14**: Force information about Black Spider operations

## Secrets & Information
**Black Spider Operations**: Knows location of Wave Echo Cave entrance
**Gundren's Fate**: Aware of dwarf's capture and current status
**Regional Threats**: Information about other Cragmaw positions
**Personal History**: Fled persecution, seeks stability and protection

## Development Options
