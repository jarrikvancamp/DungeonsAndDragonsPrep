# Agatha the Banshee

## Role
Vengeful undead spirit haunting the ruins near Conyberry

## Hook
Former elf wizard transformed into banshee, possesses vast knowledge but hates the living.

## Motivation
Eternal torment drives her to lash out at all living creatures who disturb her solitude.

## Appearance
- Translucent spectral form of what was once a beautiful elf woman
- Long flowing hair that moves as if underwater, eyes like burning coals
- Voice carries otherworldly echo that chills listeners to the bone

## Personality
- **Traits**: Hateful toward all living beings; speaks in riddles and threats
- **Bond**: Obsessed with knowledge from her mortal life as a wizard
- **Flaw**: Consumed by rage and jealousy of the living

## Stats

  Actions:
    Corrupting Touch: +4 to hit, 3d6+2 necrotic damage
    Horrifying Visage: Each non-undead within 60 ft makes DC 13 Wis save
      Failure: Frightened for 1 minute, 3d10 psychic damage

    Wail (1/day): Each humanoid within 30 ft makes DC 13 Con save
      Failure: Drop to 0 hp
      Success: Take 3d6 psychic damage

## Quest Hooks
- Sister Garaele wants information about Bowgentle's spellbook
- Harper faction mission testing party's diplomatic skills
- Knowledge of ancient magical sites and artifacts

## Key Information
- **Bowgentle's Spellbook**: Traded to necromancer Tsernoth from Iriaebor over 100 years ago
- **Regional Knowledge**: Knows about all adventure locations and major NPCs
- **Ancient Lore**: Remembers events from centuries past
- **Magical Sites**: Can provide information about powerful magical locations

## Negotiation
- **Base Attitude**: Hostile, attacks living creatures on sight
- **Persuasion**: DC 15 Charisma (Persuasion) with good roleplay
- **Sister Garaele's Comb**: Automatic success if presented as gift
- **Information Exchange**: Answers one question truthfully if pacified

## Tactics
**Initial**: Uses Horrifying Visage to frighten intruders
**Combat**: Corrupting Touch attacks while staying incorporeal
**Desperate**: Uses Wail ability when reduced to 1/4 HP
**Retreat**: Can phase through walls to escape if necessary

## Lines to Speak
1. "Foolish mortals! You dare disturb my eternal torment? I will add your screams to my collection!"
2. "Knowledge? Yes, I have knowledge... but why should I share it with the warm-blooded who mock my suffering?"
3. "A gift? How long has it been since someone offered me kindness... Very well, ask your question, but make it quick!"

## Development
- **Peaceful Resolution**: Provides valuable information and Harper faction advancement
- **Combat Victory**: Reforms in 24 hours unless specific ritual performed
- **Future Contact**: May remember party favorably for later encounters
- **Regional Reputation**: Other undead might react differently if word spreads

## Lair Features
- **Shelter**: 10x10 dome of warped tree branches
- **Furnishings**: Elven chests, shelves, table, chaise longue (all spectral)
- **Atmosphere**: Unnaturally cold air that numbs living creatures
- **Escape Routes**: Can phase through natural barriers

## Reward Suggestions
- Answer to any one question (truthfully)
- Information about magical item locations
- 3 potions of healing from Sister Garaele (upon completion)
- Harper faction membership opportunity
