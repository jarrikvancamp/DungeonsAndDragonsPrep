# Hamun Kost

## Role
Red Wizard of Thay conducting necromantic research at Old Owl Well

## Hook
Scholarly necromancer more interested in research than conquest, willing to negotiate.

## Motivation
Uncover the secrets of ancient Netheril magic and expand his necromantic knowledge.

## Appearance
- Middle-aged human with completely shaved head revealing intricate necromantic tattoos
- Wears red robes emblazoned with Thayan symbols, carries ornate staff
- Pale complexion and calculating dark eyes that seem to weigh others' worth

## Personality
- **Traits**: Pragmatic scholar who values knowledge over ideology; speaks with measured precision
- **Bond**: Devoted to advancing necromantic arts and Thayan magical traditions
- **Flaw**: Arrogance about his magical superiority leads him to underestimate others

## Quest Hooks
- Requests help dealing with marauders at Wyvern Tor
- Wants information from Agatha about the tower's original builder
- Can provide historical knowledge about Netheril and ancient magic

## Key Information
- **Tower History**: Ancient Netherese watchtower, built by wizard Dresina
- **Zombie Research**: Using undead to excavate magical artifacts
- **Thayan Politics**: Operates independently, not part of larger invasion
- **Ancient Magic**: Knows about Netherese magical techniques

## Negotiation Approach
- **Initial Attitude**: Neutral, not immediately hostile
- **Common Ground**: Scholarly pursuits and magical research
- **Mutual Benefit**: Willing to trade favors rather than fight
- **Professional Respect**: Acknowledges party's capabilities

## Research Requests
1. **Wyvern Tor Marauders**: Deal with bandits disrupting his work
2. **Agatha's Knowledge**: Ask banshee about tower's builder (Dresina)
3. **Artifact Recovery**: Might request help finding specific magical items

## Services Offered
- **Magical Knowledge**: Information about spells and magical phenomena
- **Ancient History**: Details about Netherese civilization
- **Necromantic Insight**: Understanding of undead creatures and their weaknesses
- **Spell Services**: Might cast certain spells for substantial payment

## Lines to Speak
1. "Greetings, fellow practitioners of the Art. I am Hamun Kost, servant of the Red Wizards. Perhaps we can aid each other."
2. "These ancient Netherese ruins hold secrets that could advance magical understanding by centuries."
3. "The marauders at Wyvern Tor have become... tiresome. Eliminate them, and I shall share what I've learned here."

## Combat Tactics (If Necessary)
**Round 1**: Casts *Greater Invisibility* or *Mage Armor* if unprepared
**Round 2-3**: Uses *Fireball* or *Cloudkill* for area control
**Commands Undead**: Directs 12 zombies to surround and overwhelm enemies
**Retreat**: Uses *Misty Step* to escape if losing badly

## Zombie Minions
- **12 Zombies**: Standard stats, follow Hamun's commands
- **Positioning**: Scattered around tower ruins
- **Tactics**: Form protective circle around Hamun
- **Commands**: Attack, defend, or search on his orders
