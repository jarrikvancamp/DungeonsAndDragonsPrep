## Chapter Overview

Once the characters have dealt with Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek, they must venture into the wilderness surrounding Phandalin to find Wave Echo Cave and confront the Spider. This chapter takes place in the Triboar Trail region, featuring multiple quest locations that can be explored in any order.

**Level Advancement:** Characters gain a level when they confront King Grol and learn the location of Wave Echo Cave.

## DM Read-Aloud

*The characters can't learn much more in town, and with the Redbrands defeated, Phandalin isn't in immediate danger. The heroes must enter the forests and hills surrounding the town to uncover the larger plots they're caught in.*

## Story Background

Depending on which townspeople the characters met in Phandalin, they might know some or all of the following leads:

- **The Banshee:** Sister Garaele wants them to find Agatha and ask about Bowgentle's spellbook
- **Old Owl Well:** Daran Edermath wants them to discover who is exploring the ruins
- **Thundertree:** Qelline Alderleaf suggested consulting with the druid Reidoth
- **Wyvern Tor:** Townmaster Harbin wants them to chase away raiders
- **Cragmaw Castle:** Sildar Hallwinter wants them to stop goblin attacks and find Gundren

## Quest Locations

### Conyberry and Agatha's Lair

**Travel:** From Conyberry ruins, old trail leads northwest into Neverwinter Wood.

#### DM Read-Aloud
*The forest grows dark and still as the trail winds deeper into the trees. Heavy vines and thick layers of moss drape the branches, and the air is noticeably colder than it was in the ruined village. After rounding a bend in the trail, you come upon a domed shelter made from the warped, bowing branches of nearby trees.*

**Agatha's Shelter:** 10x10 foot dome with elven furnishings (chests, shelves, table, chaise longue).

#### Encounter: Agatha the Banshee
- **Attitude:** Hateful, snarls at intruders
- **Negotiation:** DC 15 Charisma (Persuasion), advantage with good roleplay
- **Sister Garaele's comb:** Automatic success if presented as gift
- **Hostile Actions:** Uses Horrifying Visage, then attacks if not deterred

**Agatha's Knowledge:** Answers one question truthfully. Well-informed about adventure locations and NPCs. For Bowgentle's spellbook: traded to necromancer Tsernoth from Iriaebor over 100 years ago.

**Quest Completion:** Sister Garaele rewards party with 3 potions of healing.

### Old Owl Well

**Location:** 18 miles southeast of Conyberry in the hills south of Triboar Trail.

#### DM Read-Aloud
*As you crest a low ridge, you spy the crumbling ruins of an old watchtower and mounds of rubble enclosing a courtyard amid the rugged hills. A colorful tent sits in the middle of the courtyard, but no one is in sight.*

**Features:**
- **Zombies:** 12 zombies in tower ruins (DC 15 Perception to smell death)
- **Red Wizard:** Hamun Kost emerges from tent when combat starts

#### Encounter: Hamun Kost
- **Description:** Human mage in red robes, shaved scalp, necromantic tattoo
- **Recognition:** DC 10 Arcana (tattoo), DC 15 History (Thayan garb)
- **Attitude:** Not aggressive, willing to negotiate

**Hamun's Requests:**
1. Deal with marauders at Wyvern Tor
2. Ask Agatha the banshee about tower's builder (answer: Dresina)

**Treasure in Tent:**
- 35 sp, 20 ep, 20 gp, 5 pp
- 5 small agates (10 gp each)
- Potion of healing, spell scroll of darkness
- Jeweled box (25 gp) containing **ring of protection** from ancient Netheril
- **Special Property:** +4 bonus to saves vs. dropping/disarming items in ring hand

**Quest Completion:** Daran Edermath rewards party with boots of striding and springing.

### Ruins of Thundertree

**Background:** Abandoned 30 years ago after Mount Hotenow eruption and zombie plague. Now overrun with mutated vegetation and dangerous creatures.

#### DM Read-Aloud
*Gradually, the trail becomes an old, overgrown lane winding between dilapidated buildings choked with vines and brush. In the middle of the settlement rises a steep hill, upon which stands a stone tower with a partially collapsed roof and an adjoining cottage.*

**Warning Sign:** "Danger! Plant monsters and zombies! Turn back now!" (placed by Reidoth)

#### General Features
- **Ruined Buildings:** Stone walls 5-8 feet high, debris creates difficult terrain
- **Intact Buildings:** Stuck doors (DC 10 Strength), arrow slit windows
- **Trees/Brush:** 30-40 feet tall trees provide cover, brush is difficult terrain

#### Key Locations

**U1: Westernmost Cottage**
- **Enemies:** 2 twig blights hidden by doorway (Passive Perception 13 to spot)
- **Tactics:** Support twig blights in U2 if combat erupts

**U2: Blighted Cottages**
- **Enemies:** 2 twig blights in foliage (Passive Perception 13)
- **Treasure:** Hidden chest under eastern cottage floor (DC 10 Investigation): 1,400 cp, 160 sp, 90 gp

**U3: The Brown Horse (Tavern)**
- **Enemies:** 6 ash zombies (special: leave ash cloud when destroyed)
- **Features:** Common room, kitchen/brewery area with empty ale tuns

**U4: Druid's Watch Post**
- **NPC:** Reidoth (elderly human druid with long white hair)
- **Attitude:** Reasonably hospitable, laconic
- **Knowledge:** Warns of ash zombies, mutated plants, spiders, dragon, cultists
- **Emerald Enclave:** Offers membership to nature-respecting characters
- **Cragmaw Castle Directions:** Requires chasing off Venomfang or good roleplay

**U5: Blighted Farmhouse**
- **Enemies:** 12 twig blights, 2 needle blights in thicket
- **Tactics:** 2 twig blights per round for 3 rounds, then needle blights

**U6: Ruined Store**
- **Enemies:** 2 giant spiders
- **Features:** Webs across lane (difficult terrain, DC 8 Athletics or restrained)
- **Treasure:** Cocooned elf corpse with +1 leather armor, shortsword, potion of healing, 23 gp, 35 sp

**U7: Old Tower (Dragon Lair)**
- **Enemy:** Venomfang (young green dragon)
- **Features:** Tower with 40-foot ceiling, cottage with dusty furniture
- **Dragon Tactics:** Flies away when reduced to 1/4 hit points
- **Treasure:** 2,100 cp, 130 gp, 4 silver goblets with moonstones (60 gp each), spell scroll of misty step, spell scroll of lightning bolt, **Hew** (+1 battleaxe, max damage vs. plants/wood)

**U8: Old Smithy**
- **Enemies:** 4 ash zombies
- **Treasure:** Dragon scales in cabinet (75 gp total)

**U9: Herbalist's Shop**
- **Background:** Mirna Dendrar's family shop
- **Treasure:** Hidden case (DC 10 Perception): gold necklace with emerald pendant (200 gp)

**U10: Town Square**
- **Features:** Weathered statue of warrior Palien (DC 15 History to recognize)

**U11: Old Garrison**
- **Enemies:** 5 ash zombies (former soldiers in rusted mail)
- **Features:** Main room, kitchen/pantry, ladder to roof battlement

**U12: Weaver's Cottage**
- **Enemies:** 4 twig blights (south), 2 needle blights (east)
- **Detection:** DC 13 Perception within 10 feet

**U13: Dragon Cultists' Cottage**
- **Enemies:** 6 human cultists (4 on guard, 2 resting)
- **Leader:** Favric (ambitious, plans to betray party to dragon)
- **Entry:** Barred doors/shutters (DC 20/15 Strength)
- **Treasure:** 6 amethysts (50 gp each), potion of flying

### Wyvern Tor

**Location:** Prominent crag northeast of Sword Mountains, visible from 20 miles away.

#### Raider Camp
- **Finding Camp:** DC 15 Perception or DC 10 Survival check per hour
- **Approach:** Cave mouth at bottom of ravine, bugbear sentry 20 yards out

#### DM Read-Aloud
*The faint smell of smoke hangs in the air as you ascend a rugged ridge on the lower slopes of the hill. Fifty yards away, a cave mouth opens up at the bottom of a ravine. Hunkered down by a boulder twenty yards outside the cave, evidently keeping watch, is a single bugbear.*

#### Encounter: Orc Raiders
- **Enemies:** Brughor Axe-Biter (orc, 18 hp), 4 bugbears, Gog (ogre)
- **Tactics:** If Brughor dies, others flee; Gog fights to death
- **Cave Art:** Strange drawing of goblin with crystal skull (foreshadows Chapter 5)
- **Treasure:** 180 sp, 15 ep, 3 vials perfume (5 gp each)

**Quest Completion:** Harbin Wester pays 100 gp reward.

### Cragmaw Castle

**Background:** Built by wizard of ancient Phalorm, seven overlapping towers (upper levels collapsed). Ground floor habitable, ruled by King Grol.

#### General Features
- **Doors:** Wood reinforced with iron, no locks, DC 15 Strength if barricaded
- **Ceilings:** 15 feet high unless noted
- **Floors:** Cracked flagstones over dirt
- **Light:** Dim natural light through arrow slits (day), dark (night)
- **Walls:** 5 feet thick (exterior), 1 foot thick (interior)
- **Arrow Slits:** 10 feet above ground, 4 feet above interior floors, grant three-quarters cover

#### Key Locations

**C1: Castle Entrance**
- **Features:** Crumbling towers, broken bronze gates
- **Stealth:** Group Stealth vs. goblin Perception to avoid detection

**C2: Trapped Hall**
- **Trap:** Tripwire cave-in (DC 20 Perception to spot), DC 10 Dex save or 2d10 bludgeoning damage
- **Alarm:** Triggers alert to C3, C7, C8, C9

**C3: Archer Post (North and South)**
- **Enemies:** 2 goblin bosses with shortbows each
- **Attack:** Shortbow +4, 80/320 range, 1d6+2 piercing damage

**C4: Ruined Quarters**
- **Enemies:** 3 goblins sleeping
- **Features:** Collapsed southwest tower, twisting passage east

**C5: Storeroom**
- **Treasure:** Emboldening brandy (1 temp hp per glass, poisoned if 2+ glasses/hour), Sildar's gear (chain mail, shortsword, heavy crossbow, longsword with Neverwinter emblem)

**C6: Hobgoblin Quarters**
- **Enemies:** 4 hobgoblins
- **Treasure:** Various weapons, special quarterstaff (engraved feathers, 1 lb., 10 gp)

**C7: Banquet Hall**
- **Enemies:** 6 goblins, Yegg (goblin boss cook)
- **Features:** 25-foot ceiling, long tables, brass brazier

**C8: Dark Hall**
- **Enemy:** 1 grick (hidden in upper reaches, DC 14 Perception or surprised)
- **Features:** Former chapel, carvings of Oghma, Mystra, Lathander, Tymora
- **Treasure:** Gold elf statuette (100 gp) in brazier with augury power (one use per creature)

**C9: Goblin Shrine**
- **Enemies:** Lhupo (goblin boss "priest"), 2 goblins
- **Features:** Jagged Tooth shrine, bloodstained altar
- **Treasure:** Gold chalice (180 gp), silver censer (150 gp)

**C10: Postern Gate**
- **Entry:** Locked iron door (DC 15 Dexterity or DC 25 Strength)
- **Alert:** Breaking door alerts C7, C8, C9, C12, C13

**C11: Ruined Tower**
- **Features:** Collapsed tower, hidden northern entrance (DC 15/10 Perception)

**C12: Guard Quarters**
- **Enemies:** 4 hobgoblins (watch from arrow slit)
- **Tactics:** One runs to warn King Grol, returns in 2 rounds

**C13: Haunted Tower**
- **Enemy:** 1 grell (in upper shadows)
- **Entry:** Boarded door (warning sign)
- **Features:** 30-foot hollow tower
- **Treasure:** Chest in upper area (DC 15 Athletics to reach): 90 ep, 120 gp, potion of healing, spell scroll of silence, spell scroll of revivify

**C14: King's Quarters**
- **Enemies:** King Grol (bugbear), Snarl (dire wolf), Vyerith (doppelganger as drow)
- **Hostage:** Gundren Rockseeker (unconscious, 0 hp)
- **Negotiation:** Vyerith wants to question then kill Gundren; Grol wants payment
- **Northwest Room:** Collapsed bathroom with tile tub

#### King Grol's Negotiation
If warned of attack, Vyerith hides behind door for ambush while Grol threatens to kill Gundren unless characters retreat.

## Chapter Conclusion

When characters rescue Gundren or obtain the location of Wave Echo Cave through other means, they can proceed to Chapter 4. Gundren offers 25 gp each for rescue and promises 10% share of mine's wealth once operational.

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 2):**
- Reduce zombie/goblin numbers by 2-3
- Give Agatha helpful hint even on failed Persuasion
- Venomfang flees at half hit points

**Harder (Party Level 4):**
- Add goblin boss to random encounters
- Venomfang uses lair actions
- King Grol has hobgoblin reinforcements
