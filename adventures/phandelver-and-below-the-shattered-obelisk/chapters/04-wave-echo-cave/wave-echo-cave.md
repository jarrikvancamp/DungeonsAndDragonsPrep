## Chapter Overview

Wave Echo Cave is the lost mine of the Phandelver Pact, located fifteen miles east of Phandalin in the Sword Mountains. The Spider (Nezznar) and his followers have captured the Rockseeker brothers and begun exploring the dangerous mine in search of the Forge of Spells.

**Level Advancement:** Characters gain a level when they defeat Nezznar the Spider.

## DM Read-Aloud

*The rich mine of the Phandelver Pact was lost five hundred years ago during bandit attacks that devastated this area. In the centuries since, countless prospectors and adventurers have searched for the lost mine, but none succeeded until the three Rockseeker brothers found the entrance a month ago.*

## Story Background

The Spider followed the Rockseekers to their discovery, captured two brothers (Tharden and Nundro), and arranged Gundren's ambush. Now Nezznar explores the mine seeking the Forge of Spells where human mages once enchanted dwarven weapons and gnome gadgets. Dangerous monsters and undead guardians hinder his progress.

### Wave Echo Cave Features

**General Features:**
- **Atmosphere:** Cold, damp, drafty with noticeable breeze from W1 to W16
- **Ceilings:** Tunnels 10 feet, rooms 20 feet, caverns 30 feet with stalactites
- **Doors:** 6 feet tall, 4 feet wide, 6-inch thick stone slabs with iron fittings
- **Walls:** 3 feet thick hewn stone (W14, W15, W19, W20 have dressed stone blocks)
- **Floors:** Smooth natural stone
- **Light:** None (descriptions assume light sources or darkvision)
- **Stalagmites:** Provide cover in natural caverns

**Booming Waves:** Rhythmic thunder echoes throughout every 2 minutes, growing louder toward northeast. Caused by underground hot spring surging into narrow funnel.

## Cave Locations

### W1: Cave Entrance
**Features:**
- Large natural cavern with rock pillar
- Campsite behind pillar: bedrolls, supplies (flour, salt, meat, lanterns, oil, tools)
- **Tharden's Body:** Dead dwarf (Gundren's brother), killed by Spider
- **Open Pit:** 10 feet wide, 20 feet deep with hemp rope
- **Goblin Body:** Strange psionic goblin with elongated skull (foreshadowing)

**Treasure:** Tharden wears cloak of protection (overlooked by Spider).

**Tunnels:** Northwest to W2, east to W3.

### W2: Mine Tunnels
**Features:**
- Maze of intersecting passages with 6-foot ceilings
- Partially excavated rock faces, old mine workings
- **Enemy:** 1 ochre jelly (stalks party from dead end)

**Low Ceiling:** Medium+ creatures have disadvantage on melee attacks except with light weapons.

### W3: Old Entrance
**Features:**
- 30-foot high cavern at tunnel intersection
- Wall carvings of dwarf and gnome miners
- **Bodies:** Two dozen skeletons in rusted armor (ancient battle site)
- **Lighting:** 6 large unlit brass lanterns in niches

**Enemies:** 6 stirges on ceiling (DC 14 Perception to avoid surprise).

**History:** Pitched battle when bandits stormed original entrance (now buried).

### W4: Old Guardroom
**Features:**
- Splintered benches, collapsed ceiling debris
- Stone bunks, toppled weapon racks
- **Bodies:** 8 corpses including 2 ogres

**Enemies:** 2 ogre zombies, 6 dwarf zombies (animate when living creature enters).

### W5: Assayers' Office
**Features:**
- Large stone counter with 3 iron balance scales
- North wall cubbyholes with crumbling paper scraps (Dwarvish weight records)
- Several long-dead corpses

**Treasure:** Locked iron strongbox behind counter (DC 20 Dexterity): 600 cp, 180 sp, 90 ep, 60 gp.

### W6: South Quarters
**Features:**
- Miners' barracks with stone bunks in rows
- Corroded iron brazier with cold coals
- **Sounds:** Faint crunching/splintering (DC 10 Perception)

**Enemies:** 3 ghouls from W9 pack, gnawing on bones.

### W7: Ruined Storeroom
**Features:**
- Eastern wall collapsed to rubble
- North storeroom with split-open kegs and tuns
- **Safe Rest:** Secure location, door can be barred from inside

### W8: Fungi Cavern
**Features:**
- Dense carpets of weird fungi covering floor sections
- Puffballs, shelf fungus, large stalks and caps (5 feet tall)
- Green-glowing puffballs provide light for entire cavern

**Enemies:** 2 violet fungi (DC 15 Nature to spot before reaching them).

**Safely Navigate:** Stay close to southern and eastern walls.

### W9: Great Cavern
**Features:**
- Three sections: high ledges (east/west), lower middle section
- Carved stairs to ledges (10 feet high, DC 12 Athletics to climb)
- 2 large tables in middle, 1 small table on eastern ledge
- Dozens of skeletal remains from ancient battle

**Enemies:** 5 ghouls on western ledge (Passive Perception 12 to notice).

**Fall Damage:** 3 (1d6) bludgeoning + prone condition.

### W10: Dark Pool
**Features:**
- Still, dark pool fills most of cavern
- Shore of broken pale mussel shells, fishy odor
- Passage south, steps east, sluggish stream northeast

**Enemy:** 1 giant constrictor snake (attacks rear character).

**Pool Details:** 20 feet deep center, stream 3 feet deep with 2-3 foot ceiling clearance.

**Treasure:** Wizard skeleton on bottom (10 feet from shore, under 10 feet water): 3 jeweled gold rings (150 gp each), wand of magic missiles.

### W11: North Quarters
**Features:**
- Stone bunks, glowing iron brazier for light/heat
- **Eastern Door:** Barricaded from inside (DC 20 Athletics to force)
- **Sounds:** Gruff Goblin voices about hunger (DC 10 Perception)

**Enemies:** 6 bugbears (loyal to Nezznar, front line against undead).

**Treasure:** Largest bugbear carries: 15 cp, 13 ep, 2 agates (10 gp each), potion of healing.

### W12: Smelter Cavern
**Features:**
- Blast furnace and waterwheel (cold/dark)
- Coal heaps, carts of unrefined ore
- 10-foot channel (dry, 5 feet below floor)
- **Bodies:** Dozen+ withered corpses in armor remnants

**Enemies:** 8 dwarf zombies, 1 flameskull (ancient wizard servant).

**History:** Heart of mining operation, waterwheel powered bellows for furnace.

### W13: Starry Cavern
**Features:**
- Glittering ceiling minerals create starry effect
- Two damaged stone rooms (human-proportioned)
- Escarpment with carved stairs
- Dozens of skeletons from spell battle

**Magic Aura:** Subtle aura detectable with Arcana skill or detect magic (stronger near W15).

### W14: Wizards' Quarters
**Features:**
- Scorched, cracked double door (DC 15 Athletics to force)
- Fire-damaged furnishings (tables, chairs, bookshelves, beds)
- Scorched iron chest, rubble-filled south closet

**Enemy:** Mormesk the wraith (last wizard to die here, guards his treasure).

**Treasure:** Unlocked scorched chest: 1,100 cp, 160 sp, 100 ep, 5 malachites (10 gp each), platinum-filigree pipe (150 gp).

### W15: Forge of Spells ⭐
**Features:**
- Large damaged workshop with scorched worktables
- Stone pedestal with brazier of eerie green flame (untouched by destruction)
- Small northern finishing room

**Enemy:** 1 spectator (confused, believes mine still operational).

**Negotiation:** DC 15 Charisma (Deception) to convince spectator party works here and can dismiss it.

**Brazier of Green Flame:**
- **Identification:** DC 15 Arcana reveals source of cave's magic
- **Effect:** Nonmagical weapon/armor bathed 1+ minutes becomes +1 for 1d12 hours
- **Warning:** Removing brazier permanently destroys its power

**Treasure:**
- **Lightbringer:** +1 mace, glows as torch on command, +1d6 radiant vs. undead
- **Dragon Breastplate:** +1 breastplate, advantage on saves vs. dragon breath weapons

### W16: Booming Cavern
**Features:**
- Narrow ledge 15 feet above water
- Water surges every 2 minutes, raising level 10 feet for 1 minute
- Sound source of "Wave Echo" name

**Enemy:** 1 giant octopus (attracted by light, tentacles reach ledge).

**Swimming:** DC 10 Strength save to stay afloat (fail = pulled underwater).

**Northeast Cavern:** Underground lake (hundreds of yards across) leads to hot spring source.

### W17: Old Streambed
**Features:**
- 4 feet high, obstructed by boulders/pebbles
- Former streambed (now diverted)
- **Secret Route:** Circumvents undead in W12

### W18: Collapsed Cavern
**Features:**
- Wide rift in eastern half, stream flows into it
- Ropes secured to iron stakes on western edge
- **Rift:** 20 feet deep

**Enemies:** 2 bugbears on guard, 2 bugbears working in rift, Vhalak (doppelganger as drow supervisor).

**Tactics:** Doppelganger retreats to W19 if 3+ bugbears killed.

**Treasure:** Gauntlets of ogre power in rift rubble (DC 20 Perception, 1 check per hour per character).

### W19: Temple of Dumathoin ⭐
**Features:**
- 6 cracked marble pillars
- 9-foot statue of dwarf god Dumathoin with stone warhammer
- **Eyes:** Large emeralds (5,000 gp each, but see trap below)
- Table on west side, firepit, bedrolls

**Enemies:** Nezznar the Spider, 2 bugbears (+ doppelganger from W18 if escaped).

**Tactics:** If expecting trouble: bugbears hide behind pillars, Nezznar casts invisibility.

**Statue Trap:** Removing emerald triggers DC 12 Dex save. On failure, DC 15 Dex save vs. 11 (2d10) bludgeoning as ceiling collapses. Anyone still inside after 1 round is buried and killed.

**Nezznar's Personality:**
- Curious about characters despite intent to kill
- Admits to being the Spider
- Uses Cragmaws and Redbrands to keep cave secret
- Promises cooperation but betrays when able

**Treasure:** Nezznar carries potion of healing, spider staff, iron key (anvil head - unlocks W20). Black sack on table: 190 ep, 130 gp, 15 pp, 9 small gems (10 gp each), electrum ale mug (80 gp).

### W20: Priests' Quarters
**Features:**
- Locked door (DC 15 Dexterity with thieves' tools, or Nezznar's key)
- Red and gold draperies
- Bed and brazier

**Prisoner:** Nundro Rockseeker (dwarf commoner, badly disheveled, bound and unconscious).

**Rescue:** Nundro grateful, offers to accompany party through cave.

## Major NPCs

### Nezznar the Spider
- **Background:** Drow seeking Forge of Spells, orchestrated Gundren's capture
- **Motivation:** Power through ancient magic, establishing dominion over region
- **Tactics:** Uses charm and deception before resorting to violence
- **Equipment:** Spider staff, potions, magical components

### Mormesk the Wraith
- **Background:** Last wizard to die defending the mine
- **Motivation:** Centuries of anger, guards treasure he can no longer touch
- **Tactics:** Leads undead in W12 area, stays near treasure chest

### The Spectator
- **Background:** Summoned guardian, served 101 years but lost grip on reality
- **Motivation:** Believes mine still operational, follows original instructions
- **Negotiation:** Can be convinced to return to home dimension

## Chapter Conclusion

With Nezznar defeated and the Spider's plots ended, the Rockseeker brothers take over mine administration. The party receives a 10% share of profits (suggest 255 gp at start of each subsequent chapter). 

**If Continuing:** Characters may notice disturbing evidence of strange goblins threatening the area, setting up Chapter 5's psionic goblin plot.

## Scaling Options

- Reduce bugbear numbers by 1-2 per encounter
- Spectator offers warning before attacking
- Nezznar flees at half hit points

**Harder (Party Level 5):**
- Add drow elite warrior with Nezznar
- Spectator has additional spells prepared
- Random encounters occur more frequently
- Additional undead in W12 (2 more zombies)
