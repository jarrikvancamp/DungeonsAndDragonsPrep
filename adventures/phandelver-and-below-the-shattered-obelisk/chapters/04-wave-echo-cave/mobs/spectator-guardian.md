# Spectator (Wave Echo Cave Guardian)

## Role
Ancient guardian of the Forge of Spells, bound by magical contract

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Defensive ranged combatant with eye ray abilities
**Preferred Range**: 60-120 feet with flight advantage
**Key Abilities**: Eye Rays, Flight, Spell Reflection, Create Food and Water

## Stats

  AC: 14 (Natural Armor)

  Condition Immunities: Prone
  Senses: Darkvision 120 ft, passive Perception 16

      1. Confusion Ray: Wisdom save or confused for 1 minute
      2. Paralyzing Ray: Constitution save or paralyzed for 1 minute
      3. Fear Ray: Wisdom save or frightened for 1 minute
      4. Wounding Ray: +5 to hit, 3d10 necrotic damage
  
  Reactions:
    Spell Reflection: On 5-6 of d6, reflect spell back at caster

## Location: Wave Echo Cave (W15)

### Forge Guardian Chamber
- **Central Platform**: Raised area where spectator hovers
- **High Ceiling**: 30-foot vaulted stone ceiling allows full flight
- **Multiple Entrances**: Can monitor all approaches
- **Forge Proximity**: Close enough to sense threats to main objective

### Positioning Tactics
- **Hover Height**: Maintains 15-20 feet above ground level
- **Central Control**: Positions to see all entrances simultaneously
- **Retreat Options**: Can move to adjacent chambers if overwhelmed
- **Spell Safety**: Stays at range to maximize spell reflection chances

## Personality & Motivation
- **Dutiful**: Bound by ancient contract to guard the forge
- **Logical**: Evaluates threats based on actions, not appearances
- **Weary**: Centuries of service have made it somewhat tired
- **Negotiable**: Will honor agreements if properly approached

## Guardian Contract
**Primary Duty**: Protect Forge of Spells from unauthorized access
**Authorized Users**: Originally dwarves, gnomes, and humans of Phandelver's Pact
**Current Status**: Contract unclear due to pact's ancient dissolution
**Interpretation**: May accept legitimate heirs or demonstrated need

## Combat Tactics
**Round 1**: Fear Ray on leader, Paralyzing Ray on spellcaster
**Round 2+**: Wounding Ray on wounded enemies, Confusion Ray on groups
**Defensive**: Uses flight to stay out of melee range
**Spell Reflection**: Positions to maximize reflection chances against casters

## Negotiation Potential
- **Telepathic Communication**: Can speak directly to minds
- **Historical Knowledge**: Remembers original Phandelver's Pact
- **Contract Bound**: Must follow terms but can interpret reasonably
- **Persuasion DC 15**: Convince of legitimate claim to forge access

## Eye Ray Tactics
**Target Priority**:
1. Spellcasters (spell reflection opportunity)
2. Heavily armored fighters (paralyzing ray)
3. Group leaders (fear ray for morale impact)
4. Wounded enemies (wounding ray for elimination)

**Tactical Combinations**:
- Fear + Confusion = maximum battlefield chaos
- Paralyzing + Wounding = guaranteed high damage
- Fear + tactical positioning = force disadvantageous movement

## Environmental Factors
- **Flight Advantage**: Most party members limited to ground movement
- **Spell Reflection**: Magic users must be cautious with targeting
- **Chamber Size**: Adequate space for hit-and-run tactics
- **Light Conditions**: Darkvision advantage in dim areas

## Alternative Resolutions
**Peaceful Negotiation**:
- Demonstrate connection to original pact holders
- Prove worthy intent regarding forge usage
- Show respect for guardian's duty and sacrifice

**Temporary Alliance**:
- Common enemy in Black Spider forces
- Mutual interest in forge protection
- Ancient programming may recognize dwarf allies

## Contract Interpretation
**Acceptable Claimants**:
- Descendants of pact signers (Gundren Rockseeker qualifies)
- Those acting on behalf of legitimate heirs
- Heroes defending against clear threats to forge

**Unacceptable**:
- Obviously malicious intent (Black Spider)
- Purely mercenary motivations
- Those who would misuse forge power

## Development Options
**Alliance**: Becomes temporary ally against Black Spider
**Neutral**: Allows passage but doesn't assist further
**Guardian Role**: Continues protecting forge post-adventure
**Release**: Contract fulfilled, free to leave or stay as desired

## Information Source
**Historical Knowledge**: Remembers forge's construction and original purpose
**Recent Events**: Aware of Black Spider's activities and intentions
**Cave Layout**: Knows all passages and hidden areas
**Magical Lore**: Understands forge operation and safety protocols

## Treasure

**Magical Knowledge**: Can teach about aberration magic or planar lore
