# Mormesk the Wraith

## Role
Undead remnant of a human wizard killed in Wave Echo Cave's destruction

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Incorporeal hit-and-run with life drain abilities
**Preferred Range**: Melee for life drain, uses incorporeal movement tactically
**Key Abilities**: Incorporeal Movement, Life Drain, Undead immunities

## Location: Wave Echo Cave (W14)

### Wizard's Quarters
- **Ancient Study**: Collapsed bookshelves and ruined magical equipment
- **Ritual Circle**: Partially completed lichdom preparation
- **Hidden Treasury**: Concealed magical items and spellbook fragments
- **Spectral Anchors**: Objects tying Mormesk to this location

### Incorporeal Advantages
- **Wall Movement**: Can phase through cave walls and ceiling
- **Surprise Attacks**: Emerges from solid rock without warning
- **Escape Routes**: No terrain limitations for retreat
- **Positioning**: Can attack from unexpected angles

## Personality & Motivation
- **Obsessed**: Still pursuing lichdom after centuries of undeath
- **Bitter**: Angry about interrupted research and magical studies
- **Territorial**: Considers wizards' quarters his exclusive domain
- **Scholarly**: Retains fascination with magical knowledge and power

## Background & History
**Life**: Human wizard researching lichdom transformation
**Death**: Killed during orc attack that destroyed Wave Echo Cave
**Undeath**: Rose as wraith, bound to location by unfinished magical work
**Current State**: Continues attempting to complete lich transformation

## Combat Tactics
**Round 1**: Emerges from wall behind spellcaster, uses Life Drain
**Round 2+**: Phases through walls to attack different targets
**Hit-and-Run**: Uses incorporeal movement to avoid retaliation
**Target Priority**: Focuses on spellcasters and clerics first

## Incorporeal Movement Tactics
- **Wall Emergence**: Attacks from within solid stone for surprise
- **Vertical Movement**: Uses ceiling and floor for positioning
- **Escape**: Retreats into walls when seriously wounded
- **Harassment**: Continuous hit-and-run attacks

## Negotiation Potential
- **Magical Knowledge**: Desperate for spellbook fragments or magical research
- **Lichdom Obsession**: May bargain for components or information
- **Territorial Agreement**: Could allow passage for magical payment
- **Insight DC 16**: Still retains some scholarly curiosity

## Life Drain Mechanics
**Immediate Effect**: 4d8+3 necrotic damage
**Constitution Save**: DC 14 to avoid hit point maximum reduction
**Healing**: Mormesk regains half the damage dealt
**Death**: Target dies if maximum hit points reduced to 0
**Recovery**: Maximum hit points restore on long rest
