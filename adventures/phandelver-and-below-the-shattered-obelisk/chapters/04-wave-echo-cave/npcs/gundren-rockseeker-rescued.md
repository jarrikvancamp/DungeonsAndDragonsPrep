# Gundren Rockseeker (Rescued)

## Role
Rescued prisoner and quest-giver, key to accessing Wave Echo Cave's secrets

## One-Sentence Hook
A determined dwarven prospector whose capture started the party's adventure, now grateful ally and keeper of ancient mining knowledge.

## Motivation
Seeks to restore his family's mining operation and honor the Phandelver's Pact while ensuring Wave Echo Cave's power isn't misused.

## Appearance
**Physical**: Typical mountain dwarf build, graying brown beard, weathered hands
**Condition**: Shows signs of recent captivity - bruises, torn clothing, exhaustion
**Equipment**: Simple clothes, lacks his original gear (captured by Black Spider)
**Demeanor**: Grateful but determined, eyes show both weariness and resolve

## Personality
- **Trait 1**: Eternally optimistic about business prospects despite recent hardships
- **Trait 2**: Deeply grateful to those who aided him, remembers every kindness
- **Bond**: Feels responsible for protecting Wave Echo Cave and its secrets
- **Flaw**: Sometimes prioritizes business opportunities over personal safety
