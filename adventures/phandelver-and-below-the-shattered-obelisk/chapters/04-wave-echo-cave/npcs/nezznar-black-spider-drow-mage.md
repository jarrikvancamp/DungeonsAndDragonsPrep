# Nezznar "The Black Snake" (Drow Mage)

## Role
Main antagonist and final boss of the Lost Mine of Phandelver arc

## One-Sentence Hook
A cunning drow mage seeking to claim Wave Echo Cave's magical forge for his own dark purposes.

## Motivation
Believes the Forge of Spells will grant him the power to carve out his own domain in the surface world, free from drow society's restrictions.

## Appearance
**Physical**: Pale gray skin, white hair, red eyes typical of drow
**Clothing**: Dark robes with spider motifs, silver jewelry
**Demeanor**: Calm, calculating, speaks with quiet authority
**Spider Familiar**: Large spider often perched on shoulder or nearby

## Personality
- **Trait 1**: Methodical and patient, prefers to let others make the first move
- **Trait 2**: Speaks softly but with absolute conviction in his superiority
- **Bond**: Obsessed with claiming the Forge of Spells as his rightful prize
- **Flaw**: Underestimates surface dwellers, believing them inferior to drow

## Location: Wave Echo Cave (W19)

### Temple of Dumathoin
- **Magical Forge**: Ancient dwarven Forge of Spells centerpiece
- **Braziers**: Magical light sources, immune to darkness spells
- **Stone Statues**: Dwarven religious iconography
- **Multiple Entrances**: Can retreat through several passages

### Lair Preparation
- **Mage Armor**: Cast before party arrives (included in AC)
- **Concealment**: Uses darkness spell and temple shadows
- **Escape Routes**: Multiple passages mapped for retreat
- **Allies**: May have bugbear and spider reinforcements

## Quest Hooks
1. **Gundren's Captor**: Responsible for dwarf's imprisonment and torture
2. **Regional Threat**: Plans to use forge to create magical army
3. **Drow Exile**: Seeking power to return to Underdark as conqueror

## Key Dialogue
1. **Opening Threat**: "You've meddled long enough in affairs beyond your understanding."
2. **During Combat**: "The forge's power will be mine! You cannot stop destiny!"
3. **If Defeated**: "This... changes nothing. Others will come. The forge calls to those with true power."

## Faction Connections
- **Underdark Drow**: Exile seeking to prove himself worthy
- **Black Spider Network**: His network of agents and spies
- **Lords' Alliance**: Major threat to regional stability

## Combat Tactics
**Pre-Combat**: Casts Mage Armor and positions near forge
**Round 1**: Web spell to control battlefield, misty step to advantageous position
**Round 2+**: Magic missile for guaranteed damage, ray of frost to slow pursuers
**Desperate**: Summons giant spider, uses darkness and retreat
**Final Stand**: Fights near forge, using its magical emanations for cover

## Spell Selection Strategy
- **Web**: Control multiple enemies, force difficult terrain
- **Magic Missile**: Reliable damage against heavily armored foes
- **Misty Step**: Escape from melee, reposition tactically
- **Shield**: React to dangerous attacks
- **Suggestion**: Turn party members against each other

## Negotiation Potential
- **Information Trade**: Knows extensive details about regional threats
- **Conditional Surrender**: Might bargain for life if clearly defeated
- **Insight DC 18**: He's genuinely desperate to prove himself
- **Persuasion DC 20**: Could be convinced to abandon forge claim

## Secrets & Information
**The Forge**: Knows how to operate Forge of Spells safely
**Wave Echo Cave**: Complete knowledge of cave layout and dangers
**Regional Networks**: Information about other criminal organizations
**Underdark Politics**: Intelligence about drow society and threats

## Final Boss Mechanics
**Environmental Factors**:
- Forge magical emanations provide partial cover
- Braziers cannot be extinguished by darkness
- Stone construction limits area spell effects
- Multiple exits allow tactical retreat

**Defeat Conditions**:
- Reduced to 0 hit points
- Successfully intimidated into surrender (DC 18)
- Convinced to abandon claim through roleplay

## Development Options
**Death**: Standard resolution, party claims forge and completes quest
**Capture**: Valuable intelligence source, potential future plot hooks
**Escape**: Becomes recurring antagonist with knowledge of party
**Redemption**: Extremely difficult but possible character development
