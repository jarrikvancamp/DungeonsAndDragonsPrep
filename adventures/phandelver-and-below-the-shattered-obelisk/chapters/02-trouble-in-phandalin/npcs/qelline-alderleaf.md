# Qelline Alderleaf

## Role
Wise halfling farmer and mother, community information hub

## Hook
Practical farm woman whose son discovered something important about the Redbrands.

## Motivation
Protect her family and community while helping others with her practical wisdom.

## Appearance
- Sturdy halfling woman with graying brown hair tied in practical braids
- Hands stained with soil and herb work, warm brown eyes full of kindness
- Wears simple farm clothes patched with care, always smells faintly of fresh bread

## Personality
- **Traits**: Speaks with quiet wisdom earned through years of rural life; excellent judge of character
- **Bond**: Fierce protector of her son Carp and all children in Phandalin
- **Flaw**: Sometimes too willing to trust that others share her moral compass

## Quest Hooks
- Son Carp found secret tunnel to Redbrand hideout
- Knows druid Reidoth at Thundertree who might help find Cragmaw Castle
- Can provide information about local families and their troubles

## Key Information
- **Carp's Discovery**: Secret tunnel leading to Redbrand hideout (area R8)
- **Reidoth Connection**: Druid at Thundertree knows the wilderness well
- **Local Knowledge**: Understands every family's situation in Phandalin
- **Safe Houses**: Knows which homes would shelter refugees

## Services Offered
- **Fresh Food**: Excellent meals and trail rations
- **Herbal Remedies**: Basic healing herbs and practical medicines
- **Local Guidance**: Information about terrain, weather, and safe routes
- **Child Care**: Safe haven for rescued children or orphans

## Lines to Speak
1. "My Carp is curious as a cat, always poking around where he shouldn't. This time his curiosity might help you."
2. "Reidoth the druid has been in these lands longer than anyone. If the Cragmaws have a castle nearby, he'd know."
3. "The Redbrands took too much from this town already. It's time someone showed them what real courage looks like."

## Family Details
- **Son**: Carp Alderleaf (10 years old, precocious and brave)
- **Farm**: Small but productive, grows vegetables and herbs
- **Community Role**: Unofficial counselor and problem-solver
- **Reputation**: Universally respected for fairness and wisdom

## Farm Features
- **Vegetable Garden**: Fresh food and healing herbs
- **Root Cellar**: Cool storage and potential hiding place
- **Chicken Coop**: Fresh eggs and early warning system (chickens alert to strangers)
- **Well**: Clean water source away from town center

## Intelligence Network
- **Children**: Carp and friends see everything adults miss
- **Market Day**: Hears news from surrounding farms and settlements
- **Women's Circle**: Informal network of wives and mothers sharing information
- **Traveling Traders**: Stops by her farm for fresh provisions and news
