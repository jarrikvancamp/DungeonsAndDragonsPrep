# Sister Garaele

## Role
Zealous young elf cleric of Tymora and secret Harper agent

## Hook
Friendly shrine keeper who offers divine services and has special missions for worthy adventurers.

## Motivation
Serve Tymora by bringing good fortune to others while gathering intelligence for the Harpers.

## Appearance
- Youthful elf with bright silver hair and warm golden eyes that sparkle with enthusiasm
- Wears simple robes in Tymora's colors with a silver disk holy symbol
- Moves with barely contained energy, gestures expressively when speaking

## Personality
- **Traits**: Zealously devoted to Tymora's ideals; speaks rapidly when excited about her faith
- **Bond**: Believes luck favors those who fight for justice and equality
- **Flaw**: Sometimes too trusting of people's stated motivations

## Quest Hooks
- Seeks information from banshee Agatha about Bowgentle's spellbook
- Can provide Harper missions as party proves trustworthy
- Offers divine healing and guidance to those in need

## Key Information
- Recently returned wounded from investigating strange magical disturbances
- Has contacts throughout the Harper network for information gathering
- Knows legends about various magical sites in the region
- Aware of faction politics but focuses on helping common folk

## Harper Mission: Banshee's Bargain
- **Target**: Agatha the banshee near Conyberry
- **Goal**: Ask about Bowgentle's spellbook location
- **Method**: Bring jeweled silver comb as gift (provides this)
- **Reward**: 3 potions of healing + Harper faction invitation

## Services Offered
- **Healing**: *Cure Wounds* for 10 gp (once per day per person)
- **Blessing**: *Bless* spell before dangerous missions (free)
- **Sanctuary**: Shrine provides safe haven for those in need
- **Information**: Knowledge of regional lore and faction activities

## Lines to Speak
1. "Tymora smiles upon those who aid the downtrodden! Your victory over the Redbrands brings good fortune to all."
2. "I have a task that requires... discrete individuals. The Harpers value those who fight for justice and equality."
3. "The banshee Agatha is dangerous, yes, but she was once human. Perhaps she remembers what it means to help others."

## Faction Recruitment
- **Harper Membership**: Offers invitation after banshee mission
- **Ideals**: Equality, opposition to abuse of power, preserve knowledge
- **Benefits**: Information network, magical item access, noble cause

## Development
- **Growing Trust**: Provides increasingly sensitive Harper missions
- **Regional Contact**: Becomes liaison for Harper operations in the area
- **Divine Support**: Offers more powerful healing as party grows in level

## Shrine Features
- **Tymora's Blessing**: Once per day, reroll any die roll (must use second result)
- **Sanctuary**: Safe place to rest and recover
- **Information Hub**: Harper messages and regional news
- **Divine Focus**: Enhanced spellcasting for Tymora's faithful

## Reward Suggestions
- 3 potions of healing for completing banshee mission
- Harper pin and faction benefits
- Tymora's blessing for important rolls
- Access to Harper safe houses and contacts
