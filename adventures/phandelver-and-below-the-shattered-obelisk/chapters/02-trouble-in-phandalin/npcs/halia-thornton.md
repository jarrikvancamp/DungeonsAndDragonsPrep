# Halia Thornton

## Role
Guild Master of Phandalin Miner's Exchange and secret Zhentarim agent

## Hook
Ambitious businesswoman who offers lucrative work to capable adventurers.

## Motivation
Expand Zhentarim influence in the region and take control of profitable criminal enterprises.

## Appearance
- Sharp-dressed human woman in her thirties with calculating green eyes
- Wears fine clothes that suggest wealth, adorned with subtle gold jewelry
- Carries herself with confidence of someone used to being in charge

## Personality
- **Traits**: Ruthlessly pragmatic in business; speaks in measured, professional tones
- **Bond**: Believes the Zhentarim's methods are more effective than local authorities
- **Flaw**: Underestimates the loyalty small-town folk have for each other

## Quest Hooks
- Offers 100 gp to capture Glasstaff alive and bring his correspondence
- Can provide information about regional mining claims and trade routes
- Future Zhentarim missions as party reputation grows

## Key Information
- Wants to take over Redbrand operations rather than destroy them
- Has connections throughout the mining industry
- Knows which claims are most valuable in the region
- Can provide intelligence about bandit activities affecting trade

## Hidden Agenda (DC 15 Insight)
- More interested in Glasstaff's criminal network than justice
- Plans to use captured Redbrands as Zhentarim recruits
- Sees party as potential assets for future operations

## Lines to Speak
1. "The Redbrands are a problem, yes, but they're an *organized* problem. That has certain... advantages."
2. "Capture Glasstaff alive and I'll make it worth your while. Dead men tell no tales, but live ones can be quite informative."
3. "Phandalin has potential, but it needs proper management. The right people in the right positions."

## Services Offered
- **Ore Assessment**: Can evaluate mineral samples for 5 gp
- **Mining Claims**: Information about available or disputed claims
- **Trade Intelligence**: Knowledge of caravan routes and schedules
- **Fencing Services**: Will buy certain "recovered" goods, no questions asked

## Faction Recruitment
- **Zhentarim Membership**: Offers invitation after Glasstaff mission
- **Benefits**: Access to Zhentarim resources, information network, profitable jobs
- **Expectations**: Occasional missions that benefit Zhentarim interests

## Development
- **If Trusted**: Becomes valuable ally providing missions and intelligence
- **If Opposed**: Uses wealth and connections to make life difficult for party
- **Long Term**: Could become regional Zhentarim leader with party's help

## Reward Suggestions
- 100 gp for capturing Glasstaff alive with correspondence
- Discounted services at Miner's Exchange
- Access to Zhentarim safe houses and contacts
- Future profitable mission opportunities
