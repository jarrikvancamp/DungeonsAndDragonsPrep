# Iarno "Glasstaff" Albrek

## Role
Former Lords' Alliance agent turned Redbrand leader

## Hook
Mysterious wizard known only as "Glasstaff" who terrorizes Phandalin with his gang.

## Motivation
Gain wealth and power while serving the Spider's interests, seek the Forge of Spells.

## Appearance
- Pale complexion with sharp features and calculating dark eyes
- Wears fine robes adorned with silver thread, carries ornate staff
- Speaks with cultured accent that betrays his noble upbringing

## Personality
- **Traits**: Coldly intelligent, views people as resources to exploit; maintains facade of civilization
- **Bond**: Believes the Spider offers him the power he deserves
- **Flaw**: Arrogance leads him to underestimate "simple" folk and their determination

## Quest Hooks
- Needs to be captured alive for Halia Thornton's Zhentarim mission
- His correspondence reveals the Spider's plans
- Former Lords' Alliance contact gone rogue

## Key Information
- The Spider is a drow seeking to claim the Forge of Spells
- Bugbears in his employ know the route to Wave Echo Cave
- Was sent to establish Redbrand operations and watch for strangers
- None of his former Lords' Alliance colleagues know of his betrayal

## Tactics
**Round 1**: Casts *Greater Invisibility* if possible, or *Mage Armor* if not prepared
**Round 2-3**: Uses *Fireball* or *Ice Storm* to control multiple enemies
**Desperate**: Casts *Misty Step* to escape via secret door to R7
**Cornered**: Uses *Shield* reactions and *Counterspell* defensively

## Lines to Speak
1. "You fools have no idea what forces you're meddling with. The Spider's reach extends far beyond this pathetic town."
2. "The Lords' Alliance? They're weaklings clinging to outdated ideals. True power requires... flexibility."
3. "Kill me and you'll never find your precious dwarven friend. The Spider has plans for Gundren Rockseeker."

## Development
- **Captured Alive**: Reveals Spider's identity, location hints for Wave Echo Cave
- **Killed**: Letter from Spider provides same information
- **Escaped**: Becomes recurring antagonist with vengeance motive
- **Interrogated**: May reveal other Zhentarim operations in region
