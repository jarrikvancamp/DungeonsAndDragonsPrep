# Bugbears (Spider's Agents)

## Role
Elite guards working for the Spider, stationed at Redbrand hideout

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Powerful melee combatants with reach weapons and stealth
**Preferred Range**: Surprise attacks with morningstar, then sustained melee
**Key Abilities**: Brute damage, Surprise Attack, intimidating presence

## Stats
```yaml
Bugbear:
  AC: 16 (Hide Armor, Shield)
  HP: 27 (range 20-35)
  Speed: 30 ft
  STR: 15 DEX: 14 CON: 13 INT: 8 WIS: 11 CHA: 9
  
  Skills: Stealth +6, Survival +2
  Languages: Common, Goblin
  Challenge: 1 (200 XP)
  
  Equipment: Hide armor, shield, morningstar, javelin (x2)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Brute: Extra die of damage with melee weapons (included below)
    Surprise Attack: +2d6 damage if target hasn't acted yet
  
  Actions:
    Morningstar: +4 to hit, 2d8+2 piercing damage
    Javelin: +4 to hit, range 30/120, 1d6+2 piercing damage
```

## Encounter Details (R9: Guard Barracks)

### Numbers & Positioning
- **4 Bugbears**: Named Nosk (leader), Grob, Thunk, Vash
- **1 Goblin**: Droop (cowering servant)
- **Formation**: Bugbears ready for combat, Droop hides behind them

### Leader: Nosk
- **HP**: 35 (maximum for leader)
- **Equipment**: Masterwork morningstar, 13 sp, emerald (50 gp), ruby (100 gp)
- **Authority**: Carries master key to all doors in hideout
- **Personality**: Arrogant, considers Redbrands inferior

## Tactics
**Round 1**: Javelins thrown, then charge with morningstars
**Round 2+**: Focus fire on spellcasters and lightly armored targets
**Cornered**: Fight to death to avoid Spider's punishment
**Coordination**: Work together against single tough opponent

## Key Information
- **Spider's Orders**: Eliminate strangers asking about Wave Echo Cave
- **Geographic Knowledge**: Know general direction to Wave Echo Cave
- **Hierarchy**: Report directly to the Spider, not Glasstaff
- **Motivations**: Fear the Spider more than death

## Interrogation Results
- **DC 12**: Work for the Spider, a drow seeking ancient dwarven magic
- **DC 15**: Wave Echo Cave lies northeast, in mountains near Neverwinter Wood
- **DC 18**: Spider seeks the "Forge of Spells" to create magical weapons
- **Intimidation**: May reveal Cragmaw Castle connections

## Droop the Goblin
```yaml
Droop (Goblin):
  AC: 15, HP: 7, Speed: 30 ft
  Equipment: Leather armor, shield, scimitar
  
  Personality: Terrified of bugbears, grateful for rescue
  Information: Scraptop goblin captured by Cragmaws, sold to Redbrands
  Development: Can become party ally if treated kindly
```

## Environmental Advantages
- **Room Features**: Use furniture for cover and improvised weapons
- **Intimidation**: Droop's terror can distract bugbears
- **Surprise**: Party might catch them resting or eating
- **Exits**: Know all escape routes through hideout

## Combat Variants
- **Alert**: If warned by other encounters, prepared for ambush
- **Surprised**: Caught during meal or rest, weapons not immediately ready
- **Retreat**: May fall back to defend Glasstaff if losing badly
- **Prisoners**: Might try to take hostages if desperate

## Development Options
- **Master Key**: Nosk's key opens all locked doors in hideout
- **Intelligence**: Bugbears provide crucial Wave Echo Cave direction
- **Droop**: Goblin can become recurring ally or information source
- **Spider Connection**: Bodies/evidence prove larger conspiracy

## Treasure
- **Nosk**: 13 sp, emerald (50 gp), ruby (100 gp), master key
- **Others**: 2d6 sp each, assorted crude jewelry (5-15 gp total)
- **Equipment**: 4 sets of hide armor, shields, morningstars
- **Droop**: Grateful goblin service (if rescued kindly)

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce to 3 bugbears, lower HP to 20 each
**Harder**: Add 1 additional bugbear, increase Nosk to 40 HP
**Tactical**: Bugbears coordinate better, use pack tactics
**Stealth**: Some bugbears start hidden for surprise attacks
