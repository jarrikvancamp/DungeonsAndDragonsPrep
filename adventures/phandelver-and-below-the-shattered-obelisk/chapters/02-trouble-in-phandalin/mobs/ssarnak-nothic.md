# Ssarnak the Nothic

## Role
Ancient guardian creature bound to protect the Tresendar crypts

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Psychic predator using stealth and rotting gaze
**Preferred Range**: Medium range for Rotting Gaze, melee if cornered
**Key Abilities**: Keen Sight, Rotting Gaze, Weird Insight

## Stats
```yaml
Ssarnak (Nothic):
  AC: 15 (Natural Armor)
  HP: 45 (range 35-55)
  Speed: 30 ft
  STR: 14 DEV: 16 CON: 16 INT: 13 WIS: 10 CHA: 8
  
  Skills: Arcana +3, Insight +4, Perception +2, Stealth +5
  Languages: Understands Common but can't speak
  Challenge: 2 (450 XP)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Keen Sight: Advantage on Perception checks using sight
    Weird Insight: Can sense magical items within 120 ft
  
  Actions:
    Multiattack: Two claw attacks or one claw and one Rotting Gaze
    Claw: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 slashing damage
    Rotting Gaze: One creature within 30 ft, DC 12 Con save
      Failure: 3d6 necrotic damage, maximum HP reduced by damage taken
      Success: Half damage, no HP reduction
```

## Encounter Details (R8: Crevasse Cave)

### Lair Features
- **20-foot Crevasse**: Divides cave, 30 feet deep
- **Two Wooden Bridges**: One rigged to collapse (DC 10 Perception)
- **Hiding Spots**: Multiple alcoves and shadows for ambush
- **Treasure Pit**: Ssarnak's hoard at bottom of crevasse

### Behavioral Patterns
- **Territorial**: Attacks intruders but not mindlessly aggressive
- **Curious**: Fascinated by magical items and their histories
- **Lonely**: Desperate for intelligent conversation after decades alone
- **Protective**: Guards treasure zealously but might bargain

## Tactics
**Round 1**: Uses Rotting Gaze from hiding while party crosses bridge
**Round 2**: Attempts to collapse bridge if enemies still on it
**Round 3+**: Claw attacks against anyone who reaches its side
**Retreat**: Falls back to treasure area if seriously wounded

## Negotiation Potential
- **DC 15 Persuasion**: Will talk instead of fighting immediately
- **Magic Items**: Extremely interested in examining party's magical gear
- **Information Trade**: Knows secrets about magical items for bargains
- **Lonely**: Craves intelligent conversation about arcane matters

## Key Information (If Communicated With)
- **Psionic Goblins**: Recently killed two "wrong-thinking" goblins
- **Ancient History**: Remembers when Tresendar Manor was built
- **Magical Disturbances**: Senses growing aberrant magic in region
- **Glasstaff**: Knows wizard visits but avoids direct contact

## Dead Goblins Discovery
- **2 Psionic Goblins**: Bodies show signs of psychic trauma
- **Strange Features**: Elongated skulls, residual psionic energy
- **Equipment**: Broken weapons that once glowed with green energy
- **Implication**: Something is changing local goblinoid populations

## Treasure Hoard
Located at bottom of crevasse (requires 30-foot climb or flight):

### Thel Dendrar's Remains
- **Skeleton**: Wearing tattered clothes, wooden unicorn pendant
- **Tragedy**: Father thrown into pit by Redbrands
- **Pendant**: Carved by his daughter Nilsa, worth 10 gp (sentimental value)

### Ssarnak's Collection
- **Coins**: 160 sp, 120 gp
- **Gems**: 2 diamonds (100 gp each), 1 emerald (200 gp)
- **Potions**: 2 potions of healing, 1 potion of invisibility
- **Magic Weapon**: +1 longsword "Talon" (elegant elven blade)

## Development Options
- **Peaceful Resolution**: Ssarnak allows treasure access for magical item examination
- **Combat Victory**: Full treasure hoard becomes available
- **Ongoing Ally**: Might provide information about magical disturbances
- **Warning System**: Could alert party to future threats in area

## Environmental Hazards
- **Bridge Collapse**: DC 10 Dex save or fall 30 feet (3d6 damage)
- **Crevasse Climbing**: DC 15 Athletics to climb rough stone walls
- **Darkness**: Nothic has darkvision advantage over most parties
- **Unstable Footing**: Difficult terrain near crevasse edges

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce HP to 35, Rotting Gaze save DC 10
**Harder**: Increase HP to 55, add 1d4 necrotic damage to claw attacks
