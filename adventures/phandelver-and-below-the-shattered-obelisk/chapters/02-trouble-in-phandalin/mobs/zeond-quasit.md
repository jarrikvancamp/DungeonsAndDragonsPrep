# Zeond the Quasit

## Role
Glasstaff's invisible familiar and early warning system

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Invisible scout and magical support for master
**Preferred Range**: Stays hidden, uses poison and magic from safety
**Key Abilities**: Invisibility, Poison, Magic Resistance, Flight

## Stats

## Encounter Details (R11: Wizard's Workshop)

### Behavior Patterns
- **Invisible Guardian**: Watches workshop for intruders
- **Alarm System**: Warns Glasstaff of party approach via telepathic bond
- **Territorial**: Attacks if party threatens alchemical equipment
- **Cowardly**: Flees if reduced to half HP or if Glasstaff is defeated

## Tactics
**Initial**: Remains invisible and observes party
**Warning**: Uses telepathic link to alert Glasstaff in R12
**Combat**: Stays airborne, uses poison claw attacks from above
**Retreat**: Flies to R12 to defend master if seriously threatened

## Detection Methods
- **Passive Perception 15+**: Notice small disturbances in workshop
- **Active Search**: DC 15 Perception to spot invisible movement
- **Magical Detection**: *Detect Magic*, *See Invisibility* reveal position
- **Environmental**: Disturbs dust, alchemical vapors, etc.

## Workshop Interaction
- **Alchemical Equipment**: Zeond knows which items are valuable/dangerous
- **Potion Brewing**: Currently brewing potion of invisibility (75% complete)
- **Book Protection**: Defends Glasstaff's research materials
- **Treasure Knowledge**: Knows location of hidden valuables

## Communication Abilities
- **Languages**: Understands Common and Abyssal
- **Intelligence**: Smart enough for basic negotiation
- **Loyalty**: Bound to Glasstaff but not suicidally devoted
- **Information**: Knows Glasstaff's recent activities and plans

## Interrogation Results (If Captured/Convinced)
- **DC 12**: Glasstaff expects "important visitors" soon
- **DC 15**: The Spider sent recent letters about Wave Echo Cave
- **DC 18**: Bugbears in R9 are Spider's agents, not Glasstaff's
- **Persuasion**: Might reveal Glasstaff's escape plans

## Familiar Bond
- **Telepathic Link**: 100-foot range with Glasstaff
- **Shared Senses**: Can relay visual information to master
- **Dismissal**: Returns to Abyss if Glasstaff dies
- **Summoning**: Takes 10 minutes and expensive components to resummon

## Shapechanger Forms
- **Bat Form**: AC 13, 40 ft fly, echolocation, keen hearing
- **Centipede Form**: AC 11, 20 ft climb, poison bite
- **Toad Form**: AC 11, 20 ft swim, standing leap
- **Fiend Form**: Default statistics above

## Development Options
- **Early Warning**: Alert allows Glasstaff to prepare defenses
- **Negotiation**: Might switch sides if Glasstaff clearly defeated
- **Ongoing Threat**: Could return later with new master
- **Information Source**: Valuable intelligence about wizard activities

## Workshop Treasure (Zeond's Knowledge)
- **Alchemical Reagents**: 3 rare components (25 gp each)
- **Brewing Potion**: Potion of invisibility (150 gp value when complete)
- **Hidden Cache**: Knows where Glasstaff hides emergency supplies
- **Research Notes**: Understands significance of Dwarvish journal

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce HP to 5, no Magic Resistance
**Harder**: Increase HP to 10, add *Cause Fear* spell (1/day)
**Stealth**: Emphasize invisible ambush tactics
**Roleplay**: Focus on negotiation and information gathering rather than combat
