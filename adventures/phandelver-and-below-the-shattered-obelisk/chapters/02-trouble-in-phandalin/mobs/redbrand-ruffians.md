# Redbrand Ruffians

## Role
Common thugs and enforcers of the Redbrand gang

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Brutish melee fighters who rely on intimidation and numbers
**Preferred Range**: Melee with shortswords, some crossbow support
**Key Abilities**: Multiattack, Pack Tactics when coordinated

## Stats

  AC: 12 (Leather Armor)

  Actions:

    Light Crossbow: +3 to hit, range 80/320, 1d8+1 piercing damage

## Encounter Variations

### Manor Barracks (R2)
- **Numbers**: 3 Redbrand ruffians
- **Tactics**: Grab weapons and fight from behind bunks for cover
- **Morale**: Fight to death to prove toughness to Glasstaff
- **Equipment**: Each has 2d10 sp, one has 2 garnets (10 gp each)

### Holding Cells (R5) 
- **Numbers**: 3 Redbrand ruffians (guards)
- **Tactics**: One stays back to threaten prisoners, two engage
- **Prisoner Leverage**: Will kill captives if party doesn't surrender
- **Equipment**: Standard gear plus cell keys

### Common Room (R10)
- **Numbers**: 2 Redbrand ruffians + 2 human bandits
- **Condition**: All intoxicated (disadvantage on Dex saves/checks)
- **Activity**: Playing knucklebones with loaded dice
- **Tactics**: Stumbling drunk combat, poor coordination

### Town Encounters
- **Patrol**: 2-3 ruffians extorting shopkeepers
- **Tavern**: 1-2 ruffians drinking and causing trouble
- **Intimidation**: Single ruffian threatening civilians

## Group Tactics
1. **Intimidation First**: Try to scare opponents into submission
2. **Overwhelming Force**: Attack weakest-looking party member
3. **Dirty Fighting**: Use environment, threaten innocent bystanders
4. **Retreat Threshold**: Flee when reduced to 1/3 numbers

## Personality Types
- **Braggart**: Talks tough but cowardly when outmatched
- **Sadist**: Enjoys causing pain, fights longer than smart
- **Pragmatist**: Switches sides when Redbrands clearly losing
- **True Believer**: Fanatically loyal to Glasstaff

## Equipment Variations
- **Standard**: Leather armor, shortsword, light crossbow
- **Veteran**: Studded leather, scimitar, hand crossbow
- **Recruit**: Hide armor, club, javelin
- **All Carry**: Red cloak (gang identification)

## Interrogation Information
- **DC 10**: Glasstaff is the boss, operates from manor
- **DC 15**: Secret entrance through tunnel from Alderleaf farm
- **DC 20**: Glasstaff is actually a wizard named Iarno
- **Torture**: Some work for "the Spider," don't know details

## Development Options
- **Captured**: Can be turned against Glasstaff with persuasion
- **Reformed**: Some might abandon criminal life after defeat
- **Vengeful**: Survivors could organize counter-attack
- **Recruitment**: Zhentarim interested in hiring former members

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce HP to 8, remove multiattack capability
**Harder**: Increase to 15 hp, add pack tactics when 2+ engage same target
**Environmental**: Use furniture and terrain for cover and improvised weapons
**Social**: Some ruffians might be convinced to surrender or switch sides

## Red Cloak Significance
- **Gang Colors**: Instant identification as Redbrand member
- **Intimidation**: Civilians recognize and fear the red cloak
- **Disguise**: Party can use cloaks to infiltrate (DC 12 Deception)
- **Password**: "Illefarn" gets past crypts without triggering skeletons
