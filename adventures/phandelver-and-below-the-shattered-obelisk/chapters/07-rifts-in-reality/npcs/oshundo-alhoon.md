## Quick Reference

**Role:** Undead mind flayer wizard seeking redemption and knowledge
**Motivation:** Oppose the fanatics and their corruption of mind flayer society
**Key Traits:** Scholarly, remorseful, pragmatic, surprisingly honorable
**Challenge:** Potential powerful ally or dangerous enemy

## Spells

**Cantrips (at will):** *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *prestidigitation*, *ray of frost*

**1st-6th Level (4/3/3/3/2/1 slots):**
- **6th:** *disintegrate*, *globe of invulnerability*
- **5th:** *dominate person*, *hold monster*, *wall of force*
- **4th:** *confusion*, *dimension door*, *greater invisibility*
- **3rd:** *counterspell*, *fireball*, *hold person*
- **2nd:** *detect thoughts*, *misty step*, *suggestion*
- **1st:** *magic missile*, *shield*, *charm person*

## Special Abilities

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Turn Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against any effect that turns undead.

**Undead Nature:** Doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep.

**Scholar's Memory:** Has perfect recall of everything learned in centuries of existence.

## Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes two Chilling Grasp attacks or casts a spell and makes one attack.

**Chilling Grasp:** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 10 (3d6) cold damage plus 10 (3d6) necrotic damage. If target is creature, it must make DC 17 Constitution save or be paralyzed for 1 minute (repeat save at end of each turn).

**Negate Spell (3/Day):** When a creature within 60 feet casts spell of 4th level or lower, Oshundo can negate it without a reaction.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Speaks with formal, archaic language patterns
- Genuinely curious about surface world developments
- Shows unexpected respect for scholarly pursuits

**Ideals:**
- Knowledge should be preserved and shared responsibly
- Redemption is possible even for the undead
- The pursuit of understanding transcends racial boundaries

**Bond:** Dedicated to preventing Ilvaash's influence from corrupting more mind flayers

**Flaw:** Sometimes underestimates the emotional responses of "lesser" beings

## Motivation & History

**Background:** Once a powerful mind flayer wizard who sought lichdom to escape the elder brain's control. The transformation into an alhoon granted independence but also cursed him with centuries of reflection on his people's cruelties.

**Current Goal:** Stop the fanatics from completing their ritual, as he believes it will corrupt the natural order and bring catastrophe to all intelligent life.

**Redemption Arc:** Has spent decades studying ethics and philosophy, genuinely seeking to atone for past predations.

## Potential Interactions

### Initial Meeting
**Cautious Diplomacy:** Oshundo approaches under *greater invisibility*, observing before revealing himself. Opens with telepathic communication.

**Opening:** *"Curious... surface dwellers in the deep places. Your thoughts suggest noble purpose. I am Oshundo, scholar of the void. We may have common cause against the fanatics."*

### Alliance Building
**Requirements for Trust:**
- Characters must demonstrate they're genuinely opposing the mind flayer threat
- Showing respect for knowledge and scholarship helps
- Proving they won't immediately attack an undead mind flayer

**What He Offers:**
- Detailed knowledge of Illithinoch's layout and defenses
- Information about the three fanatics' abilities and weaknesses
- Magical assistance during key encounters
- Safe passage through certain areas

### Potential Betrayal
**If Characters Prove Untrustworthy:**
- Attacks without warning using ambush tactics
- Uses knowledge of their capabilities against them
- Attempts to complete his own agenda (stealing obelisk fragments for study)

## Combat Tactics (If Hostile)

**Round 1:** Cast *greater invisibility* and assess threats
**Round 2-3:** Use *hold person* or *dominate person* on biggest threat
**Round 4+:** Focus damage spells on spellcasters, use *counterspell* defensively
**Bloodied:** Cast *dimension door* to reposition, use *globe of invulnerability*
**Desperate:** Attempt to negotiate or flee using *misty step*

## Information Oshundo Provides

**About the Fanatics:**
- Chishinix leads the trio and commands respect from other mind flayers
- Hashutu specializes in mental domination and psychological warfare
- Voalsh is the most physically dangerous and unpredictably violent
- All three have been corrupted by direct communion with Ilvaash

**About Illithinoch:**
- Layout of major chambers and passages
- Location of trapped areas and guardian creatures
- The elder brain's current infected state
- Secret passages he's used to avoid detection

**About the Ritual:**
- Requires all three obelisk fragments to be assembled
- Must be performed in the Far Realm for maximum effect
- The transformation would be permanent and irreversible
- Stopping it requires disrupting the fragments or defeating the fanatics

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 8):**
- Reduce spell levels by one tier across the board
- Remove *negate spell* ability
- Lower spell save DC to 16

**Harder (Party Level 11):**
- Add 7th level spell slots with *plane shift* and *teleport*
- Increase spell save DC to 18
- Add legendary actions (3 per turn)

## Rewards for Alliance

**Immediate Benefits:**
- Magical assistance during tough encounters
- Safe rest area in his hidden sanctum
- Identification and analysis of magical items

**Long-term Benefits:**
- Potential tutor for arcane knowledge
- Access to rare spell scrolls and components
- Information broker for future adventures

## DM Notes

- Oshundo represents the possibility of redemption even for traditionally evil creatures
- His scholarly nature provides exposition without feeling forced
- Can serve as a moral test - do characters judge based on race or actions?
- His undead nature might conflict with certain party members (clerics, paladins)
- Perfect for providing crucial information at dramatic moments
- Consider having him sacrifice himself to save the party during the climax

## Dialogue Examples

**Offering Alliance:** *"The fanatics pervert everything my people once stood for. Their worship of Ilvaash is an abomination that threatens the natural order itself."*

**Sharing Knowledge:** *"In my centuries of existence, I have learned that wisdom shared is wisdom multiplied. Take this knowledge and use it well."*

**Final Sacrifice:** *"I have spent too long as a parasite upon this world. Let my final act be one of creation rather than destruction."*
