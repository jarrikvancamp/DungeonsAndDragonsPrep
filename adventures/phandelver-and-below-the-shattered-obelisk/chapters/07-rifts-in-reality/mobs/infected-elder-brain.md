## Far Realm Corruption

**Ilvaash's Infection:** The elder brain is infected with Far Realm energy, making it more alien and unpredictable.

**Reality Distortion:** Within 120 feet of the brain, reality becomes unstable:
- Spells of 3rd level or higher have a 25% chance to produce a Wild Magic effect
- Gravity randomly reverses for 1 round (save DC 18 Dexterity or fall upward)
- Colors shift and change, imposing disadvantage on Perception checks

## Spells

**Cantrips (at will):** *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *prestidigitation*, *thaumaturgy*

**1st-9th Level Spells (1/1/1/1/1/1/1/1/1 slots):** 
- **9th:** *psychic lance*, *mass suggestion*
- **8th:** *dominate monster*, *feeblemind*
- **7th:** *plane shift*, *reverse gravity*
- **6th:** *mass suggestion*, *mental prison*
- **5th:** *dominate person*, *hold monster*, *modify memory*
- **4th:** *confusion*, *locate creature*, *polymorph*
- **3rd:** *counterspell*, *fireball*, *hold person*
- **2nd:** *detect thoughts*, *hold person*, *suggestion*
- **1st:** *charm person*, *command*, *shield*

## Special Abilities

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Creature Sense:** Knows the location of all creatures within 5 miles.

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day):** If it fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.

**Psychic Link:** Can communicate with all mind flayers within 5 miles.

**Far Realm Madness:** When a creature takes psychic damage from the brain, it must make DC 18 Wisdom save or gain a short-term madness (1d10 minutes).

## Attacks

**Tentacle:** +7 to hit, reach 30 ft. Hit: 20 (4d8+2) bludgeoning damage. If target is Large or smaller, it is grappled (escape DC 15) and must make DC 18 Intelligence save or be stunned until grapple ends.

**Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6):** 60-foot cone. All creatures must make DC 18 Intelligence save. Failed save: 32 (5d12+2) psychic damage and stunned for 1 minute. Success: Half damage, not stunned.

**Psychic Pulse (3/Day):** All creatures within 120 feet must make DC 18 Wisdom save. Failed save: 21 (6d6) psychic damage and frightened for 1 minute. Success: Half damage, not frightened.

## Legendary Actions (3 per turn)

- **Move:** Swim up to speed
- **Tentacle:** Make one tentacle attack
- **Cast Spell (2 actions):** Cast a spell of 3rd level or lower
- **Reality Warp (3 actions):** Cause one of the reality distortion effects in a 30-foot radius

## Lair Actions (Initiative Count 20)

1. **Summon Minions:** 1d4 intellect devourers appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet
2. **Psychic Storm:** All creatures within 60 feet take 10 (3d6) psychic damage (no save)
3. **Gravity Shift:** Gravity reverses in a 30-foot radius for 1 round
4. **Mind Control:** Take control of one charmed or dominated creature for 1 round

## Tactics

**Phase 1 (350-250 HP):** Use long-range spells and psychic abilities, maintain distance
**Phase 2 (250-150 HP):** Aggressive use of dominate spells and mind blast
**Phase 3 (150-75 HP):** Desperate tactics, reality warping, summoning minions
**Phase 4 (Below 75 HP):** All-out assault with legendary actions and lair actions

## Environmental Hazards

**Brain Pool:** 10 feet deep, difficult terrain. Swimming creatures have advantage on saves against the brain's abilities.

**Psychic Resonance Crystals:** Throughout the chamber, these can be attacked (AC 15, 25 HP) to reduce the brain's spell save DC by 1 (minimum DC 16).

**Unstable Reality Rifts:** Random portals that can teleport creatures to different parts of the chamber.

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 8):**
- Reduce HP to 250 (range 200-300)
- Lower spell save DC to 18
- Remove reality distortion effects

**Harder (Party Level 11):**
- Increase HP to 450 (range 400-500)
- Increase spell save DC to 21
- Add resistance to all damage types when below half HP

## Defeat Conditions

**Traditional Combat:** Reduce to 0 hit points through sustained assault

**Psychic Overload:** Deal 50+ psychic damage in a single round to cause temporary shutdown (stunned for 1 round, vulnerability to all damage)

**Ritual Disruption:** If characters disrupt the Far Realm connection, brain loses legendary actions and lair actions

## Loot

- **Elder Brain Fluid:** 3 vials worth 500 gp each (alchemy components)
- **Psychic Crystal Core:** Legendary item that grants telepathy 1 mile (5,000 gp value)
- **Far Realm Essence:** Can be used to craft items with planar properties
- **Mind Flayer Archives:** Information about Ilvaash and the fanatics' plans

## Key Information Gained

When defeated, characters can extract:
- Location of the Far Realm gateway
- True nature of Ilvaash's influence
- The three fanatics' weaknesses
- How to disrupt the transformation ritual

## DM Notes

- This is a major boss encounter that should feel epic and alien
- Use the reality distortion effects to create chaos and unpredictability
- The brain's vast intellect means it knows the characters are coming
- Its infection makes it more dangerous but also potentially unstable
- Consider multiple phases with different tactics and abilities
- The chamber should feel like being inside an alien mind

## Allies During Fight

**Possible Assistance:**
- Oshundo the Alhoon (if befriended earlier)
- Rescued prisoners who provide distractions
- Environmental elements that can be turned against the brain
