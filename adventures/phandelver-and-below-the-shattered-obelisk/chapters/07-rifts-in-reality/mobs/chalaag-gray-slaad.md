## Spells

**Cantrips (at will):** *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *prestidigitation*

**1st-5th Level (4/3/3/3/1 slots):** *counterspell*, *fireball*, *fly*, *invisibility*, *fear*, *tongues*

## Special Abilities

**Shapechanger:** Can use action to polymorph into Small or Medium humanoid, or back to true form. Stats remain the same except size. Equipment doesn't change. Reverts on death.

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Regeneration:** Regains 10 hit points at start of turn. If takes acid or fire damage, doesn't regenerate at start of next turn. Dies only if starts turn with 0 HP and doesn't regenerate.

**Chaos Aura:** When a creature within 10 feet casts a spell, roll d20:
- 1-5: Spell targets random creature within range
- 16-20: Spell is cast at one level higher (if possible)

## Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes three attacks: two Claws and one Bite.

**Bite:** +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

**Claw:** +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) slashing damage. If target is humanoid, it must make DC 16 Constitution save or be infected with chaos phage (see below).

**Chaos Bolt (Recharge 5-6):** Ranged spell attack: +6 to hit, range 120 ft. Hit: 16 (3d8+3) damage. Roll d8 for damage type: 1-acid, 2-cold, 3-fire, 4-force, 5-lightning, 6-poison, 7-psychic, 8-thunder. If rolled 7-8, bolt jumps to another target within 30 feet.

## Legendary Actions (3 per turn)

- **Move:** Move up to speed without provoking opportunity attacks
- **Claw Attack:** Make one claw attack
- **Cast Spell (2 actions):** Cast a spell of 2nd level or lower

## Chaos Phage

Infected creatures must make DC 16 Constitution save at end of each long rest. Three failures: creature's alignment becomes chaotic neutral (if not already), and they sprout slaad tadpole (remove with *greater restoration* or similar). Three successes: infection ends.

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Speaks in riddles and contradictions
- Fascinated by the intersection of chaos and Far Realm energies
- Collects "interesting" specimens for study

**Motivation:** Study the reality rifts and their chaotic properties

**Tactics:**
- Uses invisibility to ambush and confuse
- Employs hit-and-run tactics with shapechanging
- Spreads chaos phage to create long-term problems
- Retreats when reduced to 40 HP or less

## Environmental Integration

**Reality Rift Explorer:** Chalaag has been studying the Far Realm rifts and can:
- Predict when rifts will shift or change
- Use rift energy to enhance his chaos bolt
- Create temporary portals between rifts (1/day)

**Lair Effects:** If fought near a rift:
- Gravity randomly shifts every 2 rounds
- Wild magic surges occur more frequently
- Slaad can dimension door between rifts as bonus action

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 8):**
- Reduce HP to 95 (range 80-110)
- Remove legendary actions
- Chaos Aura only affects spells of 3rd level or higher

**Harder (Party Level 11):**
- Increase HP to 155 (range 140-170)
- Add *teleport* to spell list
- Chaos Aura extends to 20 feet

## Dialogue

**Opening:** "Ah, more specimens for the great experiment! Do you feel it? The beautiful chaos seeping through the cracks of reality?"

**Combat Taunts:**
- "Order is an illusion! Let me show you true freedom!"
- "Your structured minds cannot comprehend what approaches!"
- "Chaos embraces all - why do you resist?"

**Fleeing:** "The rifts call to me... we shall meet again when reality unravels further!"

## Loot

- **Chaos Shard:** Crystallized chaos energy (300 gp, random magical effects when touched)
- **Slaad Control Gem:** Can command lesser slaadi (rare item)
- **Rift Study Notes:** Detailed observations about Far Realm energy (valuable to scholars)
- **Potion of Confusion:** 2 doses
- **650 gp** in various planar currencies

## Information

**What Chalaag Knows:**
- The rifts are expanding and becoming more unstable
- Far Realm energy is "delicious chaos" unlike anything he's encountered
- The mind flayers are "boring orderly creatures trying to impose structure"
- Three "interesting specimens" passed through recently heading to the "bright chaotic center"

## DM Notes

- Represents the chaotic aspect threatening reality
- His study of the rifts provides exposition about the Far Realm threat
- Can be reasoned with if characters appeal to his curiosity
- His chaos phage creates ongoing consequences
- Perfect for hit-and-run tactics using the rift environment
- Consider having him return in later chapters if he escapes

## Alternative Encounter

**Negotiation Path:** DC 16 Charisma (Persuasion) check to convince him to share information in exchange for "chaos experiences" or interesting magical items.

**Study Partner:** If characters are also investigating the rifts, he might temporarily ally with them for mutual benefit.
