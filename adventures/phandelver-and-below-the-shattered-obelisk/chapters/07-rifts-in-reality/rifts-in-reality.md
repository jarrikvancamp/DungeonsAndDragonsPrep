## Chapter Overview

The characters have learned of the fanatics' headquarters: a mind flayer enclave called Illithinoch deep in the Underdark below Phandalin. In this chapter, the characters learn more about the fanatics and discover that they have already escaped through a gateway into the Far Realm. To pursue them, the characters must explore three Far Realm rifts scattered around Illithinoch and collect keys to open the gateway.

## Quick Reference

**Key Locations:**
- Phandalin (infected townspeople)
- Tunnel Journey (12 miles to Illithinoch)
- Illithinoch (mind flayer stronghold)
- Three Far Realm Rifts (Feeder Trenches, Spawn Hollow, Labyrinth of Eyes)

**Major NPCs:**
- Wheel-of-Fortune (tiefling showing transformation)
- Thorgran Ironquill (dwarf historian, now deceased)
- Oshundo the Alhoon (knowledgeable ally)
- Mind Flayer Fanatics (Chishinix, Hashutu, Voalsh)

**Primary Objectives:**
- Investigate corrupted townspeople in Phandalin
- Navigate dangerous tunnels to Illithinoch
- Explore the mind flayer stronghold
- Collect gnawbles from three Far Realm rifts
- Open the gateway to pursue the fanatics

## Shadow over Phandalin

The mind flayer fanatics haven't begun their ritual in earnest, but ripples of Far Realm energy have infected some residents of Phandalin. Those who spent significant time around the obelisk shards absorbed latent Netherese magic and are suffering strange maladies.

### Infected Townspeople Encounter

**Location:** Shrine of Luck
**Enemies:** Sister Garaele, Halia Thornton, and Ander (all use berserker stats)
**Modified Attack:** Psychic Slam (+5 to hit, 6 bludgeoning + 3 psychic damage)
**Goal:** Subdue rather than kill the infected townspeople

### The Fourth Infection - Wheel-of-Fortune

**Symptoms:**
- Purple skin gleams with thin sheen of mucus
- Skull and limbs have elongated
- Tentacles occasionally emerge and retract
- Light sensitivity (brighter than candlelight causes pain)

**Information Provided:**
- Found goblins in Sleeping Giant cellar
- Discovered sinkhole that wasn't there before
- Goblins and townspeople fled through sinkhole
- Senses powerful mind far beneath town
- Can see psychic energy as bright-green trails

## Illithinoch: The Mind Flayer Stronghold

**Type:** Ancient mind flayer stronghold
**Current State:** Mostly abandoned, taken over by fanatics
**Key Locations:** 16 areas (X1-X16)

### Illithinoch Features

- **Ceilings:** 14 feet high throughout
- **Doors:** Mental activation (no handles), create jarring pulse
- **Smooth shapes:** Rounded corners, organic feeling
- **Illumination:** Eerie green light (dim throughout)
- **Sigils of Ilvaash:** Moving symbols on walls (tentacles, brains, jaws)

### Major Encounters

**X2: Reception Hall**
- **Enemies:** 2 mind flayers (Shalghast and Ulthundul)
- **Roleplay:** Expect surface cultists or obelisk deliveries
- **Tactics:** Use detect thoughts to uncover deception

**X7: Voalsh's Lounge - Oshundo's Location**
- **NPC:** Oshundo the Alhoon (potential ally)
- **Information:** Knowledgeable about fanatics and their plans
- **Attitude:** Arrogant but willing to share information
- **Treasure:** Empowered mind crystal with historical maps

**X10: Experimentation Chamber**
- **Enemies:** Ahooshathan (mind flayer) and 2 nothics (modified)
- **Information:** Details about Far Realm rifts and ritual
- **Rift Access:** Leads to Labyrinth of Eyes

**X15: Elder Brain Pool**
- **Enemy:** Infected elder brain (major boss encounter)
- **State:** Paranoid, obsessed, and manipulated by fanatics
- **Abilities:** Creature Sense tracks movement throughout stronghold
- **Treasure:** 3 mind crystals (1 quickened, 2 extended)

**X16: Ritual Chamber - Gateway Location**
- **Key Area:** Where fanatics opened Far Realm gateway
- **Closed Gateway:** Requires gnawbles from three rifts to reopen
- **Planning Wall:** Shows sketches of three rune locations

### Key NPCs in Illithinoch

**Oshundo the Alhoon**
- Former Illithinoch resident driven out for practicing arcane magic
- Grudging ally if characters give him mind crystals
- Provides crucial information about fanatics and their plans
- **Knowledge:** Fanatics' weaknesses, gateway mechanics, Far Realm basics

**Gulguush (X14)**
- Mind flayer prophet, Chishinix's follower
- Writing prayer book about Ilvaash
- **Information Source:** Book reveals details about Ilvaash and the Far Realm

## Far Realm Rifts

Three rifts connect to different parts of the Far Realm. Each contains a gnawble (fuzzy orb with teeth) that grows from a faded rune. All three gnawbles are needed to reopen the gateway.

### Far Realm Exposure
- **First Entry:** DC 12 Constitution save or poisoned condition for 1 hour
- **Acclimation:** Only need to save once per rift location

### Rift Features
- **Strange Stone:** Moist stone that seeps gray ichor when damaged
- **Illumination:** Green-and-silver light throughout
- **Ceilings:** 18 feet high
- **Odd Sensations:** Each rift has unique psychological effects

## Feeder Trenches (X4 Access)

**Theme:** Grell feeding ground with strange purple sludge
**Sensation:** Perpetual hunger regardless of recent meals
**Key Locations:** 4 areas (F1-F4)

### Major Encounters

**F2: Common Eatery**
- **Enemies:** 3 grells feeding from troughs
- **Environment:** Sweet-smelling but bitter purple sludge

**F3: Private Eatery**
- **Enemy:** Feedkeeper Naruv (grell) + 1 other grell
- **Challenge:** Hostile to interruption of private meal
- **Treasure:** Silver tiara (1,200 gp), quickened mind crystal

**F4: Food Source - Gnawble Location**
- **Enemies:** None (nothics are mostly dead)
- **Key NPC:** Bashudu (dying nothic with information)
- **Important:** Contains first gnawble needed for gateway

## Spawn Hollow (X5 Access)

**Theme:** Slaad breeding ground with moving flesh
**Sensation:** Feeling of skin slowly shifting over muscles
**Key Locations:** 4 areas (S1-S4)

### Major Encounters

**S1: Entry Cavern**
- **Traps:** 2 glyphs of warding (thunder damage, DC 12)
- **Hidden Enemy:** Chalaag (gray slaad) casts invisibility if glyph triggers

**S2: Crystal Pit**
- **Enemies:** 1 red slaad + 12 slaad tadpoles
- **Environment:** Whirlpool origin point for moving flesh

**S3: Flesh Vortex Chamber**
- **Enemy:** Chalaag (gray slaad explorer and leader)
- **Mechanics:** Vortex transports creatures to S2 after 2d6 hours
- **Tactics:** Uses glyph of warding and spell abilities

**S4: Rune Alcove - Gnawble Location**
- **Environment:** Only non-moving area in Spawn Hollow
- **Important:** Contains second gnawble needed for gateway

## Labyrinth of Eyes (X10 Access)

**Theme:** Former beholder lair, now maze of moving eyes
**Sensation:** Constant feeling of being watched
**Key Locations:** 7 areas (L1-L7)

### Major Encounters

**L2: Views of Madness**
- **Trap:** Magical eyepiece causes feeblemind (DC 18)
- **Benefit:** Successful view provides knowledge of Briny Pool navigation
- **Destruction:** Can be disabled with dispel magic (DC 18)

**L3: The Rolling Eye**
- **Mechanics:** Large stone orb must be rotated to access different areas
- **Navigation:** Requires coordination to reach all areas

**L6: Rune Chamber - Gnawble Location**
- **Important:** Contains third gnawble needed for gateway
- **Access:** Requires navigating the rolling orb mechanism

**L7: Golcuus's Lair**
- **Enemy:** Golcuus (oculorb - transformed beholder)
- **State:** Miserable, attacks non-mind flayers on sight
- **Treasure:** Jeweler's tools, carved rubies, valuable statuette (5,500 gp)

### Rolling Eye Mechanics
- **Function:** 8-foot holes connect to internal tunnels
- **Operation:** Must be rotated from outside to align passages
- **Strategy:** Requires leaving someone behind or careful planning

## Opening the Gateway

### Requirements
- Collect one gnawble from each of the three Far Realm rifts
- Bring all three gnawbles within 20 feet of the closed gateway in X16
- Gateway remains open for 7 days after opening

### Gateway Activation
When the gateway opens:
- Gnawbles croon in delight and explode into flesh globs
- Portal reveals grayish-pink tunnels like folds of a titanic brain
- Psychic image shows fanatics dragging Phandalin prisoners through portal

## Combat Tactics by Location

### Illithinoch Tactics
- **Mind Flayers:** Use detect thoughts and mind blast liberally
- **Elder Brain:** Tracks characters throughout stronghold with Creature Sense
- **Aberrations:** Coordinate attacks when possible

### Far Realm Rift Tactics
- **Grells:** Use aerial advantage and paralysis
- **Slaadi:** Aggressive melee with spell support from leader
- **Oculorb:** Desperate attacks due to transformed state

## Treasure Summary

### Major Magic Items
- **Mind Crystals:** Various types throughout Illithinoch
- **Historical Maps:** Worth 4,000 gp from Oshundo
- **Spell Scrolls:** Freedom of movement, comprehend languages

### Monetary Rewards
- **Illithinoch:** Various mind crystals and alchemical components
- **Far Realm Rifts:** 15,000+ gp in treasure across all three rifts

## Scaling Options

### Easier (Level 8-9 Party)
- Reduce tunnel random encounters to every 2 hours
- Provide additional mind crystals with beneficial effects
- Allow gnawbles to be obtained more easily

### Harder (Level 10+ Party)
- Add additional patrols in Illithinoch
- Increase Far Realm rift encounters
- Make gateway opening require additional components

## What's Next?

After completing this chapter, the characters should have:
- Understanding of the Far Realm threat
- Open gateway to pursue the fanatics
- Knowledge of Ilvaash and the true scope of the danger
- Rescued some townspeople but learned others are still captive

The characters are now ready to enter the Far Realm itself in Chapter 8: Beyond a Lightless Star.

## DM Notes

### Managing the Investigation
- Allow characters to explore Illithinoch in any order
- Use the infected elder brain's awareness to create tension
- Oshundo can provide exposition if characters miss key information

### Far Realm Rifts
- Each rift has a different tone and challenge type
- Characters only need the gnawbles, but exploration provides valuable treasure
- Consider allowing characters to return to rifts later if needed

### Foreshadowing Chapter 8
- Gulguush's prayer book reveals information about the Briny Maze
- Gateway vision shows the fanatics' destination
- Emphasize the scale of the threat to Phandalin
