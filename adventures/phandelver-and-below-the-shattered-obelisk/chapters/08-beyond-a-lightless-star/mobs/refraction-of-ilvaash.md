## Far Realm Nature

**Reality Distortion Aura:** Within 60 feet of Ilvaash's refraction:
- Physics operate inconsistently (DM discretion for creative effects)
- Spells of 4th level or higher have 50% chance for Wild Magic surge
- Critical hits and failures have additional random effects
- Time flows differently (initiative order may randomly change)

**Incorporeal Movement:** Can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. Takes 5 (1d10) force damage if it ends turn inside object.

**Far Realm Regeneration:** Regains 20 hit points at start of each turn. If reduced to 0 HP, makes DC 15 Constitution save to instead be reduced to 1 HP (once per encounter).

## Special Abilities

**Legendary Resistance (3/Day):** If it fails a saving throw, can choose to succeed instead.

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Reality Anchor Weakness:** Takes double damage from attacks made with objects native to the Material Plane (not summoned or created by magic).

**Multiphase Existence:** When reduced to 200 HP or fewer, the refraction splits into three smaller forms (see Phase 2).

## Phase 1: Unified Form (400-201 HP)

### Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes three Void Lash attacks or casts two spells.

**Void Lash:** +13 to hit, reach 15 ft. Hit: 18 (2d10+7) force damage plus 14 (4d6) psychic damage. Target must make DC 22 Wisdom save or be stunned until end of next turn.

**Reality Rend (Recharge 5-6):** Creates a 30-foot radius sphere of altered reality. All creatures in area must make DC 22 Constitution save. Failed save: 35 (10d6) force damage and teleported to random location within 120 feet. Success: Half damage, not teleported.

**Mind Shatter (1/Day):** All creatures within 60 feet must make DC 22 Intelligence save. Failed save: 42 (12d6) psychic damage and Intelligence reduced by 1d4. Success: Half damage, no ability reduction.

### Legendary Actions (3 per turn)

- **Move:** Fly up to speed
- **Void Lash:** Make one void lash attack
- **Warp Reality (2 actions):** Change one environmental factor within 60 feet
- **Cast Spell (3 actions):** Cast any spell of 5th level or lower

## Phase 2: Fractured Forms (200-1 HP)

When reduced to 200 HP, Ilvaash splits into three aspects:

### Aspect of Madness
- **HP:** 80 (shares damage with other aspects)
- **Focus:** Psychic attacks and mind control
- **Abilities:** *Confusion*, *dominate monster*, continuous *detect thoughts*

### Aspect of Chaos  
- **HP:** 80 (shares damage with other aspects)
- **Focus:** Reality manipulation and randomness
- **Abilities:** Wild magic effects, gravity reversal, time distortion

### Aspect of Hunger
- **HP:** 80 (shares damage with other aspects)  
- **Focus:** Life drain and physical destruction
- **Abilities:** Energy drain, disintegration, necrotic damage

**Shared HP Pool:** All three aspects share a combined 240 HP pool. Destroying one aspect removes its abilities but doesn't reduce the HP pool.

## Lair Actions (Initiative Count 20)

1. **Gravity Reversal:** Gravity reverses in a 40-foot radius for 1 round
2. **Temporal Distortion:** One creature loses their next turn, another gains an extra turn
3. **Reality Storm:** All creatures within 30 feet take 14 (4d6) force damage (no save)
4. **Summon Aberrations:** 1d4 intellect devourers appear in unoccupied spaces
5. **Mind Meld:** Two random creatures must swap positions and initiative orders
6. **Void Rift:** Creates a 10-foot radius sphere of nothingness that deals 21 (6d6) force damage to anything that enters

## Environmental Hazards

**Ilvaash's Anima Chamber Features:**
- **Floating Platforms:** Connected by bridges that may randomly appear/disappear
- **Psychic Crystals:** Can be attacked (AC 15, 25 HP) to reduce Ilvaash's save DCs by 1
- **Reality Anchors:** Ancient Material Plane artifacts that, if activated (DC 20 Arcana), grant advantage against Ilvaash's abilities
- **Obelisk Assembly:** The three fragments form a nexus that Ilvaash draws power from

## Tactics

### Phase 1 Tactics
- **Opening:** Use Reality Rend to scatter the party
- **Early Combat:** Focus void lash on spellcasters
- **Mid Combat:** Use Mind Shatter when multiple targets are grouped
- **Legendary Actions:** Warp Reality to create disadvantageous conditions

### Phase 2 Tactics (Fractured)
- **Aspect of Madness:** Dominates strongest fighter, uses confusion on groups
- **Aspect of Chaos:** Creates environmental hazards, wild magic effects
- **Aspect of Hunger:** Drains life from wounded targets, blocks healing

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 11):**
- Reduce HP to 300 (Phase 1) and 180 (Phase 2)
- Lower save DCs to 20
- Remove condition immunities in Phase 1

**Harder (Party Level 14):**
- Increase HP to 500 (Phase 1) and 300 (Phase 2)
- Increase save DCs to 24
- Add fourth aspect in Phase 2 (Aspect of Void)

## Victory Conditions

**Primary:** Reduce the refraction to 0 hit points through combat

**Alternative - Obelisk Disruption:** DC 25 Arcana check to turn the obelisk fragments against Ilvaash (requires all three fragments and 3 rounds of concentration)

**Environmental:** Activate all Reality Anchors to weaken Ilvaash sufficiently that it retreats to the Far Realm

## Defeat Sequence

**Final Form Collapse:**
1. Reality distortions intensify as Ilvaash weakens
2. The Far Realm begins rejecting the weakened godlet
3. Ilvaash makes desperate attempt to possess a character (DC 22 Charisma save)
4. Final death throes create reality rift that must be closed

**Aftermath:**
- Obelisk fragments become inert and safe
- Far Realm influence retreats from the area  
- Any transformed creatures begin reverting to normal
- Portal back to Material Plane stabilizes

## Loot

**Unique Rewards:**
- **Ilvaash's Core Fragment:** Legendary item that grants immunity to psychic damage and telepathy 1 mile
- **Reality Anchor Shards:** 3 rare items that grant advantage against aberration abilities
- **Far Realm Essence:** Crafting material for plane-shifting items
- **Obelisk Fragments:** Can be purified and used for powerful protective magic

**Standard Treasure:**
- **5,000 gp** worth of rare gems that exist in multiple dimensions simultaneously
- **Scroll of Wish** (one-time use to undo any negative effects from the campaign)
- **Manual of Bodily Health** (campaign completion reward)

## Victory Speech Options

**Heroic Ending:** *"As Ilvaash's refraction dissolves into nothingness, reality reasserts itself around you. The nightmare is over, and Phandalin is safe."*

**Bittersweet Ending:** *"Though Ilvaash retreats, its influence has left permanent scars on reality. You have won, but the cosmos remembers what nearly was."*

**Triumphant Ending:** *"Your victory echoes across dimensions. You have not merely saved Phandalin, but prevented a catastrophe that would have remade all existence."*

## DM Notes

- This is the ultimate campaign boss - should feel genuinely alien and dangerous
- Reality distortion effects should be creative but not game-breaking
- Multiple victory conditions prevent frustration if combat goes poorly
- The phase transition creates a dramatic mid-fight climax
- Environmental interactions are crucial for success
- Consider foreshadowing Ilvaash's abilities in earlier chapters
- The defeat should feel satisfying and conclusive to the entire campaign

## Campaign Conclusion

Defeating Ilvaash's refraction completes the "Phandelver and Below: The Shattered Obelisk" campaign. Characters should be 12th-13th level and have saved not just Phandalin, but potentially all of reality from a cosmic horror that defies comprehension.
