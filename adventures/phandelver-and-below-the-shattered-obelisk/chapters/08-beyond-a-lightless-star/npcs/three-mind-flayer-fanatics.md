## Overview

The three mind flayer fanatics - Chishinix, Hashutu, and Voalsh - serve as the primary antagonists of the campaign. Each has been enhanced by their communion with Ilvaash and represents a different aspect of mind flayer corruption.

```yaml

  AC: 17 (Natural Armor)
  HP: 180 (range 160-200)

  STR: 11, DEX: 12, CON: 15, INT: 22, WIS: 20, CHA: 18
```

### Chishinix Spells

**Cantrips (at will):** *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *thaumaturgy*, *toll the dead*

**1st-8th Level (4/3/3/3/2/1/1/1 slots):**
- **8th:** *dominate monster*, *feeblemind*
- **7th:** *plane shift*, *reverse gravity*
- **6th:** *mass suggestion*, *mental prison*
- **5th:** *dominate person*, *geas*, *hold monster*
- **4th:** *confusion*, *greater invisibility*, *polymorph*
- **3rd:** *counterspell*, *fireball*, *hold person*
- **2nd:** *detect thoughts*, *suggestion*, *hold person*
- **1st:** *charm person*, *command*, *shield*

### Chishinix Abilities

**Ilvaash's Voice:** Can cast *command* (save DC 20) on all creatures within 60 feet as a bonus action (3/day).

**Prophet's Vision:** Can see 6 seconds into the future, gaining +4 to AC and all saving throws for 1 minute (1/day).

**Ritual Leadership:** While all three fanatics are alive, Chishinix gains advantage on concentration saves and can cast spells as legendary actions.

### Voalsh Abilities

**Enhanced Physique:** Ilvaash's corruption has made Voalsh unnaturally strong and resilient.

**Berserker Rage:** When reduced to half HP, gains resistance to all damage types and +2 to all attack rolls for remainder of combat.

**Tentacle Flurry:** Can make four tentacle attacks as an action instead of multiattack (recharge 5-6).

## Shared Abilities (All Three)

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Ilvaash's Blessing:** Each fanatic can cast one spell of 5th level or lower without expending a spell slot (1/day).

**Collective Consciousness:** While all three are alive, they share initiative, can communicate telepathically across any distance, and gain +2 to all saving throws.

**Far Realm Adaptation:** Immune to the exhaustion effects of being in the Far Realm.

## Legendary Actions (3 per turn, shared pool)

Only when all three fanatics are alive:
- **Move:** One fanatic moves up to its speed
- **Attack:** One fanatic makes one attack
- **Cast Spell (2 actions):** One fanatic casts a spell of 3rd level or lower
- **Coordinate (3 actions):** All three fanatics can move and take one action immediately

## Encounter Phases

### Phase 1: All Three Alive (Most Dangerous)
- Chishinix coordinates from the back, casting high-level spells
- Hashutu focuses on dominating key party members
- Voalsh charges into melee to protect the others
- They use legendary actions to maintain constant pressure

### Phase 2: Two Remaining
- Surviving fanatics become more desperate and aggressive
- Lose legendary actions but individual abilities become stronger
- Focus fire on whoever killed their ally

### Phase 3: Final Fanatic
- Gains multiattack if they don't already have it
- All abilities recharge on a 4-6 instead of 5-6
- Becomes suicidally aggressive, using all resources

## Environmental Factors

**Ilvaash's Anima Chamber:**
- Reality is unstable - spell effects may be altered
- Psychic energy enhances all mind flayer abilities
- Gravity shifts randomly every 3 rounds
- The obelisk fragments pulse with dark energy, providing cover

## Tactics by Fanatic

### Chishinix Tactics
- Stays mobile using flight
- Prioritizes *dominate monster* and *feeblemind*
- Uses *counterspell* to protect allies
- Retreats to cast *plane shift* if alone and dying

### Hashutu Tactics
- Dominates the strongest fighter immediately
- Uses *hypnotic pattern* to control battlefield
- Maintains multiple dominated targets
- Focuses on disabling rather than killing

### Voalsh Tactics
- Charges strongest melee threat immediately
- Uses *haste* on self for maximum attacks
- Protects spellcasting allies from interruption
- Goes berserk when bloodied, attacking recklessly

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 10):**
- Reduce each fanatic's HP by 30
- Lower spell save DCs by 1
- Remove legendary actions entirely

**Harder (Party Level 13):**
- Increase each fanatic's HP by 40
- Add 9th level spell slots to Chishinix
- Grant each fanatic a unique lair action

## Defeat and Resolution

**Disrupting the Ritual:**
- Defeating all three stops the transformation immediately
- Destroying the obelisk assembly also ends the threat
- Characters can potentially turn the ritual against Ilvaash itself

**Final Words:**
- **Chishinix:** *"Ilvaash... forgive us... the grand design... incomplete..."*
- **Hashutu:** *"Your minds... so small... you cannot comprehend... what you've lost..."*
- **Voalsh:** *"Death... is nothing... the Far Realm... eternal..."*

## DM Notes

- This is the campaign's climactic encounter and should feel epic
- Each fanatic has distinct personality and tactics
- The phases create a dynamic, evolving battle
- Environmental effects add chaos without being overwhelming
- Consider multiple victory conditions beyond just combat
- Their defeat should feel satisfying and complete the story arc
