# Psionic Goblins

## Role
Standard goblins enhanced with minor telepathic abilities by mind flayer influence

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Coordinated group tactics using telepathic communication
**Preferred Range**: Mixed ranged and melee with perfect coordination
**Key Abilities**: Telepathic communication, enhanced tactics, mental resistance

## Stats
```yaml
Psionic Goblin:
  AC: 15 (Leather Armor, Shield)
  HP: 9 (range 7-12)
  Speed: 30 ft
  STR: 8 DEV: 14 CON: 10 INT: 12 WIS: 10 CHA: 8
  
  Skills: Stealth +6
  Damage Resistances: Psychic
  Senses: Darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 9
  Languages: Common, Goblin, telepathy 30 ft
  Challenge: 1/2 (100 XP each)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Nimble Escape: Disengage or Hide as bonus action
    Telepathic Network: Silent communication with other psionics within range
    Pack Tactics: Advantage when ally adjacent to target
    Mental Resilience: Advantage against charm and fear effects
  
  Actions:
    Scimitar: +4 to hit, 1d6+2 slashing damage
    Shortbow: +4 to hit, range 80/320, 1d6+2 piercing damage
    Mind Spike: +3 to hit, range 30 ft, 1d4+1 psychic damage
```

## Group Dynamics

### Squad Formations
**Scout Pair**: 2 goblins with enhanced stealth and reconnaissance
**War Band**: 4-6 goblins with mixed ranged and melee capabilities  
**Assault Pack**: 8-10 goblins for major attacks on settlements

### Telepathic Coordination
- **Silent Communication**: No verbal commands needed
- **Tactical Adaptation**: Instant strategy changes mid-combat
- **Ambush Perfection**: Coordinated attacks without sound cues
- **Emergency Alerts**: Instant warning system across network

## Enhanced Intelligence
**Strategic Thinking**: Plan complex ambushes and tactical maneuvers
**Tool Use**: Employ traps, environmental hazards, and improvised weapons
**Learning Capacity**: Adapt tactics based on previous encounters
**Command Structure**: Follow orders from psionic leaders without question

## Psionic Abilities
**Telepathy Range**: 30-foot radius for silent communication
**Mind Spike**: Limited psychic attack for direct mental damage
**Mental Resistance**: Enhanced willpower against mental intrusion
**Network Effect**: Stronger when in groups with other psionics

## Combat Tactics
**Opening Round**: Coordinated ranged volley with perfect timing
**Melee Engagement**: Surround isolated targets using pack tactics
**Retreat Coordination**: Synchronized withdrawal when outmatched
**Environmental Use**: Employ terrain advantages with group coordination

## Encounter Locations

### Phandalin Outskirts
- **Patrol Groups**: 2-3 goblins scouting settlement approaches
- **Ambush Points**: 4-6 goblins at strategic road positions
- **Observation Posts**: Individual scouts with telepathic relay capability

### Goblin Territory
- **Camp Guards**: 6-8 goblins protecting psionic leadership
- **Hunting Parties**: 3-4 goblins gathering resources and intelligence
- **Ritual Circles**: Groups participating in psionic enhancement ceremonies

## Behavioral Changes
**Pre-Enhancement**: Standard goblin cowardice and disorganization
**Post-Enhancement**: Calm confidence and coordinated action
**Loyalty Shift**: From tribal to absolute devotion to psionic cause
**Intelligence Boost**: From cunning to genuine strategic thinking

## Conversion Process
**Stage 1**: Exposure to brain coral emanations for 1-2 weeks
**Stage 2**: Direct telepathic contact with enhanced goblins
**Stage 3**: Ritual enhancement by Ruxithid or other psionic leader
**Result**: Permanent mental enhancement and loyalty programming

## Environmental Effects
**Brain Coral Influence**: Areas with 10+ psionic goblins develop coral growths
**Mental Resonance**: Non-psionics feel uneasy in heavily influenced areas
**Animal Behavior**: Local wildlife avoids psionic goblin territories
**Plant Changes**: Vegetation shows geometric growth patterns

## Telepathic Network Mechanics
**Range Limitation**: Individual telepathy limited to 30 feet
**Network Extension**: Range extends through chain of connected minds
**Information Flow**: Intelligence passes instantly through entire network
**Disruption Effects**: Killing network member causes temporary confusion

## Alternative Resolutions
**Conversion Reversal**: Destroying brain coral source may restore normal goblins
**Negotiation**: Enhanced intelligence allows for complex bargaining
**Infiltration**: Telepathic detection makes stealth approaches difficult
**Environmental**: Attacking coral growths weakens psionic abilities

## Regional Impact
**Settlement Threat**: Coordinated raids more effective than standard goblin attacks
**Intelligence Network**: Perfect reconnaissance of all regional activities
**Recruitment**: Converting other goblin tribes to expand influence
**Preparation**: Gathering resources for larger mind flayer operations

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce telepathy range, remove mind spike ability
**Harder**: Add psionic goblin sergeants with enhanced abilities
**Investigation**: Emphasize intelligence gathering over direct combat
**Horror**: Focus on unnatural coordination and mental influence

## Treasure
**Individual**: Standard goblin equipment plus brain coral fragments
**Group**: Collected intelligence documents and regional maps
**Ritual Sites**: Psionic enhancement materials and aberrant components
**Network Value**: Information about mind flayer operations and goals

## Development Hooks
1. **Conversion Source**: Tracking down the origin of psionic enhancement
2. **Network Infiltration**: Attempting to use captured goblins for intelligence
3. **Reversal Research**: Finding ways to break psionic conditioning
4. **Escalation Warning**: Evidence of larger aberrant threat approaching

## Long-term Consequences
- **Regional Security**: Enhanced goblin threat requires organized response
- **Detection Network**: Psionic goblins provide early warning of party movements
- **Conversion Risk**: Party members may become targets for enhancement
- **Campaign Escalation**: Introduction to mind flayer influence mechanics

## Tactical Notes for DMs
**Silent Coordination**: No verbal cues for party to detect tactical changes
**Perfect Timing**: All actions coordinated for maximum effectiveness
**Intelligence Advantage**: Goblins learn from previous encounters
**Moral Questions**: Should enhanced goblins be treated as victims or threats?
