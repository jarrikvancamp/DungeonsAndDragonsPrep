# Duergar Guards (Zorzula's Rest)

## Role
Gray dwarf guards maintaining order at the underground trading outpost

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Disciplined formation fighting with magical abilities
**Preferred Range**: Melee with crossbow support and battlefield control
**Key Abilities**: Enlargement, Invisibility, Formation tactics, Underdark experience

## Stats
```yaml
Duergar Guard:
  AC: 16 (Scale Mail, Shield)
  HP: 26 (range 20-32)
  Speed: 25 ft
  STR: 14 CON: 14 DEX: 11 INT: 11 WIS: 10 CHA: 9
  
  Damage Resistances: Poison
  Senses: Darkvision 120 ft, passive Perception 10
  Languages: Dwarvish, Undercommon
  Challenge: 1 (200 XP each)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Duergar Resilience: Advantage against poison, spells, illusions
    Sunlight Sensitivity: Disadvantage in bright light
    Duergar Magic (1/day each): Enlarge/reduce (self only), invisibility (self only)
  
  Actions:
    War Pick: +4 to hit, 1d8+2 piercing damage
    Javelin: +4 to hit, range 30/120, 1d6+2 piercing damage
    Heavy Crossbow: +2 to hit, range 100/400, 1d10 piercing damage
    
  Enlarged Form (when enlarged):
    Size: Medium
    War Pick: +4 to hit, 2d8+2 piercing damage
    Advantage on Strength checks and saves
```

## Group Formations

### Standard Patrol
**Composition**: 2-3 duergar guards with mixed equipment
**Formation**: Shield wall with crossbow support from rear
**Tactics**: Coordinated advance with enlarged front fighters

### Outpost Defense
**Gate Guards**: 2 guards at main entrance, alternating enlarged/invisible
**Interior Patrol**: 1-2 guards making rounds, using invisibility for surprise
**Emergency Response**: 4-6 guards respond to major threats

### Trading Escort
**Caravan Guard**: 3-4 guards protecting valuable shipments
**Scout**: 1 invisible guard ranging ahead for threats
**Rearguard**: 1-2 guards watching for pursuit

## Tactical Abilities

### Enlarge/Reduce Usage
**Combat Opening**: Front-line fighters enlarge for increased damage
**Defensive**: Reduce to fit through smaller spaces or gain AC
**Intimidation**: Enlarge during negotiations for psychological advantage
**Tactical**: Enlarge heavy hitters, keep crossbow users normal size

### Invisibility Tactics
**Ambush Setup**: Turn invisible before combat to gain surprise
**Flanking**: Invisible guards move to advantageous positions
**Escape**: Use invisibility to retreat and regroup
**Reconnaissance**: Scout enemy positions without detection

## Combat Tactics
**Round 1**: Front guards enlarge, rear guards prepare crossbows
**Round 2**: Enlarged guards charge, crossbows provide covering fire
**Round 3+**: Maintain formation, use invisibility for repositioning
**Retreat**: Invisible guards cover withdrawal of enlarged fighters

## Environmental Advantages
**Underdark Knowledge**: Superior navigation in underground areas
**Low Light**: Darkvision gives significant advantage in dim conditions
**Terrain Use**: Familiar with underground tactical positions
**Fortifications**: Know all defensive positions in Zorzula's Rest

## Formation Fighting
**Shield Wall**: Overlapping shields provide enhanced AC to formation
**Crossbow Line**: Coordinated volleys for maximum impact
**Spear Hedge**: Extended reach weapons prevent enemy charges
**Disciplined Retreat**: Organized withdrawal maintaining formation integrity

## Zorzula's Rest Duties
**Gate Security**: Control access to outpost, inspect visitors
**Patrol Routes**: Regular circuits of common areas and perimeter
**Merchant Protection**: Escort valuable traders and their goods
**Order Maintenance**: Resolve disputes and maintain trading protocols

## Negotiation Approach
**Professional**: Business-like approach to most interactions
**Suspicious**: Wary of surface dwellers and unusual requests
**Lawful**: Follow outpost rules strictly but fairly
**Practical**: Open to reasonable arrangements and mutual benefit
