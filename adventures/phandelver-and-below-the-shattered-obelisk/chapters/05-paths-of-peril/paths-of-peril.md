## Chapter Overview

When characters return to Phandalin after Wave Echo Cave, they find the town plagued by crimes committed by strange psionic goblins called the Sawplees. These goblins seek obelisk shards scattered around town, following orders from their leader Ruxithid the Chosen, who believes he serves mighty gods (actually mind flayer fanatics from the Far Realm).

**Level Advancement:**
- Gain a level when discovering Zorzula's Rest location and determining they must confront Ruxithid
- Gain a level when defeating Ruxithid and learning of the larger sinister plan

## DM Read-Aloud

*The rumbling of an agitated crowd greets your journey back into town. A gaggle of townsfolk has gathered around townmaster Harbin Wester's home, some shouting for Harbin to come outside and others decrying his leadership. A few individuals display minor injuries, and all seem anxious and frustrated.*

## Story Background: The Goblins' Story

The Sawplee goblins inhabit Zorzula's Rest, an abandoned duergar mining outpost. Centuries ago, mind flayers conquered the outpost, exposing the goblins to aberrant powers that granted some psionic abilities. Recently, mind flayer fanatics planted a mysterious gemstone for their leader Ruxithid to find, creating a telepathic link. The "gods" demand Ruxithid's goblins find black stone shards from an ancient Netherese obelisk once erected near Phandalin.

## Part 1: Investigation Phase

### Townmaster's Plight

**Harbin's Condition:** Frazzled, flushed cheeks, dark bags under eyes.

**The Job:** Investigate four crime scenes, help victims, stop the culprits. Payment: 50 gp each up front, 150 gp each on completion (DC 11 Intimidation/Persuasion doubles to 100/300 gp).

**Harbin's Fear:** DC 12 Insight reveals he wants characters to investigate because he's too frightened to do it himself.

### Crime Scenes (Investigate in Any Order)

#### Barthen's Well
**Location:** Behind Barthen's Provisions

**Crime:** Goblins destroyed well winch to steal obelisk shard used as winch drum, then fled underground, caving in tunnel and damming water source.

**Crime Scene Investigation:**
- Well walls toppled, roof splintered and caved
- Winch and pulley smashed (DC 15 Investigation: drum is missing)
- Low gurgling growl from well (Passive Perception 15, DC 17 Nature: irritated creature)

**Witness: Ander** (halfling commoner, teenage clerk)
- **Base of Operations:** Heard vandal shout "Hurry! Back to the Rest!"
- **Description:** "Strange folk, bigger than me, long flat ears, big heads, hands that glowed with magic"
- **Dry Well:** Been dry since attack, suspects vandals dammed water source
- **Strange Behavior:** Arrived at sundown, ignored store stock, went straight for well
- **Well Curse:** DC 13 Persuasion reveals Ander had nightmares from well water (actually caused by obelisk shard)

**Encounter: Well Cavern**
- **Access:** 20-foot climb (DC 10 Athletics with rope, 2d6 damage if fail)
- **Enemy:** 1 otyugh (feeding on trash, hostile)
- **Cave-In:** Blocks headstream, takes 1 hour to clear (10 min per additional helper, min 10 min)
- **Goblin Body:** Strange psionic goblin with elongated skull and green skin streaks (not Cragmaw)
- **Northern Tunnel:** Leads to Zorzula's Rest area (optional approach)

#### Miner's Exchange
**Location:** Phandalin's mining guild building

**Crime:** Goblins stole obelisk shard from ceremonial cornerstone, weakening building foundation.

**Crime Scene Investigation:**
- Building tilts precariously, miners working frantically on foundation
- Halia Thornton directing repairs with tense expression

**Repairing the Building:** 
- Each character: DC 12 Strength or Dexterity check
- Proficiency with mason's tools grants advantage
- Need 2+ successes to help finish repairs
- Alternative: Creative magical solutions

**Witness: Halia Thornton** (after repairs completed)
- **Lone Witness:** Only one at trading post during evening attack
- **Stolen Stone:** Goblins took "lucky stone" from cornerstone
- **Swapping Blows:** She was injured, hurt several goblins badly
- **Strange Magic:** One goblin pushed her with invisible magical force
- **Following Goblins:** Points toward woods south of Phandalin

**Encounter: Goblin Camp**
- **Location:** 1 hour south of Phandalin in clearing
- **Enemies:** 3 goblin psi brawlers (injured from Halia fight)
- **Eavesdropping:** "Hope the others made it back, else Ruxithid's gonna have our heads!"
- **Stealth:** DC 16 to avoid detection
- **Surrender:** If one killed, others try to surrender
- **Interrogation:** See "What the Goblins Know" section

#### Shrine of Luck
**Location:** Tymora shrine outside town

**Crime:** Goblins removed obelisk shard from shrine's archway centerpiece, causing collapse.

**Crime Scene Investigation:**
- Sister Garaele and 10-year-old Tamara Stonehill sorting rubble
- Collapsed cairn of colorful rocks and offerings
- **Items Found:** Broken mining pick, small hasty footprints, axe embedded in wall
- **The Clue:** Axe has duergar runes (DC 17 History to recognize, Gwyn knows immediately)

**Helping with Repairs:** DC 15 Survival, Religion, or Acrobatics (failure = 1d4 damage, shrine crumbles more). Need 3 successes.

**Witness: Tamara Stonehill** (after DC 14 Persuasion or shrine repair)
- **Regular Worship:** Weekly dawn offering to Tymora with mother Trilena
- **Small Shadows:** Three squat shadows fled after collapse
- **Stolen Centerpiece:** Pretty, shimmery black stone from arch center
- **Voices:** Heard shrill voices in unknown language before collapse

#### Sleeping Giant
**Location:** Former Redbrand tavern

**Crime:** Goblins smashed bar to extract obelisk shard embedded in counter, ransacked kitchen.

**Crime Scene Investigation:**
- Bar in splinters, top split in half
- Lockbox broken open and looted
- **Graffiti Under Counter:** "Zorz ws here" (intended: "Zorzula was here")
- **Kitchen Trail:** DC 15 Perception reveals trail of 5 sp, 7 cp leading to fireplace

**Witness: Wheel-of-Fortune** (nervous tiefling bartender)
- **Missing Stone:** Large black shard used to be in counter for luck
- **Tapping Ritual:** Used to tap mugs against stone
- **DC 12 Insight:** Hiding something - drinks were always "off" (headaches, iridescent mead)
- **Not Present:** Wasn't there during raid, directs to Grista

**Witness: Grista** (dwarf owner, surly)
- **Awakened Rudely:** Sound of wood being shattered
- **Churlish Children:** Initially mistook for misbehaving children
- **Damaged Bar:** Focused on destroying bar, not stealing food
- **Fled When Chased:** Escaped through fireplace/chimney when chased with broom

**Encounter: Rooftop Stash**
- **Enemies:** 1 goblin psi commander (with sword and sack), 2 goblin psi brawlers
- **Tactics:** Stay on two-story roof for high ground
- **Retreat:** Flee after one defeated, drop coin sack
- **Treasure:** 10 gp, 14 sp, 17 cp (return to Grista for free meals for week)

### Scholarly Aid: Gwyn Oresong
**Timing:** After investigating at least one crime scene

**Description:** Cheery dwarf in blue robes with white sigil tattoos, leather satchel of books/scrolls. Lost, needs directions to Stonehill Inn.

**Background:** Neutral good dwarf acolyte from Neverwinter, visiting sister Elsa (Stonehill bartender). Researcher specializing in dwarven history, goblin culture, and ancient empires.

**Assistance:** Asks party to bring clues for investigation help. Freely shares knowledge (though rambles).

### Uncovering the Clues
Need to find clues to locate Zorzula's Rest:

1. **Duergar Site:** From axe at Shrine or goblin interrogation
2. **Fragments of Location:** "The Rest" from Ander or "Zorz" graffiti at Sleeping Giant  
3. **Goblins' Purpose:** Stealing obelisk shards from goblin interrogation or NPC discussions
4. **Leader's Name:** Ruxithid from eavesdropping at goblin camp or interrogation

**If All Four Clues Found:** Gwyn easily traces goblins to Zorzula's Rest
**If Fewer Clues:** DC 15 History check to help Gwyn organize research. Failure requires more clues.

## Part 2: Zorzula's Rest

### Approaching the Outpost
**Travel Time:** One day from Phandalin

**Environmental Change:**
*Gray gravel darkens to ash-black, shrubbery becomes dry and sparse. Instead of the brisk cold of mountain peaks, the air starts to warm. Ahead rises a tall cliff face with forbidding obsidian doors: the entrance to Zorzula's Rest.*

**Entry Points:** 
- Front doors (Z1) - main entrance
- Hot spring vent (Z6) - DC 16 Perception to discover  
- Pantry tunnel (Z9) - from investigation or DC 16 Perception

### Features of Zorzula's Rest

**Sulfuric Lava:** DC 10 Constitution save or poisoned for 1 hour (immune on success for 24 hours). Residents immune.

**Walls/Floors:** Gray igneous stone, mining tunnels through earth/sediment

**Ceilings:** Rooms 15 feet vaulted, tunnels 10 feet flat

**Lighting:** Geometric grooves channel blue lava light (dim illumination), mining tunnels dark

### Key Locations

#### Z1: Entrance
**Features:** Heavy stone double doors, 2 arrow slits 10 feet up (each side)

**Guards:** 2 goblin psi commanders at arrow slits (three-quarters cover)
- **Negotiation:** DC 15 Deception/Persuasion or 25+ gp bribe opens doors
- **Threats:** Guards attack, reinforcements from Z3 arrive in 3 rounds

#### Z2: Vestibule  
**Features:** Spiral staircases to arrow slits, parapet with door-opening lever

**Sounds:** Faint chatter and utensil clatter from Z3

#### Z3: Mess Hall
**Occupants:** 2d4 goblins, 1d6 goblin psi brawlers, 1d4 goblin psi commanders

**Combat:** Goblins panic and attack, aim to subdue rather than kill
**Disguise:** DC 15 Deception as goblins to pass unnoticed
**Negotiation:** DC 13 Intimidation/Persuasion stops fighting, goblins want help with forge monsters (Z7)

#### Z4: Bunks
**Occupants:** 1d6 goblin psi brawlers (sleeping)
**Stealth:** DC 17 to avoid waking
**Awoken Goblins:** Flee and call for help unless DC 16 Intimidation/Persuasion (want forge help)

**Mine Shaft:** 30-foot drop to mining tunnels, broken magical lift
- **Repair:** DC 10 Investigation (find crystal), DC 16 Arcana to fix (advantage with tinker's tools)

#### Z5: Latrines
**Features:** Waste trenches, murky water trough

**Secret Door:** Passive Perception 13+ notices depression in northwest corner (leads to Z6)

#### Z6: Hot Spring
**Features:** 15-foot deep turquoise pool, steam vent 40 feet up

**Vent Exit:** DC 18 Athletics to climb to mountain vent (alternate entrance)

**Features:** Stone bridge 20 feet above lava, obsidian pyramid with relief carvings

**Elevator Platform:** 15-foot diameter, 30-second travel time, 1-minute stops each level

## Part 3: Indigo Sanctum

### Entering the Sanctum
*Massive sanctum of obsidian stone. Two lava waterfalls cascade down either side, casting eerie light. At the opposite end looms a towering statue of a dwarven woman, cracked and half-smashed but retaining a faint kindly smile.*

**Layout:** 20-foot vaulted ceilings, polished obsidian construction

### Sanctum Showdown

#### Ruxithid the Chosen
**Description:** Taller goblin with crystal splitting skull, exposing brain. Eyes and veins glow otherworldly green.

**Personality:** 
- Speaks in calm, sing-song voice
- Initially cordial, believes he serves divine harbingers  
- DC 16 Insight: Eyes roll back - having second mental conversation
- Claims quest will bring Material Plane metamorphosis

**Combat Trigger:** When characters refuse to join or threaten violence

*Green light pulses from Ruxithid's chest to the statue. Like a parasite, it burrows into the shattered face. The statue lurches forward, still smiling, while Ruxithid commands it to kill you.*

**Enemies:** Ruxithid the Chosen, 1 shield guardian (animated statue), optional: 2 goblin psi brawlers (arrive round 3)

**Tactics:** Ruxithid uses psionic flight to hover, directs guardian to tank damage

**Shield Guardian Special:** Can cast phantasmal killer (stored from original duergar creator)

### A Disturbing Truth Revealed
When Ruxithid dies:

*Ruxithid falls, crystalline brain splits asunder. The sanctum fills with cacophony like thousands of voices crying. Three voices emerge in discordant unison:*

*"So you have succeeded. Good. The four shards shall arrive here soon. Now three remain to be found. You know your task, little one. Collect the shards from Talhundereth, the Crypt of the Talhund, and Gibbet Crossing, and bring them to us. You shall be reborn greater than you can imagine, and Phandalin shall become ours."*

**Recognition:** DC 13 Religion - communication method unlike any divine tradition

**Other Areas:**
- **Hostage Room (West Wing):** 3 survivors (Dareth Grint, Paulina Previne, Tumblewick Rollins) - barely conscious, mention stone pillar assembly and other victims taken elsewhere
- **War Room (East Wing):** Map with "Gibbet Crossing," "Crypt of the Talhund," "Talhundereth." Sketch of broken obelisk (4 pieces crossed out, 3 circled)

### The Sawplee Goblins' Future
Without Ruxithid's fear-based leadership, goblins become peaceful and curious (if mischievous). Characters can broker peace with Phandalin townspeople.

## Chapter Conclusion

Characters learn of larger sinister forces threatening Phandalin. Four obelisk shards already delivered to mind flayers - now it's a race to find the remaining three before the Far Realm plot succeeds.

**Harbin's Payment:** Agreed amount plus deep gratitude
**Next Step:** Journey to find remaining obelisk shards in Chapter 6
