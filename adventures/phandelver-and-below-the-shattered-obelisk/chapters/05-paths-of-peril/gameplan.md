## travel to neverwinter

⚔️ 1d12 — Encounters on the Road to Neverwinter
d12 Encounter Description / Hook
1 Bandit Toll A group of 6 bandits and a thug “guard captain” have set up a fake toll gate claiming to collect for “Lord Albrek.” They’ll try diplomacy first; if exposed, they flee into nearby ruins.
2 Corpse Wagon The party meets a wagon piled with bodies, driven by a silent cloaked man (actually a wight) transporting his “merchandise” north. At night, the corpses rise.
3 Collapsed Bridge A washed-out section of the Triboar Trail requires ingenuity — build, jump, or find another way. Bandits or opportunistic gnolls stalk travelers who linger.
4 Goblin Ambush (Redux) Remnants of the Cragmaw tribe still haunt the woods. They know of the heroes who ruined their lair and want revenge. 1 hobgoblin captain + 3 goblins per PC.
5 The Singing Stone A standing stone hums at dusk, attracting fey lights. A dryad offers safe passage if the heroes free her bound tree from blight.
6 Merchant in Trouble A half-elf merchant’s cart is stuck in mud while 2 ogres approach. If saved, she rewards the party with discounted goods or an enchanted trinket from Neverwinter.
7 Ruined Watchtower Exploring yields old banners of Neverwinter’s guard and specters bound to eternal watch. Lay them to rest, and one ghost knight may offer guidance to the city.
8 Fey Pranksters Invisible pixies steal food or boots for fun. If befriended, they share gossip: “Strange lights over the Mere of Dead Men — something stirs there again…”
9 Traveling Priest of Lathander Cheerful cleric offering blessings for the road. He’s genuine — but his donkey carries a cursed relic that radiates faint necrotic energy. Curiosity may reveal it.
10 Night of the Comet A meteor streaks across the sky, crashing nearby. Investigating reveals a small crater with a glowing shard (wild magic surge area for 1d4 days).
11 Runaway Refugees A group of frightened farmers from Leilon flee “something in the marsh.” They speak of undead rising near the coast — a hook into Neverwinter or Saltmarsh adventures.
12 Knight of Neverwinter A patrol led by a stern but honorable knight questions the heroes. If they prove trustworthy, she offers escort for the final leg — or, if tensions rise, a duel of honor.

## random neverwinter

🏙️ 1d12 — Neverwinter Exploration Encounters
d12 Location / Event Description / Hook
1 The Rebuilding District Workers rebuild houses destroyed by the eruption years ago. A half-orc foreman offers coin for help lifting beams — but someone’s stealing tools nightly. The thief? A mischievous dust mephit that collects shiny things.
2 Market of a Thousand Smells The docks bazaar overflows with spices, fried eel, and exotic perfumes. A merchant “accidentally” sells the party a potion of gaseous form, mislabeled as “perfume of courage.”
3 Chasm Shrine Ruins The Chasm is sealed, but whispers still rise from beneath the old wards. A cleric of Oghma asks the heroes to investigate tremors at night — the cause may be an ooze feeding on old magic.
4 Street Duel A flashy young duelist challenges a pompous noble in the streets. The crowd chants for the adventurers to referee or even take sides. The noble’s sword is secretly enchanted — or cursed.
5 The Blacklake Masquerade The party stumbles upon an uninvited evening masquerade near Blacklake District. Nobles gossip of “a masked lord returning.” One guest recognizes a PC from rumors — could be ally or enemy.
6 Library of the Moonstone Mask An eccentric scholar named Avara Thul claims she can prove a demon sleeps beneath Neverwinter Wood. Her “proof” was stolen — by a talking raven who haunts the rooftops.
7 Smugglers’ Tunnel The adventurers find a half-collapsed tunnel entrance under the docks. Local thieves warn them to “leave it be.” The tunnel leads to a forgotten shrine to Umberlee — or worse, to the sea’s edge.
8 The Singing Statue One of the city’s restored statues begins humming at dawn. Crowds gather daily to hear it. Arcana checks reveal the tone matches an ancient elven melody that opens something — somewhere — in Neverwinter Wood.
9 Cloaked Figures in the Alley PCs witness a covert meeting between masked figures exchanging black coins marked with a single eye. They flee if followed, leaving one coin behind — cold to the touch and faintly pulsing.
10 Festival of Renewal Street performers, illusionists, and clerics bless the city’s rebirth. A young mage accidentally opens a minor rift that sucks in part of a stall. The city guard calls for heroes to stabilize it!
11 The Laughing Lute Tavern A bard spins tales of a lost noble’s crypt sealed beneath the city. If paid in ale or coin, he offers a riddle-map to its entrance. The catch: the tavern’s owner wrote it to lure adventurers into helping her clear out real undead.
12 The Lord’s Watch A stern guard captain questions the party, suspecting them of harboring fugitives from Luskan. Whether true or not, she offers them work: “Find my missing informant in the River District.” The truth? The informant became a were-rat.

🧭 Optional Flavor Discoveries (1d8)

Use between main encounters:

You overhear rumors of a new cult preaching “the Watchful Eye.”

A beggar’s coin purse is actually a sending stone.

Strange frost creeps from sewer grates at night.

An abandoned chapel door bears claw marks — from the inside.

A child offers to sell you a “real dragon’s tooth” (it’s genuine).

You find a mural that moves subtly when not watched.

Two mages argue loudly about “Ley drift under the Hall of Justice.”

You catch sight of a raven with a tiny scroll tied to its leg — addressed to you.
