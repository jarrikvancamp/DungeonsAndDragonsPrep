# Gwyn Oresong (Duergar Scholar)

## Role
Scholarly duergar researcher studying psionic phenomena and mind flayer influence

## One-Sentence Hook
A curious duergar scholar whose academic interest in aberrant magic has made her both valuable ally and potential target for mind flayer agents.

## Motivation
Seeks to understand and document the growing psionic influence in the region while protecting her research from those who would misuse it.

## Appearance
**Physical**: Typical duergar build with gray skin and white hair tied in scholarly braids
**Clothing**: Practical robes with many pockets for research tools and specimens
**Equipment**: Carries notebooks, quills, and collection jars for aberrant samples
**Demeanor**: Intensely curious but cautious about sharing sensitive information

## Personality
- **Trait 1**: Asks probing questions about everything, especially unusual magical phenomena
- **Trait 2**: Maintains detailed notes and sketches of all observations
- **Bond**: Believes knowledge should be preserved and protected from misuse
- **Flaw**: Sometimes prioritizes research over immediate safety concerns
