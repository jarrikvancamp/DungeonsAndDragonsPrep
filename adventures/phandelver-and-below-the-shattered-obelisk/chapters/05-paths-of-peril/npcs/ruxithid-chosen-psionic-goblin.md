# Ruxithid the Chosen (Psionic Goblin Leader)

## Role
Psychically enhanced goblin leader serving the emerging mind flayer threat

## One-Sentence Hook
A goblin whose mind has been opened to psionic power, leading her tribe in service to an alien intellect from the Far Realm.

## Motivation
Believes she has been chosen for a great destiny and seeks to prove worthy of her mind flayer patron's attention and approval.

## Appearance
**Physical**: Standard goblin size but with enlarged, pulsing veins around temples
**Eyes**: Occasionally flash with purple energy when using psionic abilities
**Bearing**: Unusually confident and composed for a goblin
**Equipment**: Simple robes with strange geometric patterns, staff topped with brain coral

## Personality
- **Trait 1**: Speaks with eerie calm and uses complex vocabulary beyond typical goblin intelligence
- **Trait 2**: Completely devoted to her "patron" and the promised evolution of her people
- **Bond**: Fanatically loyal to the mind flayer influence that enhanced her abilities
- **Flaw**: Underestimates those without psionic abilities as "lesser minds"

## Location: Phandalin Surroundings

### Psionic Goblin Camp
- **Hidden Location**: 2 miles south of Phandalin, concealed by illusions
- **Geometric Patterns**: Strange symbols carved into trees and stones
- **Brain Coral Growths**: Alien growths that pulse with psychic energy
- **Mental Resonance**: Area affects minds of non-psionics (Wisdom save DC 10 or disadvantage on Wisdom checks)

### Leadership Structure
- **Inner Circle**: 3 other psionic goblins with lesser abilities
- **Followers**: 12 regular goblins becoming increasingly loyal
- **Scouts**: Network of goblin spies infiltrating Phandalin
- **Communication**: Uses telepathy to coordinate operations

## Quest Hooks
1. **Phandalin Investigation**: Strange goblin behavior reported by townspeople
2. **Mind Flayer Threat**: First sign of aberrant influence in region
3. **Psionic Corruption**: Environmental changes around goblin territory

## Key Dialogue
1. **First Contact**: "Your minds buzz with such... limited thoughts. But perhaps they can be... expanded."
2. **About Her Patron**: "The Great Mind has shown me truths beyond your comprehension. We are the future."
3. **Combat Threat**: "You resist the inevitable. Your minds will be opened, willingly or not."

## Faction Connections
- **Mind Flayer Network**: Serves unknown elder brain or rogue illithid
- **Aberrant Cults**: Connected to larger Far Realm influence
- **Local Goblins**: Converting tribal members to psionic service
- **Opposition Forces**: Targeted by Lords' Alliance and adventurers

## Psionic Abilities
**Telepathic Communication**: Can speak directly to minds within 60 feet
**Mind Reading**: Uses detect thoughts to gather intelligence
**Mental Domination**: Employs charm and suggestion for control
**Psychic Combat**: Psionic blast and mind lash for direct confrontation

## Combat Tactics
**Pre-Combat**: Uses detect thoughts to assess party capabilities
**Round 1**: Hold person on primary threat, position behind cover
**Round 2+**: Mind lash to control battlefield, psionic blast for damage
**Desperation**: Commands all followers to attack while attempting escape

## Conversion Process
**Stage 1**: Exposure to brain coral emanations
**Stage 2**: Telepathic contact and mental conditioning
**Stage 3**: Direct psionic enhancement by patron
**Result**: Enhanced intelligence and psionic abilities, absolute loyalty

## Negotiation Potential
- **Insight DC 16**: Genuinely believes she's helping her people evolve
- **Persuasion DC 18**: Might be convinced patron is using her
- **Mind Reading**: She can detect deception through telepathy
- **Conversion Offer**: May try to recruit party members

## Intelligence Network
**Phandalin Infiltration**: Several townspeople under mental influence
**Regional Scouts**: Goblins monitoring all major roads and settlements
**Psychic Communication**: Instant coordination across wide distances
**Information Gathering**: Uses detect thoughts on travelers and merchants

## Environmental Effects
**Brain Coral Influence**: 
- Wisdom save DC 10 every hour in affected area
- Failure: Disadvantage on Wisdom checks and saves
- Extended exposure may cause personality changes

**Psionic Resonance**:
- Magic users may experience feedback
- Telepathic creatures drawn to area
- Animals become nervous and flee

## Development Options
**Defeat**: Reveals extent of mind flayer influence in region
**Conversion**: Becomes ally if convinced patron is malevolent
**Escape**: Returns with greater numbers and enhanced abilities
**Intelligence**: Provides crucial information about aberrant threat

## Master Plan
**Phase 1**: Convert local goblin tribes to psionic servants
**Phase 2**: Infiltrate and subvert human settlements
**Phase 3**: Establish permanent mind flayer presence in region
**Ultimate Goal**: Create thrall population for elder brain consumption
