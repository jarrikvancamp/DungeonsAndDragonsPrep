## Quick Reference

**Role:** Mind Flayer Warlock serving Ilvaash
**Motivation:** Gather obelisk fragments for the grand ritual
**Key Traits:** Arrogant, calculating, fanatically devoted to Ilvaash
**Challenge:** CR 8 boss encounter with minions

## Stats

## Spells

**Cantrips (at will):** *eldritch blast*, *mage hand*, *minor illusion*, *prestidigitation*

**1st-5th Level (2 5th-level slots):** *armor of Agathys*, *charm person*, *counterspell*, *dimension door*, *dominate person*, *fireball*, *hold person*, *invisibility*, *misty step*, *suggestion*

## Special Abilities

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Ilvaash's Blessing:** Once per day, can cast *dominate monster* (save DC 15) without expending a spell slot.

**Eldritch Invocations:**
- **Agonizing Blast:** +3 damage to eldritch blast
- **Repelling Blast:** Push targets 10 feet on hit
- **Devil's Sight:** See normally in darkness

## Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes two attacks: one Tentacle and one Eldritch Blast, or casts a spell.

**Tentacles:** +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 15 (2d10+4) psychic damage. If target is Medium or smaller, it must make DC 15 Constitution save or be stunned for 1 minute (repeat save at end of each turn).

**Extract Brain:** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft, one incapacitated humanoid grappled by Qunbraxel. Hit: The target takes 55 (10d10) piercing damage. If this damage reduces target to 0 hit points, Qunbraxel kills target by extracting brain.

**Eldritch Blast:** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +7 to hit, range 120 ft. Hit: 13 (1d10+8) force damage, push target 10 feet away.

**Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6):** 60-foot cone. All creatures must make DC 15 Intelligence save. Failed save: 17 (4d8-1) psychic damage and stunned for 1 minute. Success: Half damage, not stunned.

## Legendary Actions (3 per turn)

- **Move:** Move up to speed without provoking opportunity attacks
- **Eldritch Blast:** Make one eldritch blast attack
- **Cast Spell (2 actions):** Cast a spell of 2nd level or lower

## Personality

**Traits:**
- Speaks in condescending telepathic whispers
- Constantly analyzes and judges the "inferior minds" around him
- Shows genuine excitement when discussing Ilvaash's grand design

**Ideal:** The transformation of all inferior species into mind flayers is inevitable and glorious.

**Bond:** Absolutely devoted to Ilvaash and the three fanatic leaders.

**Flaw:** Underestimates "lesser beings" and their potential for cunning.

## Tactics

**Round 1:** Cast *armor of Agathys* (5th level) for 25 temp HP and cold damage
**Round 2-3:** Use Mind Blast, then focus eldritch blast on spellcasters
**Round 4+:** Dominate strongest fighter, use counterspell defensively
**Bloodied:** Cast *dimension door* to reposition, use *dominate person* tactically
**Desperate:** Attempt to extract brain from stunned/grappled targets

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 7):**
- Reduce HP to 95 (range 80-110)
- Remove legendary actions
- Lower spell save DC to 14

**Harder (Party Level 10):**
- Increase HP to 155 (range 140-170)
- Add lair actions (see below)
- Increase spell save DC to 16

## Lair Actions (If used)

On initiative count 20 (losing ties), Qunbraxel can take one lair action:

1. **Psychic Pulse:** All creatures within 30 feet make DC 15 Wisdom save or take 7 (2d6) psychic damage
2. **Mind Control Grimlocks:** Command all grimlocks to move and attack immediately
3. **Illusory Duplicates:** Create 2 illusory duplicates that last until start of next turn

## Dialogue

**Opening:** *"Inferior creatures dare enter my sanctum? Your minds will serve Ilvaash's grand design, whether willing or extracted."*

**Combat Taunts:**
- *"Your thoughts are so... limited. Let me expand them."*
- *"The obelisk fragments call to their true masters."*
- *"Ilvaash sees through my eyes. You cannot hide."*

**Defeated:** *"You... delay... the inevitable. The three... await in the Far... Realm..."*

## Loot

- **Qunbraxel's Staff:** +1 quarterstaff, can cast *detect thoughts* (3 charges/day)
- **Warlock Spellbook:** Contains details about Ilvaash and the ritual
- **Map to Illithinoch:** Shows the route to the mind flayer stronghold
- **Aberrant Pearl:** Worth 300 gp, glows near aberrations
- **1,200 gp** in various Underdark currencies

## Key Information

When defeated or interrogated, Qunbraxel can reveal:
- The three fanatic leaders have fled to Illithinoch
- The ritual requires all obelisk fragments to be assembled
- Phandalin's population is the primary target
- Ilvaash grants power beyond normal mind flayer capabilities

## DM Notes

- First major mind flayer encounter - establish their threat level
- His arrogance can lead to tactical mistakes if players are clever
- Use his telepathy to unnerve players with personal observations
- The grimlocks serve as meat shields while he casts spells
- His knowledge connects this chapter to the next adventure phase
- Consider having him attempt to flee if reduced below 30 HP

## Minions

**4 Grimlocks** typically accompany Qunbraxel:
- Serve as bodyguards and distractions
- Follow his telepathic commands instantly
- Will fight to the death to protect him
- Use pack tactics to grapple dangerous foes
