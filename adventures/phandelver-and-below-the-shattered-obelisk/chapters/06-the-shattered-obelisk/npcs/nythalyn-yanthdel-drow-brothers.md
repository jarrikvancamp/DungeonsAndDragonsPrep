## Shared Abilities

**Fey Ancestry:** Advantage against being charmed, magic can't put them to sleep.

**Sunlight Sensitivity:** Disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks in direct sunlight.

**Innate Spellcasting:** Charisma-based (spell save DC 12):
- **At will:** *dancing lights*
- **1/day each:** *darkness*, *faerie fire*, *levitate* (self only)

## Attacks (Both Brothers)

**Multiattack:** Makes two Shortsword attacks or two Hand Crossbow attacks.

**Shortsword:** +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) poison damage.

**Hand Crossbow:** +7 to hit, range 30/120 ft. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) poison damage.

## Personality Differences

### Nythalyn (Older Brother)
**Traits:**
- More cautious and diplomatic
- Takes responsibility for both their safety
- Speaks for the pair in negotiations

**Ideal:** Family loyalty above all else

**Bond:** Will sacrifice anything to save his father and brother

**Flaw:** Sometimes too trusting of those who show respect

### Yanthdel (Younger Brother)
**Traits:**
- More aggressive and impulsive
- Quick to draw weapons when threatened
- Protective of his older brother's reputation

**Ideal:** Strength and honor in combat

**Bond:** Idolizes Nythalyn and follows his lead

**Flaw:** Hot-tempered, especially regarding their father

## Tactical Coordination

**Combat Synergy:**
- Nythalyn uses *faerie fire* to mark targets
- Yanthdel flanks for advantage while Nythalyn provides cover
- Both use *darkness* to escape or reposition
- Protect each other with coordinated movement

**Non-Combat:**
- Nythalyn handles negotiations
- Yanthdel watches for threats and ambushes
- Share watch duties and survival tasks

## Story Integration

**Their Quest:** 
- Father Ontharyx went missing weeks ago while exploring ancient ruins
- Tracked him to this area but lost the trail
- Desperate to find him before something terrible happens

**Potential Ally Path:**
- If characters mention finding mutated drow or show compassion
- Willing to share information about Underdark routes
- Can provide warnings about mind flayer activity
- May join party temporarily if convinced their father might be saved

**Potential Enemy Path:**
- If characters are hostile or dismissive
- If they discover the party killed their father (even as a mutate)
- Will attack with coordinated tactics and no mercy

## Dialogue

**Nythalyn Opening:** "Surface dwellers in the deep places... speak quickly. Have you seen a drow warrior named Ontharyx? Our father is missing."

**Yanthdel Warning:** "Brother, they could be the ones who... we should not trust so easily."

**If Father's Fate Revealed:**
- **Nythalyn (Grief):** "No... father would never... what have these creatures done to him?"
- **Yanthdel (Rage):** "They will pay for this! Every last one of them!"

## Information They Can Provide

**Underdark Knowledge:**
- Safe routes through dangerous areas
- Locations of other drow outposts
- Warnings about aberrant creatures they've encountered
- Knowledge of ancient dwarven ruins

**Recent Events:**
- Strange psychic disturbances in the area
- Other drow parties that have gone missing
- Unusual creature sightings near obelisk sites

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 6):**
- Reduce HP to 55 (range 45-65) each
- Remove poison damage from weapons
- Only one brother present initially

**Harder (Party Level 9):**
- Increase HP to 85 (range 75-95) each
- Add *misty step* to spell list
- Both brothers have +1 weapons

## Loot (If Hostile)

- **Drow Elite Equipment:** Studded leather, shields, shortswords, hand crossbows
- **Family Heirloom:** Silver amulet with Henlifel family crest (150 gp)
- **Survival Gear:** Underdark navigation tools and rations
- **Poison Vials:** 3 vials of drow poison (each worth 200 gp)

## DM Notes

- Use them to humanize the drow and show family bonds
- Their reaction to their father's fate can drive future plot threads
- Excellent opportunity for moral complexity - do you tell them the truth?
- Can serve as recurring allies or enemies based on party choices
- Their knowledge of Underdark can be valuable for navigation
- Consider having them appear again in later chapters if they survive

## Resolution Paths

**Best Case:** Become trusted allies who help navigate the Underdark and provide ongoing support

**Neutral:** Part ways peacefully with mutual respect and information exchange

**Worst Case:** Become recurring enemies who may ally with other dark forces against the party
