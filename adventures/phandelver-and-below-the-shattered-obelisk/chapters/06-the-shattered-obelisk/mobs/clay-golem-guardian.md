## Special Abilities

**Acid Absorption:** Whenever the golem is subjected to acid damage, it takes no damage and instead regains hit points equal to the acid damage dealt.

**Berserk:** When the golem starts its turn with 60 hit points or fewer, roll d6. On 6, the golem goes berserk. While berserk, attacks all creatures within reach, gaining advantage on attack rolls and +3 damage to all attacks.

**Immutable Form:** Immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

**Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.

**Magic Weapons:** Attacks are magical.

**Temple Guardian:** While within the temple, the golem has advantage on all saving throws and its reach increases to 10 feet.

## Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes two Slam attacks.

**Slam:** +8 to hit, reach 5 ft (10 ft in temple). Hit: 16 (2d10+5) bludgeoning damage. If target is a creature, it must make DC 15 Constitution save or have its hit point maximum reduced by 10 (3d6). Target dies if this reduces hit point maximum to 0. Reduction lasts until target finishes long rest.

**Haste (Recharge 5-6):** For 3 rounds, the golem's speed doubles, it gains +2 AC, has advantage on Dexterity saves, and gains one additional action each turn (attack or dash only).

## Legendary Actions (3 per turn)

- **Move:** Move up to speed without provoking opportunity attacks
- **Slam Attack:** Make one Slam attack
- **Guardian's Wrath (2 actions):** All creatures within 10 feet make DC 15 Strength save or be knocked prone

## Tactics

- **Defensive Position:** Guards the temple entrance, using reach advantage
- **Targeting Priority:** Focus on biggest threats first
- **Berserk State:** When bloodied, becomes unpredictable and more dangerous
- **Environmental Use:** Smash pillars to create difficult terrain
- **Endurance:** Relies on immunities and high HP to outlast opponents

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 7):**
- Reduce HP to 100 (range 85-115)
- Remove legendary actions
- Berserk triggers at 40 HP instead of 60

**Harder (Party Level 10):**
- Increase HP to 165 (range 150-180)
- Add reaction: When hit by melee attack, can make slam against attacker
- Berserk triggers at 80 HP

## DM Notes

- Ancient guardian created by dwarven priests
- Has been maintaining its vigil for centuries
- Not inherently evil - follows its programming to protect the temple
- Can be reasoned with if characters speak Dwarvish and show proper respect
- Its immunities make this a tactical challenge requiring creative solutions
- The temple environment provides opportunities for dynamic combat

## Environmental Features

**Dwarven Temple Layout:**
- **Sacred Pillars:** Can be toppled (AC 17, 50 HP) to create cover or difficult terrain
- **Holy Braziers:** Still burning with sacred flame, can be used for fire damage
- **Trap Doors:** Lead to lower temple levels if activated

## Alternative Resolution

**Diplomatic Solution:** DC 18 Religion check + speaking Dwarvish + showing respect for dwarven traditions can convince the golem to stand down temporarily.

**Sacred Ritual:** Characters with appropriate religious background can attempt to complete the temple's protective ritual, causing the golem to recognize them as worthy.
