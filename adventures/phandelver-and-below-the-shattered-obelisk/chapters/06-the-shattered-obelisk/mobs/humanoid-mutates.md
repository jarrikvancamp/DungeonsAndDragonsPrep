```yaml
  Saving Throws: Con +4
  Skills: Perception +2

  Senses: Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 12
  Languages: Common (broken speech)

```

## Special Abilities

**Aberrant Transformation:** The mutate's body shows signs of mind flayer influence - elongated limbs, patches of slimy skin, and small tentacles around the mouth.

**Pack Tactics:** Advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one ally is within 5 feet of the target and not incapacitated.

**Mental Feedback:** When the mutate takes psychic damage, all creatures within 10 feet must make DC 12 Wisdom save or take 3 (1d6) psychic damage.

**Unstable Mutation:** When reduced to 10 hit points or fewer, rolls d6 at start of each turn:
- 1-2: Gains 10 temporary hit points
- 3-4: Speed increases to 50 feet until end of turn
- 5-6: Next attack deals extra 1d8 psychic damage

## Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes two Mutated Claw attacks.

**Mutated Claw:** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) psychic damage.

**Psychic Shriek (Recharge 5-6):** All creatures within 15 feet must make DC 12 Wisdom save. Failed save: 10 (3d6) psychic damage and frightened until end of next turn. Success: Half damage, not frightened.

## Named Mutates

### Malinia (Enhanced Mutate)
- **HP:** 58 (enhanced version)
- **Special:** Can cast *detect thoughts* once per day
- **Tactics:** Uses her retained intelligence to coordinate attacks

### Ontharyx (Drow Father)
- **Background:** Father of Nythalyn and Yanthdel, captured and mutated
- **HP:** 52 (retains some drow agility)
- **Special:** Retains *darkness* spell (once per day)
- **Roleplay:** Occasionally recognizes his sons' names

## Tactics

- **Group Coordination:** Work together using pack tactics
- **Psychic Harassment:** Use Psychic Shriek to disrupt spellcasters
- **Unstable Desperation:** Become more dangerous when wounded
- **Environmental Use:** Push enemies toward hazards or other threats

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 6):**
- Reduce HP to 35 (range 28-42)
- Lower save DCs to 11
- Remove Mental Feedback ability

**Harder (Party Level 9):**
- Increase HP to 58 (range 50-66)
- Increase save DCs to 13
- Add telepathic coordination (advantage on initiative)

## Loot

- Tattered clothing and equipment (worthless)
- Aberrant Tissue Samples (25 gp each to the right buyer)
- Personal effects (wedding rings, family portraits) for emotional impact

## DM Notes

- These were once normal humanoids transformed by mind flayer experimentation
- Use them to show the horror of the mind flayer threat
- Ontharyx's presence adds emotional weight if his sons are encountered
- Their broken speech can provide cryptic warnings or pleas for death
- The mutations are disturbing but not grotesque enough to derail the game
- They serve as a preview of what could happen to Phandalin's population

## Roleplay Elements

**Broken Memories:** Occasionally mutter fragments of their former lives
- "My children... where are my children?"
- "The light... it burns the thoughts..."
- "Please... make it stop..."

**Recognition Moments:** May briefly recognize familiar faces or objects from their past, creating tragic story beats.
