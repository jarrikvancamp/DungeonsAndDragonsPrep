## Stats

```yaml
Encephalon Gemmule:
  AC: 11
  HP: 13 (range 10-16)
  Speed: 30 ft
  STR: 6, DEX: 13, CON: 12, INT: 10, WIS: 11, CHA: 8
  Skills: Stealth +3
  Damage Resistances: Psychic
  Senses: Blindsight 60 ft, Passive Perception 10
  Languages: Deep Speech (telepathy 30 ft)
  CR: 1/4 (50 XP)
```

## Special Abilities

**Psychic Link:** Can communicate telepathically with other encephalon creatures within 1 mile.

**Probe Mind:** As an action, target one humanoid within 30 feet. Target must make DC 11 Wisdom save or the gemmule learns one piece of surface information (current intent, recent memory, etc.).

**Death Burst:** When reduced to 0 hit points, releases a psychic scream. All creatures within 10 feet must make DC 11 Wisdom save or take 3 (1d6) psychic damage.

## Attacks

**Psychic Lash:** +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 4 (1d6+1) psychic damage.

## Tactics

- **Scouts:** Use stealth to observe before engaging
- **Swarm:** Attack in groups of 3-4 to overwhelm single targets
- **Telepathic Coordination:** Share information instantly with larger encephalon creatures
- **Retreat:** Fall back to alert encephalon clusters when badly wounded

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 6):**
- Reduce HP to 8 (range 6-10)
- Lower save DCs to 10

**Harder (Party Level 8):**
- Increase HP to 18 (range 15-21)
- Add +1 to all save DCs
- Death Burst affects 15-foot radius

## Loot

- No treasure (aberrations)
- Psychic residue can be harvested by enchanters (10 gp value)

## DM Notes

- These are the "children" of encephalon clusters
- Use them as early warning system for larger threats
- Their telepathic link makes surprise difficult
- They're cowardly but fanatically loyal to mind flayer masters
- Often found in groups of 3-6 near obelisk fragments
