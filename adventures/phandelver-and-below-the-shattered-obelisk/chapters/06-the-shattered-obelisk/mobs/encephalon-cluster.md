## Special Abilities

**Gemmule Bond:** The cluster can sense and communicate with all encephalon gemmules within 1 mile. When the cluster dies, all gemmules within this range take 7 (2d6) psychic damage.

**Psychic Overload (Recharge 5-6):** All creatures within 30 feet must make DC 14 Wisdom save. Failed save: 14 (4d6) psychic damage and stunned until end of next turn. Success: Half damage, not stunned.

**Obelisk Guardian:** If protecting an obelisk fragment, the cluster gains resistance to all damage types except psychic and force while within 30 feet of the fragment.

## Attacks

**Multiattack:** Makes two Tentacle Lash attacks.

**Tentacle Lash:** +5 to hit, reach 10 ft. Hit: 8 (1d10+3) bludgeoning damage plus 4 (1d8) psychic damage. Target must make DC 14 Constitution save or be grappled (escape DC 14).

**Mind Drain (Grappled Target):** Target must make DC 14 Intelligence save. Failed save: 10 (3d6) psychic damage and target's Intelligence is reduced by 1d4. If Intelligence reaches 0, target becomes catatonic. Success: Half damage, no ability reduction.

## Legendary Actions (3 per turn)

- **Move:** Move up to speed without provoking opportunity attacks
- **Tentacle:** Make one Tentacle Lash attack
- **Psychic Pulse (2 actions):** All creatures within 15 feet make DC 14 Wisdom save or take 7 (2d6) psychic damage

## Tactics

- **Initial Phase:** Use gemmules as scouts and early warning
- **Engagement:** Focus Psychic Overload on spellcasters
- **Grapple Priority:** Target high-Intelligence characters for Mind Drain
- **Defensive:** Stay near obelisk fragment for damage resistance
- **Desperate:** Use legendary actions to maintain pressure when bloodied

## Scaling Options

**Easier (Party Level 6):**
- Reduce HP to 65 (range 55-75)
- Lower save DCs to 13
- Remove legendary actions

**Harder (Party Level 9):**
- Increase HP to 105 (range 90-120)
- Increase save DCs to 15
- Add reaction: When damaged, can use psychic pulse

## Loot

- Obelisk Fragment (plot item)
- Psychic Crystal (150 gp, can be used as spellcasting focus)
- Aberrant Brain Matter (rare alchemy component worth 200 gp)

## DM Notes

- Primary guardian of obelisk fragments
- Serves mind flayer masters with fanatical devotion
- Its death causes psychological trauma to connected gemmules
- The obelisk fragment amplifies its psychic abilities
- Represents the first major aberrant threat players face
- Use environmental hazards (unstable terrain) to add complexity

## Environmental Hazards

**Psychic Resonance:** The obelisk fragment creates a 20-foot radius of psychic energy. Creatures starting their turn in this area must make DC 12 Wisdom save or have disadvantage on concentration saves until start of next turn.
