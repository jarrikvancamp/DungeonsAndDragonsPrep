## Chapter Overview

The characters have defeated Ruxithid, the Sawplee goblin leader who collected fragments of a Netherese obelisk. However, an unsettling truth has emerged: Ruxithid acted at the behest of much more powerful overlords who want the obelisk pieces to impose some terrible fate upon the town. The characters must collect the remaining obelisk shards before the mind flayer fanatics do.

## The Sinister Truth

The Sawplee goblins indirectly served three mind flayers that draw power from a malevolent Far Realm godlet named Ilvaash. Using their godlet as a warped sort of elder brain, these mind flayers are even more alien than others of their kind.

### The Ritual

The mind flayers seek to enact a dreadful ritual that will transform the entire population of Phandalin into mind flayers. The ritual would work from a significant distance—even from the Far Realm itself—and would affect individuals on a large scale. The fanatics learned from Ilvaash that transforming beings into mind flayers from afar requires a powerful source of magic: the Netherese obelisk.

## Return to Phandalin

Before the characters travel to any dungeons, they should return to Phandalin from Zorzula's Rest. Gwyn Oresong invites the characters to join her at the Stonehill Inn and provides crucial information about the locations marked on the Sawplee goblins' map.

### Gwyn's Information

**Talhundereth:** A great temple to Dumathoin (dwarven god of secrets, mining, and gemstones). Many important priests were laid to rest in the Crypt of the Talhund beneath it. Only Dumathoin's priests would know the secret to unlock the crypt's entrance.

**Gibbet Crossing:** Near the temple, but much deeper underground. A trading hub where deep gnomes, drow, and duergar once worked together. All were consumed or destroyed by mind flayers centuries ago.

## Crypt of the Talhund

**Type:** Sacred burial complex beneath Talhundereth
**CR Range:** 7-9
**Key Locations:** 18 areas (P1-P18)

### Crypt Features

- **Dwarven Made:** Solid construction with gemstone carvings that glitter
- **Unnamed:** No names on tombs (Dumathoin gives secret names in death)
- **Haunted:** Psychic echoes from mind flayer invasion

### Crypt Hauntings

Roll d6 when specified:
1. Creeping tentacle sensations on scalp
2. Ghostly dwarf priest with battleaxe stained with purple ichor
3. Suspicion that mind flayers are invading somewhere important
4. Muttering voices: "The brain eaters have come! Save us!"
5. Ghostly image of character with cracked skull
6. Zombie revealing gem in chest cavity

### Puzzle: Crypt Entrance (T20)

**Challenge:** Rotate dial to show gemstone cutting progression (rough to finished)
**Solution:** Start with least finished carving, proceed in order to most finished
**Key Required:** Any faceted gemstone (available in vault T8)
**Alternative:** Deal 20 psychic damage to overload the lock

### Major Encounters

**P1: Lower Landing**
- **Enemies:** 4 crypt guardians (revenant stats)
- **Trigger:** Disturbing sarcophagi or attempting to leave west

**P5: Watery Chamber**
- **Enemies:** Grandlejaw (hydra) and Kellikilli (water weird)
- **Roleplay:** Creatures interact before noticing intruders

**P8: Honored Dead**
- **Enemy:** Clay golem maintaining vigil
- **Treasure:** 14 gems worth 100 gp each (in corpses' ribcages)

**P13: General's Tomb**
- **Enemies:** Brelta Gemblade and 2 aides (ghosts)
- **Treasure:** Mindguard crown, 3 gems worth 100 gp each

**P14: Rotating Sanctum**
- **Enemies:** 3 wraiths
- **Mechanics:** Room rotates when levers in P9 and P13 pulled simultaneously
- **Treasure:** 10-foot-diameter gemstone (not actually an emerald)

**P18: Obelisk Chamber - SECOND FRAGMENT LOCATION**
- **Enemies:** 7 specters (emerge from wall carvings)
- **Importance:** Second of three fragments needed

### Purifying the Crypt

**Requirements:** Quell all hauntings and defeat the ghosts
**Reward:** Blessing of Dumathoin (darkvision, daily truesight for 1 minute)

## Gibbet Crossing

**Type:** Abandoned Underdark trading nexus
**CR Range:** 7-9
**Key Locations:** 25 areas (G1-G25)

### Historical Background

Once a crossroads where drow, duergar, and svirfneblin communities lived in uneasy peace. When mind flayers attacked centuries ago, the groups didn't fight together and were individually annihilated.

### Major Encounters

**G3: Cage Workshop**
- **Enemies:** 4 feral ashenwights
- **Treasure:** Mithral-reinforced folding cage worth 200 gp

**G6: Prison**
- **Enemy:** Vakketar (chain devil)
- **Restriction:** Can only leave area for 1 minute at a time
- **Treasure:** 2 Large-sized folding cages worth 400 gp each

**G14: Toppled Tower**
- **NPCs:** Zoklork (xorn) and 2 earth elementals
- **Key Item:** Mudslick tower blocking passage to vault
- **Solution:** Command word "petrification" in Terran or petrified gravel

**G20: Azfadiel Shrine**
- **Enemy:** Zuluthl (yochlol demon)
- **Information:** Command word for mudslick tower found in notes
- **Treasure:** 80 gp in animated coins, spell scroll of freedom of movement

**G24: Guard Room - QUNBRAXEL'S LAIR**
- **Enemy:** Qunbraxel (mind flayer warlock) and 4 grimlocks
- **Key Information:** Map showing obelisk reassembly and Illithinoch location
- **Tactics:** Uses grimlocks as front-line fighters while casting spells

**G25: Drow Vault - THIRD FRAGMENT LOCATION**
- **Treasure:** 5,900 gp, 14,050 sp, wand of magic detection, Heward's handy haversack
- **Importance:** Third and final fragment location

### Key NPCs

**Qunbraxel the Mind Flayer Warlock**
- Arrogant mind flayer seeking to join the Ilvaash fanatics
- Commands grimlock minions in Gibbet Crossing
- Possesses detailed map of the fanatics' plans
- **Goal:** Collect obelisk fragments to gain favor with the fanatics

## Combat Tactics by Location

### Talhundereth Tactics
- **Medusa (T3):** Uses petrification on "kneeling" characters
- **Cult Members:** Coordinate attacks, feed captives to encephalon cluster
- **Encephalon Cluster:** Uses gemmules as scouts and early warning

### Crypt Tactics
- **Undead:** Can be temporarily stopped by invoking Dumathoin (DC 14 Religion)
- **Hydra:** Prefers aquatic environment, uses water weird ally
- **Ghosts/Wraiths:** Fight until destroyed or crypt is purified

### Gibbet Crossing Tactics
- **Qunbraxel:** Uses Mind Blast and domination abilities
- **Grimlocks:** Form protective wall around mind flayer
- **Environmental:** Use narrow passages and verticality to advantage

## Scaling Options

### Easier (Level 6-7 Party)
- Reduce HP of major enemies by 25%
- Remove 1-2 minions from group encounters
- Provide additional healing potions

### Harder (Level 8-9 Party)
- Add legendary actions to major enemies
- Include additional patrol encounters
- Increase save DCs by 1-2

## What's Next?

After completing this chapter, the characters should have:
- All three obelisk fragments (if successful)
- Knowledge of Illithinoch and the mind flayer threat
- Understanding that missing townspeople are elsewhere
- Clear direction to pursue the fanatics into the Underdark

The characters' next step is to return to Phandalin to rest and learn what they can about the mind flayer fanatics before venturing deeper underground to Illithinoch.
