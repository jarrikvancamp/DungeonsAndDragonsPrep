# Sildar Hallwinter

## Role
Lords' Alliance agent and Gundren's escort

## Hook
Wounded warrior with crucial information about missing wizards and the Spider's network.

## Motivation
Investigate the disappearance of Iarno Albrek and restore order to the region.

## Appearance
- Weathered face with graying beard, piercing blue eyes that miss nothing
- Armor dented and bloodied from recent capture, still carries himself with military bearing  
- Speaks with cultured accent despite current disheveled state

## Personality
- **Traits**: Methodical investigator who notices details; maintains dignity even when captured
- **Bond**: Sworn to protect innocent folk and uphold the Lords' Alliance
- **Flaw**: Sometimes too trusting of authority figures

## Quest Hooks
- Needs escort to Phandalin to recover from wounds
- Seeks information about missing wizard Iarno Albrek
- Can provide introductions to Lords' Alliance contacts

## Key Information
- Iarno Albrek went missing while investigating bandit activity
- The Spider has extensive network throughout the region
- Strange psionic goblins are new development that concerns him
- Can authorize rewards for clearing bandit threats

## Lines to Speak
1. "The Spider's reach extends further than we thought. These goblins spoke of powers beyond simple banditry."
