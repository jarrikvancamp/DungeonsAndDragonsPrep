# Yeemik

## Role
Ambitious goblin boss in Cragmaw hideout

## Hook
Scheming goblin who wants to overthrow Klarg and offers desperate bargains.

## Motivation
Seize control of the Cragmaw hideout and prove himself worthy of higher rank.

## Appearance
- Larger than typical goblins, with scars across his arms from previous fights
- Wears mismatched armor pieces looted from various victims
- Eyes dart constantly, always calculating angles and opportunities

## Personality
- **Traits**: Opportunistic schemer; speaks in broken Common mixed with Goblin curses
- **Bond**: Believes he deserves to lead rather than follow Klarg
- **Flaw**: Cowardly when faced with real danger, quick to betray allies

## Quest Hooks
- Offers to help kill Klarg in exchange for party sparing him
- Might provide information about Cragmaw operations
- Could become recurring antagonist if he escapes

## Key Information
- Knows layout of Cragmaw hideout intimately  
- Aware of King Grol's location at Cragmaw Castle
- Has heard rumors about strange new goblins with psionic powers
- Understands Cragmaw hierarchy and politics

## Lines to Speak
1. "You kill Klarg, yes? Yeemik help you! Yeemik good goblin, not like others!"
2. "Stupid bugbear think he so strong! You show him real power!"
3. "Human still breathing? Yeemik keep him alive for trade! Very valuable, yes!"

## Reward Suggestions
- 3 agates worth 10 gp each if defeated
- Potion of healing from his stash
- Information about Cragmaw operations (if interrogated)
- Potential ally for infiltrating other goblin strongholds
