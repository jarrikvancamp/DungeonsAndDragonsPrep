# Gundren Rockseeker

## Role
Dwarf patron and employer of the party

## Hook
Secretive dwarf merchant who hired the party to transport supplies, now mysteriously captured.

## Motivation
Reopen Wave Echo Cave with his brothers and restore the Forge of Spells to create magical items.

## Appearance
- Stocky dwarf with iron-gray hair braided with small mining picks
- Calloused hands speak of years working stone, eyes gleam with entrepreneurial excitement
- Wears practical traveling clothes with hidden pockets for valuables

## Personality
- **Traits**: Enthusiastic about his "big discovery" but secretive about details; generous with those who help him
- **Bond**: Fierce loyalty to his brothers Nundro and Tharden
- **Flaw**: Tendency to take dangerous risks when treasure is involved

## Quest Hooks
- Hired party to deliver supplies to Barthen's Provisions in Phandalin
- Promised 10 gp each for safe delivery
- His rescue becomes urgent priority

## Key Information
- Found the entrance to legendary Wave Echo Cave with his brothers
- The map to Wave Echo Cave was stolen during his capture
- Has connections throughout the region's mining community
- Knows the true value of the Forge of Spells

## Lines to Speak
1. "My friends, we've found it! Wave Echo Cave! The legends were true!"
2. "The supplies must reach Barthen's safely - Phandalin depends on it."
3. "The Forge of Spells will change everything. We'll craft wonders not seen for centuries!"

## Reward Suggestions
- Payment of 10 gp each for initial delivery job
- Substantial reward upon safe rescue
- Future partnership in Wave Echo Cave mining operations
- Access to magical items from restored Forge of Spells
