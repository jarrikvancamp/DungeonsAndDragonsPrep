# Wolves

## Role
Guard animals and goblin companions

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Pack hunters with bite and knockdown tactics
**Preferred Range**: Melee charge with pack coordination
**Key Abilities**: Pack Tactics, keen hearing/smell, trip attacks

## Stats
```yaml
Wolf:
  AC: 13 (Natural Armor)
  HP: 11 (range 8-15)
  Speed: 40 ft
  STR: 12 DEX: 15 CON: 12 INT: 3 WIS: 12 CHA: 6
  
  Skills: Perception +3, Stealth +4
  Languages: —
  Challenge: 1/4 (50 XP)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Keen Hearing and Smell: Advantage on Perception checks using hearing/smell
    Pack Tactics: Advantage on attacks if ally within 5 ft of target
  
  Actions:
    Bite: +4 to hit, 2d4+2 piercing damage
    If damage is 7+, target must make DC 11 Str save or be knocked prone
```

## Encounter Variations

### Chained Wolves (H3 - Kennel)
- **Numbers**: 3 wolves chained to stalagmites
- **Chains**: 10-foot reach, can't reach steps area
- **Release**: Goblins can release as action, or successive DC 15/10 Strength checks to break free
- **Tactics**: Snarl and threaten, attack anyone within reach
- **Calming**: DC 15 Animal Handling (DC 10 with food) to calm

### Pool Wolves (H7)
- **Numbers**: 2 wolves with 3 goblins
- **Tactics**: Coordinate with goblins, use pack tactics
- **Terrain**: Stone dams and pools limit movement
- **Development**: Fight alongside goblins to defend flood controls

### Ripper (H8 - Klarg's Wolf)
- **Numbers**: 1 wolf (Klarg's companion)
- **Special**: Slightly tougher (15 hp), very loyal to Klarg
- **Tactics**: Fights to death defending Klarg
- **Personality**: Scarred veteran, missing piece of left ear

## Group Tactics
1. **Pack Formation**: Surround single target for pack tactics advantage
2. **Knockdown Focus**: Trip strongest-looking party member
3. **Mobility**: Use 40-foot speed to reposition and flank
4. **Persistence**: Fight until death or master commands retreat

## Environmental Usage
- **Cave Terrain**: Use superior darkvision and stealth
- **Water Areas**: Comfortable crossing streams and pools
- **Tight Spaces**: Better maneuverability than larger creatures
- **Scent Tracking**: Can follow party's trail easily

## Animal Handling
- **DC 10**: Calm with food offering
- **DC 15**: Calm without food
- **DC 20**: Turn against goblin masters
- **Impossible**: If wolves are already in combat

## Development Options
- **Escaped Wolves**: Could track party if they flee
- **Tamed Wolves**: Might join party if goblins defeated and handled well
- **Wounded Wolves**: May retreat if reduced to 1/4 hp

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce wolves to 8 hp each, remove pack tactics
**Harder**: Add 1 additional wolf per encounter, increase hp to 15
**Environmental**: Wolves know secret passages in cave system
**Roleplay**: One wolf might be convinced to switch sides with good Animal Handling
