# Klarg

## Role
Bugbear leader of the Cragmaw hideout

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Aggressive bruiser who uses fire pit and minions tactically
**Preferred Range**: Melee with morningstar
**Key Abilities**: High damage, moderate durability, commands respect from goblins

## Stats
```yaml
Klarg (Bugbear):
  AC: 16 (Hide Armor, Shield)
  HP: 27 (range 20-35)
  Speed: 30 ft
  STR: 15 DEX: 14 CON: 13 INT: 8 WIS: 11 CHA: 9
  
  Skills: Stealth +6, Survival +2
  Languages: Common, Goblin
  Challenge: 2 (450 XP)
  
  Equipment: Hide armor, shield, morningstar, javelin (x2)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Brute: Extra die of damage with melee weapons (included below)
    Surprise Attack: +2d6 damage if target hasn't acted yet
  
  Actions:
    Morningstar: +4 to hit, 2d8+2 piercing damage
    Javelin: +4 to hit, range 30/120, 1d6+2 piercing damage
```

## Personality
- **Grandiose**: Refers to himself in third person: "Klarg will crush you!"
- **Dramatic**: "Who dares defy Klarg?" and "Klarg will build a throne from your bones!"
- **Cowardly**: Attempts to flee if reduced to 7 hp or less

## Tactics
**Round 1**: Javelin throw while goblins engage in melee
**Round 2+**: Charge with morningstar, use fire pit for tactical advantage
**If Surprised**: Hide behind supply crates and coordinate goblin ambush
**Retreat**: Attempts to flee down chimney to area H3 if alone

## Lair Features
- **Fire Pit**: Center of cave, 5-foot square, 1 fire damage to enter, 1d6 if knocked prone
- **Supply Crates**: Provide half cover, marked with Lionshield Coster symbol
- **Natural Chimney**: Escape route to area H3 (DC 10 Athletics to climb)

## Minions
- **2 Goblins**: Standard tactics, fight to defend Klarg
- **Ripper (Wolf)**: Loyal companion, fights alongside Klarg

## Development
- **Alerted**: Hides with allies for ambush (passive Perception 14+ to avoid surprise)
- **Surprised**: Can be caught off-guard by climbing from H3
- **Isolated**: Becomes desperate and attempts dangerous escapes

## Treasure
- **Stolen Supplies**: Lionshield Coster goods worth 50 gp reward
- **Treasure Chest**: 1,700 cp, 150 sp, 2 potions of healing, jade frog statuette (40 gp)

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce HP to 20, remove one goblin ally
**Harder**: Add 1 additional wolf companion, increase HP to 35
