# Cragmaw Goblins

## Role
Standard goblin raiders and hideout defenders

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Hit-and-run ambush tactics, overwhelming numbers
**Preferred Range**: Shortbow at range, scimitar in melee
**Key Abilities**: Nimble Escape, pack tactics, environmental advantages

## Encounter Variations

### Ambush Goblins (Road Encounter)
- **Numbers**: 4 goblins (2 per side of road)
- **Tactics**: Wait for party to approach horses, focus fire on one target
- **Morale**: Fight to death except last goblin flees
- **Terrain**: Dense thickets provide 3/4 cover

### Guard Post Goblins (H2)
- **Numbers**: 2 goblins + 1 goblin boss
- **Tactics**: Use thicket cover, retreat to H3 when boss falls
- **Equipment**: Boss has additional 16 sp
- **Terrain**: Wooden planks in hollowed thicket

### Den Goblins (H6)
- **Numbers**: 5 goblins + 2 goblin bosses (Yeemik, Errk)
- **Tactics**: Use escarpment for height advantage
- **Prisoner**: Guard Sildar Hallwinter (1 hp)
- **Terrain**: 10-foot escarpment divides cave

### Pools Goblins (H7)
- **Numbers**: 3 goblins + 2 wolves
- **Tactics**: One flees to warn Klarg, others control flood trap
- **Special**: Can trigger flood by destroying dam
- **Terrain**: Waterfall masks sound between areas

### Klarg's Goblins (H8)
- **Numbers**: 2 goblins (Klarg's bodyguards)
- **Tactics**: Coordinate with Klarg and Ripper
- **Equipment**: Standard gear
- **Terrain**: Fire pit and supply crates provide tactical options

## Group Tactics
1. **Surprise Round**: Focus all ranged attacks on single target
2. **Round 1**: Spread out, use Nimble Escape to find cover
3. **Round 2+**: Coordinate attacks, protect spellcasters and leaders
4. **Retreat**: When reduced to 1/3 numbers or leader falls

## Environmental Advantages
- **Dense Thickets**: 3/4 cover for ranged attacks
- **Cave Terrain**: Use stalactites and rocks for cover
- **Water Features**: Can trigger flood trap in H5/H7
- **Elevated Positions**: Rope bridges and escarpments

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Reduce each encounter by 1 goblin
**Harder**: Add 1 goblin to each encounter, give pack tactics
**Very Easy**: Goblins have only 5 hp each
**Very Hard**: Some goblins have poisoned arrows (+1d4 poison damage, DC 10 Con save)
