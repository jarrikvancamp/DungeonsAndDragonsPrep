# Giant Poisonous Snake

## Role
Ambush predator guarding side passage

## Tactical Overview
**Combat Style**: Single devastating poisonous bite from hiding
**Preferred Range**: Surprise attack from concealment
**Key Abilities**: Poison bite, stealth ambush, environmental trigger

## Stats
```yaml
Giant Poisonous Snake:
  AC: 14 (Natural Armor)
  HP: 11 (range 8-15)
  Speed: 30 ft, swim 30 ft
  STR: 10 DEX: 18 CON: 13 INT: 2 WIS: 10 CHA: 3
  
  Skills: Perception +2
  Languages: —
  Challenge: 1/4 (50 XP)
  
  Special Abilities:
    Keen Smell: Advantage on Perception checks using smell
  
  Actions:
    Bite: +6 to hit, 1d4+4 piercing damage
    Target must make DC 11 Constitution save
    Failure: Take 2d6 poison damage
    Success: Take half damage
```

## Encounter Details (H4 - Western Passage)

### Location
- **Passage**: Unstable ledge across western side of stream
- **Concealment**: Coiled among rocks, DC 15 Perception to spot
- **Trigger**: Attacks creatures attempting to climb the ledge

### Environmental Hazard
- **Unstable Ledge**: Snake's movement can trigger collapse
- **Collapse**: DC 10 Dexterity save or fall into stream below
- **Fall Damage**: 1d6 bludgeoning + prone in 2 feet of water
- **Stream**: Difficult terrain, potential drowning for unconscious creatures

## Tactics
1. **Ambush**: Wait motionless until prey within striking distance
2. **Strike**: Single bite attack with poison
3. **Retreat**: Slither away into water if seriously wounded (1/4 hp)
4. **Environmental**: Movement may trigger ledge collapse

## Behavioral Notes
- **Solitary**: Acts independently, not allied with goblins
- **Territorial**: Defends passage instinctively
- **Non-communicative**: Cannot be reasoned with or intimidated
- **Escape Route**: Can swim downstream if threatened

## Development Options
- **Avoided**: Party can bypass entirely with careful climbing
- **Triggered**: Ledge collapse creates permanent obstacle
- **Wounded**: Snake retreats deeper into cave system
- **Captured**: Could be used as improvised weapon vs. goblins

## Environmental Integration
- **Sound**: Hissing masked by waterfall noise from H7
- **Light**: Cave darkness aids snake's ambush tactics
- **Terrain**: Unstable footing favors snake's natural movement
- **Water**: Escape route and potential drowning hazard

## Scaling Options
**Easier**: Snake has only 8 hp, poison save DC 9
**Harder**: Snake has 15 hp, adds constrict attack (grapple + restrained)
**Environmental**: Ledge collapse blocks passage permanently
**Alternative**: Replace with 2 regular poisonous snakes (CR 1/8 each)

## Treasure
- **None**: Natural predator has no treasure
- **Environment**: Passage might lead to small hidden cache (DM discretion)
- **Organic**: Snake's venom glands could be harvested for poison (1 dose)
