## Session Notes

### Session 0

- Lion and Tiger escaped marketplace, wounded father in front of family
- Party split up initially:
  - Fa, Zelaerys, Cappy → Serpent Inn
  - Dárin → Hall of Justice
  - Lyssandra → Escaped city guards, went to Granny
- Reunited at Serpent Inn
- Met Gundrun (dwarf questgiver) at Driftwood Tavern
- Agreed to protect caravan to Phandalin
- Decided to enter hunting tournament first

### Session 1: The Hunt in Neverwinter Wood

- Entry fee: 25 gold
- Goal: Biggest head(s)
- Encounters:
  1. **Griffin** - DEFEATED!
  2. Human shapeshifter (werewolf)
  3. Rock golem (awakened by thrown rock)
  4. Young bronze dragon "Fendos" - helped defeat golem
- **Prize**: Legendary armor of invulnerability → Dárin

### Session 2: Fight in the Woods

- 2 horses killed in goblin ambush
- Fought goblins with stones from behind carriage
- Encountered large goblin

### Session 3: Into the Caves (Mario DM's first session)

- One goblin escaped from previous fight
- 2 goblins ambushed from bushes at cave entrance
- Entered cave system via river
- **Wolf chamber**: Tried feeding dead goblins to wolves (failed), fought them, Zelaris smoked and ate wolf meat
- River flooding caused Cappy headaches
- **Bridge encounter**: Zelaris jumped and broke bridge, had to cross one by one
- **Final chamber**: 3 goblins on bedrolls, chained humanoid, hidden goblins, goblin boss
- Rescued **Syldar Hallwinter**

### Session 4: Towards Phandalin

- Released Syldar
- Fought 3 goblins, wolf, and bugbear "Clark"
- Found Lionshield Coster supplies (owner: Linene Graywind)
- Met Fa at Barton's Provision
- **Quest**: Save Gundren from castle for 500GP
- Stayed at **Stonehill Inn** (Toblyn & Tralina, innkeepers)
- **Town info**:
  - Townmaster: Harbin Wester
  - Red Brands killed carpenter, kidnapped family
  - Red Brands rob Linene's supplies
  - Red Brands base: Sleeping Giant
  - Carp (Alderleaf's son) knows secret passage
- **Religious moment**: Dárin prayed to Tymora, got advantage on one d20 roll
- **Sister Garele**: Wants ancient elven mage's spellbook, was attacked by banshee

### Session 5: The Sleeping Giant

- **Missing person**: Larno (Sildar's friend, old wizard) near ruined castle
- **Rewards negotiated**:
  - 200GP for eliminating Red Brands
  - 100GP → 50GP for defeating orcs (Dárin's poor negotiation)
  - Unknown reward for rescuing carpenter's family
- **Alderleaf Farm visit**: Met Ms. Alderleaf and 10-year-old Carp
- **CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT**: Dárin developed Scottish accent
- **Sleeping Giant tavern fight**: Zelaerys went down, Dárin healed him, killed 3 enemies
- **Level up**: Party reached Level 3
- **Woodcarver investigation**: Found abandoned, boarded house
- **Miner's Exchange**: Met ambitious Halia Thornton (guild master)
  - Revealed Red Brands leader: **"Glasstaff"**
  - Lyssandra distrusts Halia

### Session 6: The Bugbears

- Entered forest cave system
- Fought and defeated **Notic**
- **Bugbear encounter**: Killed 3 bugbears, rescued goblin "Droop"
- **Loot**: 32 silver, eyepatch (50 gold), iron key
- Bugbear "Mosk" wore the eyepatch
- Connection revealed: Bugbears from Redbrand's castle
- **Learning moment**: Can knock out enemies for questioning

### Session 7: The Gamblers, the Wizard and the Glass Staff

- **Card players fight**: 4 drunk humans attacked party
- **Interrogation**: Woman and crying child taken to cellblock
- **Glasstaff's workshop**: Found wizard supplies, books in Elvish and Dwarvish
- **Important book**: Dwarfish book about Urmon's adventures (Forge of Spells, LightBringer, Phandelver Pact)
- **Boss fight**: Glasstaff paralyzed Zelaerys, Cappy transformed to panther
- **Major revelation**: Larno IS Glasstaff
- **Loot**:
  - Glass staff (Staff of Defense) → Cappy
  - 130 gold, silk, gems (carnelians, peridots, pearl)
  - Scroll of Charm Person
  - Scroll of Fireball → Lyssandra
  - 30 beaver pelts from stolen caravan
- **Discovery**: Letter with spider sign, platinum signet ring

### Session 8

- **Rescue mission**: Found mother with 2 children + injured Fa in cells
- Healed Fa, Zalaerys gave him sake
- **Pit trap**: Zalaerys fell, party used door as bridge
- **Guard fight**: Killed 2 guards, knocked out 1
- **Poisoned food discovery**: 50 gold found
- **Interrogation ended**: Guard jumped in water and drowned
- **Family rescued**: Returned carpenter's family home
- **Zhentarim connection**: Halia gave party letter with spider emblem
- **Banshee info**: Sister Garele saw banshee west of Conyberry
- **Owlbear boss fight** en route to Conyberry
- **Conyberry arrival**: Met elf "Verron" with Zhentarim tattoo
- **Level up**: Party reached Level 4

### Session 9: Banshee Quest

- **Reidoth revealed**: Verron's real name, lives in Thundertree
- **Route options**: Cragmaw Castle (1 day SW via Triboar Trail vs dangerous forest)
- **Thundertree info**: Flooded, volcanic activity, monster problems
- **Decision**: Party chose Banshee quest first
- **Anouk's inspiration**: +1 for helping Conyberry residents
- **Forest journey**: Dense woodland, abandoned campsite, orc ambush (4 orcs killed)
- **Reidoth's druid marks**: Forest druid tattoos, taught Anouk Entangle cantrip
- **Banshee encounter**:
  - Hut built on tree stump, white rabbit outside (Sam killed and ate it)
  - Banshee: "Sister of elvenkind"
  - Darren insulted banshee and vanished temporarily
  - Wine cellar with monk emblems and fine wines
  - **Bowgentle's spellbook**: Held by mage Tsarnot in Ireighbough
  - Banshee's husband was killed by this mage
- **Magic potions obtained**:
  - Sam: Dark red potion (condition heal + fear effect)
  - Fa: Potion of Strength (+1d6 damage for 3 rounds)
  - Anke: Potion of the Berserker (rage + happy side effect)

### Session 10: Old Owl Well & Darrin's Father

- **Necromancer encounter**: Zombies at Old Owl Well, powerful necromancer appeared
- **Orc directions**: Pointed party to orc camp (2 hours south in mountains)
- **Cappy's bomb**: Received from necromancer
- **Orc camp discovery**: Heads on spears (half-orcs, elves, humans)
- **Tragic revelation**:
  - 6 orcs + uber orc "Brughar" + ogre + skilled dwarf juggernaut
  - Juggernaut revealed as **Darrin's undead father** (raised by necromancer)
  - Father asked Darrin to kill him
- **Victory**: All orcs and ogre defeated
- **Zalaris' prize**: Magical +1 axe from Brughar

### Session 11: Necromancer's Bargain

- **Looting**: 10GP each
- **Orc head trade**: Exchanged for book information from necromancer
- **Spellbook location**: With traveling wizard, no longer in region
- **Blood ritual**: Fa and Sam participated with necromancer
- **Romantic moment**: Cappy and Fa after necromancer's potion
- **Chase scene**: Party followed, arrows fired, everyone escaped
- **Reidoth consultation**: Warned about necromancer
- **Thundertree departure**: Traveled with Reidoth
- **Level up**: Party reached Level 5

### Session 12: Journey Through Thundertree Ruins

- **Thundertree layout**: Northern tower (intact), rest ruined
- **Reidoth's map**: Two question marks (cult and dragon)
- **Emerald Enclave member**: Reidoth's affiliation revealed
- **Brown Horse Inn exploration**: Former brewery, zombie infestation
- **Zombie immunity**: Fire and poison ineffective, water seemed to work
- **Spider encounter**: 2 large spiders, Lyssandra found 23GP + light crossbow
- **Cultist encounter**: Dragon-masked cultists, difficult fight, boss wounded and fled

### Session 13: Tower Approach

- **House T exploration**: Darrin collapsed roof with warhammer
- **5 Twigblights defeated**
- **Mysterious direwolf**: Appeared with party, bit Fa when leashed
- **Ash zombies**: Multiple encounters in ruined houses
- **Tower approach**: Iron door locked, dragon sleeping on top
- **Infiltration**: Climbed to balcony, reached tower top

### Session 14: Dragon Battle & Major Loot

- **Epic dragon fight**:
  - Lissandra first up tower, then Cappy
  - Cappy fell but rescued by "friend dragon" (Anouk's new character)
  - Lissandra and Fa knocked unconscious, saved by Darrin
  - **Darrin dealt killing blow**

  - Cappy: Can summon dragon companion (1/long rest, fights 3 rounds)

- **Major loot distribution**:
  - **Fa**: 250 gold + malachite (10g) + potion of lesser restoration
  - **Lissandra**: 2000 gold + bronze crown (250g) + onyx moon stone (50g)
  - **Cappy**: 1500 gold + wooden box with animal figurines (250g) + jasper (50g)
  - **Darrin**: 5000 gold + copper chalice (25g) + gemstone (10g)
  - **Zalaris**: 2000 gold + golden bracelet (25g) + zircon (50g)

- **Travel to Conyberry**: Storm approached and retreated
- **Eran encounter**: Gave wanderer rations, gemstone, 20 gold
- **Followers**: 3-4 small creatures tracking party
- **Mysterious children**: Appeared at night, Fa fed them, gone by morning

### Session 15: Cragmaw Castle

- **Stealth infiltration**: Side entrance, killed 3 sleeping goblins
- **Owlbear escape**: Ran past party
- **Dwarf rescue**: Found Gundren's brother
- **Boss fight**: Bugbear leader, drow, wolf, multiple bugbears
- **Fa abandoned**: Left behind during fight
- **Leadership change**: Bugbears now follow Zalaris after defeating their leader
- **Drow escape**: Vanished during battle
- **Important discovery**: Torn paper with Lost Mine of Phandelver plans
- **Cursed flask**: Huatan ritual flask (1 sip = +1 HP, multiple sips = poison, doesn't work on Fa, refills halfway daily)

### Session 16: Wave Echo Cave & Family Tragedy

- **Gundren's brothers**: Thardin (found dead) & Nindru (alive, rescued)
- **Cave exploration**:
  - Large pit with two crawl holes
  - Maze of passages (always chose right)
  - Slimy chamber discovery
- **"Assayes" door**: Right door marked
- **Guardroom**: Left door, many old corpses, dusty (years abandoned)
- **Heartbreaking discovery**:
  - Lissandra's mother crucified to wall (wings pinned)
  - Gnome collaborator also found
  - Lissandra's father found in gas-filled chamber
- **Respectful burial**: All three victims buried together outside cave
- **Wine cellar fight**: Empty barrels burned, attacked by Jelly (split into 4 parts)
- **Water wheel chamber**: Skeleton head with green flames prevented right passage
- **Clever bypass**: Jumped in empty creek to avoid skeleton's sight
- **Cleaned chamber**: Recent cleanup, skeletons moved aside, connects to skeleton head area

### Session 17: The Spider - Final Battle

- **Wraith ambush**: Angry about orc raids, hostile to Zalaris (half-orc)
- **Darrin's armor lost**: Taken by wraith for his rudeness
- **Wraith's quest**: Retrieve lost staff from next chamber
- **Spectator fight**: Mini-beholder guarding staff
- **Staff retrieved**: Darrin recovered but armorless and bound/gagged
- **Brother rescue**: Found one of Gundren's brothers tied up
- **Final boss**: "The Spider" - summoned snakes (ironic nickname)
- **Tragic victory**: The Spider defeated but **Cappy killed in battle**
- **Campaign end**:
  - **Darrin**: Stubbornly stayed behind to search for armor
  - **Rest of party**: Returned to Phandalin
  - **Status**: Campaign ended with question mark
